A Guide to Debts That Are Ineligible for Settlement Programs

2025-10-16 15:14:33 EDT

Debt settlement programs can offer relief by negotiating unsecured debts like credit card balances for less than the full amount owed, but many types of debt do not qualify. Generally, debts backed by government authority or secured by collateral are excluded from these programs. This includes secured loans like mortgages and auto loans, where lenders can repossess the asset, as well as federal student loans, which have their own specific relief options. Tax debts, court-ordered payments like child support and alimony, and legal judgments are also typically ineligible for negotiation through standard debt settlement companies.

The forecast predicts above-normal precipitation for the Pacific Northwest, northern Rockies, and western Great Lakes.
