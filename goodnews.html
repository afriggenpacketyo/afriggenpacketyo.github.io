Massive Hydrothermal Vent Field Discovered in Pacific Ocean

2025-08-30 15:00:28 EDT

Researchers have discovered a massive hydrothermal vent field in the Pacific Ocean, named the Kunlun field, which is over a hundred times larger than the famous "Lost City" field in the Atlantic. Located northeast of Papua New Guinea, the site features deep craters and carbonate walls gushing hydrogen-rich fluids at relatively low temperatures. Scientists believe this unique environment, which supports a diverse ecosystem of deep-sea life, may resemble the conditions on early Earth where life first began. The discovery challenges previous assumptions about where such systems can form and could be a target for future research into abiogenesis.
