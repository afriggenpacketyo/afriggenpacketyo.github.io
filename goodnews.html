UK Experiences Significant Diurnal Temperature Swings in Spring

2026-03-19 22:30:56 EDT

The UK is currently experiencing a "temperature rollercoaster," characterized by warm, sunny spring days followed by significantly chilly nights, a phenomenon known as the diurnal temperature range. High-pressure systems create clear skies, enabling rapid warming during the day due to higher sun angles and longer daylight hours, but also facilitating quick heat loss overnight. This has resulted in temperature swings of approximately 21C in some English and Welsh locations, with daytime highs around 19C dropping to below freezing. This pronounced contrast is typical for early spring, further amplified by dry air, which heats and cools more quickly than humid air.
