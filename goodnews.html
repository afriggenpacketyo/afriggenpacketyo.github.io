2025-05-02 19:48:30 EDT

Tim Friede's blood contains antibodies that protect against fatal doses from a wide range of venomous snakes. Current antivenoms are species-specific, but Friede's 18-year mission could pave the way for a universal antivenom. The resulting antivenom cocktail protected mice from lethal doses of 13 out of 19 snake species.
