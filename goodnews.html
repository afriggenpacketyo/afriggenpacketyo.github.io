When applied to annuities, this rule serves as a benchmark to compare the guaranteed income stream from an annuity against potential portfolio withdrawals. An annuity might offer a higher payout rate, providing protection against outliving one's savings, but often lacks the inflation adjustments and flexibility of a traditional portfolio.
