Experts Advise Caution on Reverse Mortgages Amidst Rising Costs and Fluctuating Home Prices

2025-09-03 09:48:13 EDT

Experts are advising homeowners to carefully weigh the risks of reverse mortgages, despite their appeal in the current housing market, due to rising insurance, maintenance, and property tax costs. While reverse mortgages allow seniors to convert home equity into cash without monthly payments, borrowers must maintain their homes and stay current on these increasing expenses to avoid foreclosure. Falling home prices in some areas could also reduce the amount of equity available or make qualification more difficult. Homeowners are encouraged to consider long-term plans and alternatives like home equity loans or selling their property, as upfront fees and variable interest rates can impact overall benefits.
