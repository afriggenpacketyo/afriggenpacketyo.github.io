Understanding Interest Earnings on a 4.20% CD Account

2026-07-08 15:26:49 EDT

Savers can expect to earn approximately $4 for every $100 deposited in a 4.20% Certificate of Deposit (CD) account, with actual returns varying significantly based on the deposit amount and chosen term.
