Sir Andreas Whittam Smith, Co-founder of The Independent Newspaper, Dies at 88

2025-12-01 00:48:34 EST

Sir Andreas Whittam Smith, the esteemed co-founder of The Independent newspaper, has died at the age of 88, leaving behind a profound legacy in British journalism. He launched the publication in 1986 with colleagues Stephen Glover and Matthew Symonds, pioneering a politically independent newspaper that marked a thrilling moment in Fleet Street's history. Described as a "radical and a pioneer," Sir Andreas was instrumental in transforming British journalism by creating a platform "of no party or faction." His career also encompassed significant public service roles, including First Estates Church Commissioner and President of the British Board of Film Classification, reflecting a life dedicated to both media and civic duty.
