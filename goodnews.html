Vincent used "Hocus Pocus" as a brand, repeating it during his tricks until it became a shorthand for magic and deception itself. Over time, the spoken phrase "hocus" was shortened to "hoax," which entered the lexicon around 1800 to describe a grander, more elaborate deception.
