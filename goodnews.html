2026-07-16 14:27:00 EDT

Extensive scientific evidence, including a comprehensive analysis of over 123,000 participants across 19 large trials, unequivocally demonstrates no increased risk of dementia or cognitive decline among statin users. In fact, some research suggests statins may actively help lower the risk of certain types of dementia by reducing elevated blood lipids. While the FDA previously noted temporary memory issues in some users, these were not serious or permanent, and the brain's cholesterol supply is largely independent of blood cholesterol levels, clarifying this crucial public health issue.
