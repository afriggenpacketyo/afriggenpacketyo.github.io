Clarifying the Impact of Reverse Mortgages on Social Security and Medicare Benefits

2025-08-28 14:25:15 EDT

This article clarifies whether reverse mortgages affect Social Security or Medicare benefits for seniors. It explains that reverse mortgages do not directly reduce standard Social Security retirement benefits or Medicare eligibility, as these are not means-tested. However, a crucial caveat is that need-based programs like Supplemental Security Income (SSI) and Medicaid can be impacted. Large lump-sum withdrawals from a reverse mortgage, if kept in a bank account, could count towards asset limits, potentially reducing or eliminating eligibility for these specific programs. Financial consultation is advised for those relying on such benefits. (not investment advice)
