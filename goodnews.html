Popular 'Win-Win' Hedge Trade Leverages Semiconductor Volatility

2026-05-07 12:05:58 EDT

A popular trading strategy involves selling downside protection in semiconductor stocks, where implied volatility is high, and simultaneously buying downside protection in the S&P 500, where volatility is lower. This 'win-win' approach allows traders to remain bullish on the rallying semiconductor sector while hedging broader market risks. The strategy capitalizes on the rich premiums in semiconductor options by selling puts, using the income to fund protective puts or VIX calls in the S&P 500. If semiconductors rise, the trader profits from the net credit; if they fall, the S&P 500 puts provide a buffer. This trade is seen as particularly compelling due to the significant volatility spread between the two markets.
