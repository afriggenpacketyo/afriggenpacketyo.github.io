2025-10-17 19:58:22 EDT

The Orionid meteor shower is anticipated to peak around October 22nd, offering a spectacular annual display visible until November 7th. These bright, fast-moving meteors originate from debris left by Halley's Comet as Earth traverses its orbit, burning up in our atmosphere to create distinctive light trails. During the peak, observers can expect approximately 15 meteors per hour, traveling at speeds up to 148,000 mph.
