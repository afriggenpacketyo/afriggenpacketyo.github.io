2026-03-19 22:30:56 EDT

However, clear skies allowing for warm days also lead to significant overnight temperature drops, resulting in frosty mornings. Some areas in the Midlands and southern England saw a diurnal temperature range of around 21C. This phenomenon, where dry air heats and cools quickly, is typical in early spring as days lengthen and the sun rises higher, but the ground remains chilly, leading to rapid overnight cooling.
