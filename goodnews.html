The Significance of Rare Earth Elements

2025-06-11 15:44:08 EDT

Rare earth elements are essential components in a wide range of technologies, from cellphones and medical devices to wind turbines and weapons. These 17 chemically similar elements, found within the Lanthanide series, are often described as the "vitamins" of industry, enabling technologies to become smaller, faster, and stronger. China dominates the production and processing of rare earths, accounting for a significant portion of global output, while the extraction and refining processes can pose environmental challenges. They are not rare in the sense of abundance, but rather in the difficulty of extracting and separating them.
