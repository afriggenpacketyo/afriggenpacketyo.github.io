Los Angeles Community Group Provides Safe Space for Black Men's Mental Health

2025-10-18 16:41:19 EDT

A Los Angeles-based initiative called "Mental Health Is Real Wealth" is providing a crucial safe space for Black men to discuss their mental health and be vulnerable. Founded by Desmond Carter, the bi-monthly group in Leimert Park addresses the unique societal pressures, systemic racism, and emotional suppression that Black men often face. Amid rising suicide rates among Black men and boys, such spaces are vital as they are less likely to seek mental health support and often receive culturally incompetent care when they do. The group aims to break generational cycles and foster emotional expression, offering a gateway to therapy and community support.
