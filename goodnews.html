2025-06-26 18:31:57 EDT

The planet, named TWA 7b, is believed to be about the mass of Saturn and orbits a young star relatively close to our solar system. Scientists used high-contrast imaging with a coronagraph to suppress the star's glare and reveal the faint planet. While a small possibility remains that the object is a background galaxy, researchers state the evidence strongly points to it being a new planetary discovery.
