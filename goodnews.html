Business Professor Suggests a Key Interview Question to Uncover a Company's True Culture

2025-07-01 10:54:32 EDT

NYU Stern professor Suzy Welch recommends that job seekers ask, "What kind of person should not work at this company?" during interviews. This question often elicits candid, unrehearsed responses that reveal a company's true values beyond generic platitudes like "innovation" or "empowerment." The answers, such as "someone who wants to try out different roles," provide genuine insight into the workplace culture, helping candidates assess if it aligns with their personal values. Welch advises asking this question late in the interview process, after an offer is made, to maintain rapport.
