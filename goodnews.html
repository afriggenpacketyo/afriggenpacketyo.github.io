The UK government is urging local councils across England to widely adopt the National Parking Platform (NPP), a universal parking payment app designed to minimize "unfair" fines for drivers. This system enables motorists to use any major parking application to pay in NPP-registered car parks, streamlining the payment process. Ministers are set to introduce statutory guidance to encourage greater participation, aiming to significantly increase the number of councils using the platform. This initiative, part of the "Better Connected" transport plan, has already processed over 10 million payments and could potentially expand to cover road tolls and electric vehicle charging in the future.

The crew, comprising Reid Wiseman, Victor Glover (the first Black astronaut on a lunar mission), Christina Koch (the first woman), and Jeremy Hansen (the first Canadian), shared insights from their quarantine at Kennedy Space Center. They emphasized the profound historical significance of their upcoming flight, which marks NASA's first crewed lunar mission in over half a century. Despite a potential liquid hydrogen leak causing a previous delay, the mission is currently 80% cleared for its April 1st launch, with weather being the primary concern.
