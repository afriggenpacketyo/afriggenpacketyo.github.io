This engineered organoid, capable of beating autonomously, represents a significant breakthrough for cardiac disease research and drug development. The sinoatrial node regulates heart rhythm, and its failure can lead to life-threatening conditions. This advancement offers potential for new treatments for abnormal heart rhythms and could revolutionize the study of cardiac function.
