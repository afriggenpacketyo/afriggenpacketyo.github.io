Tsinghua University Develops Chinese Jam-Proof Satellite Navigation Network

2026-03-29 22:01:05 EDT

Researchers at Tsinghua University have developed an innovative Chinese jam-proof satellite navigation network that employs coded light signals from "beacon" satellites, presenting a robust alternative to conventional radio wave-based systems like GPS and BeiDou. This advanced system integrates powerful light sources on satellites to transmit coded signals to Earth. Ground-based receivers then detect this light, using its precise direction and the satellites' known positions to calculate location with enhanced accuracy. The inherent narrowness and straight-line propagation of light beams render them significantly more resistant to interference than radio signals, promising greater resilience for guiding various vehicles and spacecraft.
