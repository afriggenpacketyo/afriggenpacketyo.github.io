A Comparison of Annuities and Certificates of Deposit for Retirement Planning

2025-08-06 12:51:59 EDT

This article explores the roles of annuities and certificates of deposit (CDs) in retirement planning, noting that neither is universally superior. CDs offer simplicity, predictability, and FDIC-insured safety, making them suitable for short-term goals and capital preservation. However, their returns often fail to outpace inflation. Annuities are designed to provide a guaranteed income stream for life, addressing the risk of outliving one's savings, but are more complex and less liquid. The best choice depends on an individual's specific needs, risk tolerance, and desire for lifetime income versus flexibility. (not investment advice)
