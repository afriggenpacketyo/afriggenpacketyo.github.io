2025-10-16 14:27:18 EDT

The prediction is heavily influenced by an active La Niña climate pattern, which typically brings cooler, wetter conditions to the northern states and warmer, drier weather to the South. Consequently, the Pacific Northwest, northern Rockies, and western Great Lakes are expected to see above-normal precipitation. In contrast, the Southwest, southern Texas, and the Southeast are likely to experience a drier winter than usual.
