Cardiff Considers Implementing a Parking Surcharge for SUVs and Larger Vehicles

2025-10-16 08:44:30 EDT

Cardiff is poised to become the first UK city to charge drivers of SUVs and other large vehicles a premium for parking permits under a new 10-year plan. The proposal, aimed at reducing congestion and air pollution, would introduce a surcharge for vehicles weighing over 2,400kg and make those over 3,500kg ineligible for permits. The plan also includes scrapping residential permits in the city center and creating new permit types for carers and NHS workers. The move has generated mixed reactions, with some residents supporting the environmental goals while others, including tradespeople, feel unfairly targeted.
