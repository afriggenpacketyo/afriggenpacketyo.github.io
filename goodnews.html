UK Government to Legally Extend Bereavement Leave for Early-Stage Miscarriages

2025-07-07 02:17:42 EDT

The UK government plans to amend the Employment Rights Bill to grant parents who experience a miscarriage before 24 weeks the legal right to bereavement leave. Currently, this right is only available for pregnancy loss after 24 weeks. Deputy Prime Minister Angela Rayner stated the change aims to give people adequate time to grieve without work-related pressure. The exact length of the leave is under consultation but is expected to be at least one week, a move welcomed by advocacy groups like the Miscarriage Association.
