2025-12-27 00:44:52 EST

Cambridgeshire artist Mia Hansson is meticulously working to complete her full-size replica of the 70-meter Bayeux Tapestry by October 2027. Her goal is to coincide with the original's display at the British Museum and its return to France for William the Conqueror's 1,000th birth anniversary. Hansson, who began this ambitious project nine and a half years ago, works from a "roadmap" and has identified "Mildred," a rebel seamstress from the 11th century, for original errors.
