New Driving Test Booking Rules to Enhance Fairness for Learner Drivers

2026-03-17 13:58:01 EDT

The Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency (DVSA) has announced new rules for booking driving tests, set to be implemented in May and June, aimed at creating a fairer system for learner drivers. From May 12, only learner drivers will be able to book, change, or swap car driving tests. From June 9, restrictions will be placed on changing test locations, limiting changes to the three nearest centers. These measures are intended to reduce waiting lists and prevent test slots from being resold at inflated prices. The DVSA is banning instructors from booking tests on behalf of students to combat touts who have been selling login details for profit. The standard test fees will remain, ensuring learners only pay the official charge.
