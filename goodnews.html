Global Study Debunks 'Lab Leak' Theory of COVID-19 Origin

2025-05-23 09:00:18 EDT

A study led by the University of Edinburgh, involving researchers from 20 institutions, challenges the "lab leak" theory of COVID-19's origin. Analyzing 167 bat coronavirus genomes, the study traces the virus's origins to bat populations in northern Laos and southwest China, circulating five to seven years before the pandemic. The findings contradict claims from the White House that a Chinese lab leak was the "real source" of the pandemic. The study focused on sarbecoviruses, including Sars-CoV-1 and Sars-CoV-2, tracing the original Sars-CoV-1 to western China before its emergence in Guangdong.
