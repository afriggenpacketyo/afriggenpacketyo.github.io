2025-07-05 01:00:08 EDT

By reactivating this pathway, they achieved complete restoration of the lost tissue, including cartilage. The researchers believe this discovery proves the existence of a fundamental mechanism for regeneration that could potentially be present in other organs and, one day, be applied to humans.
