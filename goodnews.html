While its exact re-entry time and location remain unclear, most of the probe was expected to burn up, with NASA estimating a low 1 in 4,200 chance of harming anyone on Earth. This article highlights its crucial discoveries, including the existence of a third radiation belt during intense solar activity, contributing to hundreds of scientific publications and advancing our understanding of space weather.
