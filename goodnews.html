Strong Geomagnetic Storm Increases Likelihood of Northern Lights Visibility Across the UK

2025-11-11 13:02:49 EST

A strong to severe geomagnetic storm is expected to make the Northern Lights visible across many parts of the UK, potentially even in southern areas. The phenomenon is caused by multiple Coronal Mass Ejections from the sun, which send charged solar particles toward Earth, creating the aurora upon interaction with the atmosphere. While these storms pose no direct harm to humans, they can potentially disrupt satellites, power grids, and electronic devices. Sky-watchers are advised to find dark locations with clear views of the northern horizon for the best chance of witnessing the display.
