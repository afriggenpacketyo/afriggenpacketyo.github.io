2025-10-09 02:00:12 EDT

China has launched the world's first thermal power station utilizing dual towers in the Gobi Desert, constructed by the China Three Gorges Corporation. The plant employs nearly 27,000 mirrors to focus sunlight onto two 200-meter towers, producing concentrated heat that melts and stores salt at high temperatures. This system generates steam to power turbines, ensuring continuous electricity production even after sunset or on cloudy days. The dual-tower design, with overlapping mirror fields, enhances efficiency by approximately 25% compared to single-tower setups, reducing mirror requirements and overall construction costs.
