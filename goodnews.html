Consumer Protection Law Grants Borrowers Key Rights During Debt Collection

2025-10-07 12:22:47 EDT

The Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA) provides crucial protections for consumers dealing with third-party debt collectors. Under the law, collectors are prohibited from using harassment, abusive language, or deceptive tactics to collect a debt. Consumers have the right to demand written verification of the debt, and if a dispute is filed within 30 days, the collector must cease all collection efforts until they provide proof. Borrowers can also legally demand that a collector stop contacting them altogether.
