According to a pilot and a flight attendant, this procedure allows passengers' and crew's eyes to pre-adjust to darkness. In the event of an emergency evacuation, especially one involving a power loss, this quick adaptation is crucial for seeing emergency floor path lighting and spotting potential hazards outside the aircraft.
