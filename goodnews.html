China Develops Jam-Proof Satellite Network Using Coded Light Signals

2026-03-29 22:01:05 EDT

Led by Professor Xing Fei, the project seeks to augment existing navigation systems like GPS and BeiDou, which are susceptible to interference. The innovative system works by deploying powerful light sources on satellites that transmit coded signals to Earth, enabling ground receivers to accurately determine their location based on the light's direction and the satellites' known positions. This novel approach promises significantly enhanced accuracy over traditional star-based navigation and is considerably more resistant to disruption, potentially transforming guidance for various vehicles and spacecraft through "lighthouses in space."
