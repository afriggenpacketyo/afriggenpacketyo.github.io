2025-05-23 11:23:28 EDT

While MRI is more effective at detecting tumors, CEM is more cost-effective and can find smaller tumors than standard mammography. The study suggests that CEM should become a standard of care for women with dense breasts at high risk of breast cancer, potentially averting tragedies through early detection.
