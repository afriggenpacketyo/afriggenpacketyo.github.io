"God of Chaos" Asteroid to Pass Near Earth in 2029

2026-04-15 15:43:16 EDT

The asteroid Apophis, named after the Egyptian "god of chaos," will safely pass within 20,000 miles of Earth on April 13, 2029, closer than many orbiting satellites. NASA assures there is no risk of impact for at least 100 years, but this rare event offers scientists an unprecedented opportunity to study a colossal space rock from close range. The asteroid, roughly the size of three football fields, may experience "stretching" and "squeezing" due to Earth's gravitational pull, potentially causing surface changes. Observations will help scientists understand its composition and behavior.
