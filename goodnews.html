Reverse Mortgages Do Not Directly Affect Social Security or Medicare Benefits for Seniors

2025-08-28 14:25:15 EDT

Reverse mortgages generally do not directly impact Social Security retirement benefits or Medicare eligibility, offering a financial tool for seniors without jeopardizing these vital lifelines. Social Security payments are based on earnings history, not assets, and Medicare is not means-tested. However, seniors should be aware that large lump-sum withdrawals could affect eligibility for need-based programs like Supplemental Security Income (SSI) or Medicaid, which have strict income and asset limits. Financial advisors recommend careful planning of payout structures to avoid unintended consequences for these specific benefits. (not investment advice)
