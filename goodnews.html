Google Invests in Proxima Fusion for Europe's First Commercial Fusion Power Plant

2026-07-07 02:24:28 EDT

Google has invested €411 million ($468 million) in Germany-based Proxima Fusion, a startup aiming to build Europe's first commercial nuclear fusion power plant. This significant funding round, led by XTX Ventures and East X Ventures, underscores Google's sustained interest in fusion as a potential source of abundant, carbon-free energy. Proxima Fusion is developing stellarator technology and targets a demonstrator by the early 2030s, with a commercial plant later that decade. While the technology faces immense challenges, this investment highlights the global race to commercialize fusion power and its promise for the future.
