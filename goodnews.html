Met Gala 2025 to Celebrate Menswear and Black Style

2025-05-04 22:32:23 EDT

The Met Gala 2025 will focus on menswear and Black style, coinciding with the Costume Institute's exhibition "Superfine: Tailoring Black Style," inspired by Monica L Miller's book. This year's dress code, "Tailored for You," references the suiting and menswear featured in the exhibition, marking the first Met Gala in over two decades to exclusively highlight menswear. Co-chairs for the event include Pharrell Williams, Lewis Hamilton, Colman Domingo, A$AP Rocky, and LeBron James, with a host committee featuring Black talent from various fields, such as Simone Biles, Spike Lee, and Usher. The gala, a major fundraising event for the Metropolitan Museum of Art's Costume Institute, will take place on May 5th in New York City, with Vogue providing a live stream of the red carpet arrivals.
