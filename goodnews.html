Following in his father Ub Iwerks' footsteps, Don helped develop groundbreaking technologies such as Circle-Vision 360-degree cameras and 3-D effects for attractions like Captain EO. He co-founded Iwerks Entertainment, which became a major player in large-format films and theme park projection systems. Iwerks, who served in the U.S. Army and spent 34 years in Disney's Studio Machine Shop, received numerous accolades for his contributions, including the Gordon E. Sawyer Award. His legacy is celebrated as a humble genius who believed technology should always serve a good story.
