Evaluating Annuities Versus CDs for Retirement Planning

2025-08-06 12:51:59 EDT

This article evaluates the suitability of annuities versus Certificates of Deposit (CDs) for retirement planning, concluding that neither is universally superior but rather serves distinct financial needs. CDs offer simplicity, predictability, and FDIC-insured safety, making them ideal for short-term goals or as a conservative portfolio anchor, yet they often yield returns that struggle against inflation and lack lifetime income guarantees. Annuities, conversely, are specifically designed to provide guaranteed lifetime income, a crucial benefit for mitigating longevity risk in retirement. However, annuities are more complex financial products, typically involving various fees, surrender charges, and limited liquidity. Many retirees find that a balanced approach, combining CDs for short-term stability and annuities for long-term income, offers a comprehensive and effective strategy. (not investment advice)
