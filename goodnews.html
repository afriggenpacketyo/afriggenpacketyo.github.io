Apartment Renter Behavior Shifts

2025-05-09 11:54:18 EDT

A CNBC article discusses a striking trend in the apartment rental market: low turnover. Typically, about half of apartment renters in large urban markets move when their leases expire, but that is not happening now. The low turnover is attributed to factors such as an unaffordable for-sale market, lack of rental supply on the coasts, economic uncertainty, the cost of moving, and a shift to suburban apartments. As a result, landlords are getting better pricing from renewals and improving cash flow.
