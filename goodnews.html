Washington State Enacts Rent Control Legislation

2025-05-07 18:32:08 EDT

Washington Governor Bob Ferguson has signed a rent control bill into law, establishing limits on rent increases across the state. The new law caps rent increases at 7% plus inflation or 10%, whichever is lower, and includes single-family homes and manufactured homes. The measure is intended to provide stability for renters and address the state's homelessness crisis. Senator Emily Alvarado, the bill's sponsor, emphasized that housing is a basic human need and that the law will protect hardworking families and older adults from excessive rent increases. Ferguson also signed nine other bills to support homeowners and renters.
