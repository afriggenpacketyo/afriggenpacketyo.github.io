2025-06-21 11:50:03 EDT

The discovery occurred 15 years after the woman's blood sample was collected for routine surgery tests, and the unusual antibody was detected in 2011. High-throughput DNA sequencing in 2019 revealed a genetic mutation, making the woman the only known carrier compatible with herself. Researchers hope to find others with the same blood group to improve care for patients with rare blood types.
