Social Security Benefits Generally Protected from Medical Debt Garnishment

Federal law prevents direct garnishment of retirement, disability, and survivor benefits for consumer debts. Exceptions exist for federal debts like taxes or child support, but not for standard medical bills. Debt relief options, such as negotiating with providers or exploring debt settlement, are available to manage unmanageable medical debt.
