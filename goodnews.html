2026-07-15 17:22:53 EDT

While no specific source has been definitively identified, leafy greens are under investigation in Michigan, the outbreak's epicenter. Experts recommend thoroughly washing all fruits and vegetables under running water, scrubbing firm-skinned produce, and preventing cross-contamination by using clean utensils and surfaces.
