Chinese Team Develops First Commercial '3-Lane Highway' in Optical Fibre to Boost AI Capacity

2026-06-13 00:00:06 EDT

China has successfully activated the world's first three-band optical fibre communication system, a technological breakthrough that developers claim will significantly expand the carrying capacity of future AI networks. This innovative system utilizes the S-band, C-band, and L-band simultaneously within a conventional optical fibre, enabling a single fibre to transmit over five times the traffic of existing systems and nearly double the transmission capacity per core. The 35km link connects major computing facilities and serves as a commercial test bed for next-generation optical networking. This advancement is likened to expanding a two-lane motorway into a three-lane highway without constructing entirely new infrastructure, crucial for supporting data-intensive AI operations.
