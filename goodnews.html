EFL Championship Play-Offs Expand to Six Teams for 2026-27 Season

2026-03-05 06:05:16 EST

English Football League (EFL) Championship clubs have voted to expand their promotion play-offs from four to six teams, starting in the 2026-27 season. This new format, mirroring the National League, will see teams finishing third to eighth compete for a spot in the Premier League. The third and fourth-placed teams will directly enter the semi-finals, while fifth will play eighth and sixth will host seventh in one-legged quarter-finals. Semi-finals will remain two legs, with the final still at Wembley. The change aims to increase competitive fixtures and provide more clubs with a chance for promotion, boosting the Championship's drama.
