New Daily Pill Shows Promise in Maintaining Weight Loss After Obesity Injections

2026-05-12 19:06:23 EDT

A new daily pill, orforglipron, shows significant promise in helping individuals sustain weight loss after discontinuing GLP-1 obesity injections. Clinical trials, funded by its manufacturer Eli Lilly and published in Nature Medicine, revealed that patients taking orforglipron daily for a year successfully maintained over 70% of their previously lost weight, a notable improvement compared to the placebo group. This oral medication functions by mimicking a natural hormone that curbs appetite and promotes feelings of fullness, offering a potentially more appealing and cost-effective alternative to injectable treatments. Further research is needed to determine the optimal duration of treatment, which experts suggest could even be life-long.
