2026-05-15 12:57:59 EDT

This 88-foot-long dinosaur roamed the region 100-120 million years ago and is the largest ever found in Southeast Asia. Dubbed "the last titan," its discovery is significant because it was unearthed in one of the youngest rock formations where dinosaurs are found in Thailand. A life-size reconstruction is now on display at Bangkok's Thainosaur Museum.
