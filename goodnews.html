Ancient Chinese text 'Guiguzi' offers insights into persuasion and influence

2026-03-27 21:30:08 EDT

The ancient Chinese text 'Guiguzi' is being presented as a practical manual for persuasion, akin to Dale Carnegie's 'How to Win Friends and Influence People.' Written sometime between the Warring States Period and the Han dynasty, its authenticity and prestige have been debated. The text focuses on influencing those in power, a skill considered relevant even today. Stephen Selby's English translation aims to make this historical work more accessible, providing context for its practical advice on navigating complex social and political landscapes.
