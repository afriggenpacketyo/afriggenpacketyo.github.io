Fact Check: Statins Do Not Cause Dementia, Research Confirms

2026-07-16 14:27:00 EDT

Recent claims circulating online that statins, cholesterol-lowering drugs, cause dementia or memory loss have been debunked by extensive medical research. A comprehensive analysis from the Cholesterol Treatment Trialists' Collaboration found no difference in cognitive impairment between statin and placebo groups across 19 trials. Experts suggest that statins may even reduce the risk of certain types of dementia by lowering blood lipids. While the FDA previously added a warning label for temporary, reversible memory issues, current evidence does not support permanent cognitive decline, and brain cholesterol is largely separate from blood cholesterol.
