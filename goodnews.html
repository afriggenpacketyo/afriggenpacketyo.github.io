Astronaut Christina Koch even performed on-board repairs for the toilet, while ground control swiftly addressed communication problems with the flight termination system and a high-temperature reading in the launch abort system. Despite these near-mishaps, the crew successfully launched and is now intentionally pushing the spacecraft's systems to their limits in Earth's orbit. This rigorous testing phase is crucial for ensuring the Orion capsule's reliability and the crew's safety on their upcoming journey to the Moon.
