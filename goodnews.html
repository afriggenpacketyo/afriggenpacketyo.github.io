The Defense POW/MIA Accounting Agency plans to commence disinterments in late 2026, comparing DNA samples from the remains with those collected from family members. This initiative represents a notable shift after years of military reluctance, largely driven by persistent advocacy from groups like Operation 85, dedicated to honoring these fallen service members.
