Tibet quartz discovery enhances China's self-sufficiency in high-tech materials

2026-06-07 22:00:10 EDT

A recent study has identified high-purity quartz deposits in Tibet, a critical material for advanced technologies, potentially boosting China's self-sufficiency in this sector. Researchers from the University of Science and Technology of China found that Tibetan leucogranites hold significant potential for producing high-purity quartz with silica content exceeding 99.995 percent, meeting the threshold for hi-tech manufacturing. This discovery offers a strategic opportunity to develop domestic high-end quartz resources, ensuring a stable supply for industries where China currently dominates the global market. The findings were published in the European Journal of Mineralogy.
