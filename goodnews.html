UK Households May Receive Free Electricity for Off-Peak Appliance Use

2026-04-14 16:33:15 EDT

The Demand Flexibility Scheme, updated by the National Energy System Operator (NESO) and approved by Ofgem, will now incentivize increased usage when renewable energy generation is high and demand is low. This initiative aims to manage surplus power and improve grid resilience, particularly during summer months when solar power generation is significant. Participating customers with smart meters could receive rewards like discounted or free electricity.
