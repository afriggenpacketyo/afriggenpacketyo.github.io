Chinese Team Releases World's First Free Bamboo Drone Flight Control Software

2026-04-04 02:00:10 EDT

A Chinese team has developed and released the world's first bamboo drone flight control software for free, addressing unique challenges posed by bamboo airframes. Conventional flight controllers struggle with the low-frequency vibrations inherent in bamboo structures. The team redesigned control algorithms to match these properties, tuning an extended Kalman filter and leveraging bamboo's natural vibration-damping. This innovation reduces control latency from 15-20 milliseconds to an impressive 8-10 milliseconds, significantly improving responsiveness and stability. This open-source development aims to overcome limitations of existing commercial and open-source controllers, potentially advancing the industrialization of bamboo-based unmanned aerial vehicles.
