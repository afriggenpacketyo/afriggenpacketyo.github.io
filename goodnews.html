Iga Swiatek Argues Women's Tennis Doesn't Need 'Battle of the Sexes' Exhibitions

2026-01-03 04:00:25 EST

Wimbledon champion Iga Swiatek asserts that women's tennis no longer requires "Battle of the Sexes" exhibitions, such as the recent match between Aryna Sabalenka and Nick Kyrgios. Swiatek firmly believes the women's game "stands on its own," boasting numerous exceptional athletes and compelling narratives, rendering direct comparisons to men's tennis largely unnecessary. She views these contemporary exhibitions primarily as entertainment, distinct from the 1973 Billie Jean King match which championed social change and legitimacy for women in the sport. Instead, Swiatek advocates for mixed-team events like the United Cup as a more constructive and inclusive platform to celebrate both men's and women's tennis.
