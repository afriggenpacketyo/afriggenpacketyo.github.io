NASA Launches Mission to Recover Sinking Swift Telescope

2026-07-03 15:59:00 EDT

NASA has launched a high-risk, high-reward mission to recover its Swift Observatory telescope, which is sinking towards Earth due to recent solar storms. A three-armed spacecraft, Link, developed by Katalyst Space Technologies, successfully launched from the Marshall Islands to intercept and boost Swift's orbit. The $30 million mission aims to raise the telescope's altitude by 150 miles, potentially allowing it to return to work by September. If unsuccessful, the aging telescope, launched in 2004 to track cosmic explosions, was predicted to plunge to its demise by October.
