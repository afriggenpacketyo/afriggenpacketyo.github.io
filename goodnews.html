Explaining the Difference Between an E-CVT and a Traditional CVT

2025-07-07 17:05:00 EDT

This article clarifies the distinction between a continuously variable transmission (CVT) and an electronic-continuously variable transmission (e-CVT), commonly found in hybrid vehicles. While a traditional CVT uses a system of belts and pulleys to provide seamless gear ratio changes, an e-CVT has no belts or pulleys at all. Instead, it employs a planetary gearset and two electric motors to blend power from the gas engine and electric drivetrain. Although the driving feel can be similar, with a smooth acceleration that some dislike, the underlying technology is completely different, often resulting in better reliability, responsiveness, and fuel efficiency.
