China Develops World's First Zero-Emission Coal Fuel Cell Technology

2026-04-26 06:00:15 EDT

China has unveiled a groundbreaking direct coal power technology: the world's first coal fuel cell capable of producing electricity with zero emissions. This innovative system bypasses combustion entirely, instead using electrochemical oxidation of pulverized coal to generate electricity directly. A key feature is the in-situ capture and catalytic conversion of high-purity carbon dioxide into valuable chemical feedstocks or mineralized compounds. This development challenges traditional perceptions of coal as a heavily polluting fuel and offers a potential pathway for cleaner, more efficient energy production from an abundant resource.
