Descended from the Duwamish and Upper Puyallup peoples, they emphasize a deep connection to the fish and water, passing down traditional fishing practices like net casting. Despite historical struggles for fishing rights, culminating in the 1974 Boldt Decision, the tribe now faces environmental challenges.
