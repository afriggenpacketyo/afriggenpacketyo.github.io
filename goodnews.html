2025-10-23 05:00:00 EDT

The study, published in The Lancet, quantifies how each drug can affect weight, blood pressure, cholesterol, and heart rate. Researchers found that widely prescribed SSRIs like Zoloft and Prozac generally have fewer physical side effects than older medications. The findings are intended to help create individualized treatment plans, allowing patients to choose medications based on which side effects they most want to avoid, rather than labeling certain drugs as definitively better or worse.

China's Chang'e-7 Moon Mission to Conduct First-Ever Search for Water in South Pole Craters

2025-10-23 02:00:14 EDT

China's upcoming Chang'e-7 lunar mission will be the first in history to specifically search for water ice inside the permanently shadowed craters of the moon's south pole, according to the country's space agency. This resource is considered vital for future crewed missions, as it could be converted into drinking water, oxygen, and rocket fuel. The highly complex Chang'e-7 spacecraft will consist of five components: an orbiter, lander, rover, a mobile hopper, and a relay satellite. The mission plans to land in a location that receives over 100 consecutive days of sunlight to facilitate long-term operations.
