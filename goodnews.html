2025-10-20 16:57:17 EDT

The photovoltaic retina implant microarray (PRIMA) involves inserting a hair-thin implant under the retina, with glasses projecting images via infrared light. In trials, 26 out of 32 patients showed meaningful visual acuity improvement, offering hope for the 5 million people worldwide affected by this leading cause of blindness.
