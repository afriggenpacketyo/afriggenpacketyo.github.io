2025-10-19 00:56:00 EDT

It is common for sexual desire to wane in long-term relationships, a phenomenon that psychologists attribute to both hormonal changes and evolving relationship dynamics. Research also highlights a "gender orgasm gap" and the importance of female sexual satisfaction, which is often not prioritized in heterosexual relationships, leading to decreased desire over time. Experts emphasize that open communication about needs, along with maintaining physical affection and intimacy outside of sex, is key to navigating these natural shifts.
