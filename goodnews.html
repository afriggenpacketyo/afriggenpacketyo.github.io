2025-08-18 03:29:00 EDT

This success is attributed to its comprehensive Traffic Safety Development Programme, which includes reducing speed limits to 30 kmph on over half of its streets and around schools. The city also utilizes crash and driving speed data to identify dangerous areas and improve infrastructure like bike paths and street lighting. Helsinki's efforts align with the EU's "Vision Zero" program, demonstrating how coordinated planning and enforcement can drastically enhance urban safety.
