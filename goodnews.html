Elderspeak includes using overly simplistic language, inappropriate endearments, and plural pronouns that undermine an individual's autonomy. Studies show that elderspeak can lead to resistance to care among older adults, while CHAT training has been shown to reduce elderspeak and resistant behaviors. The program emphasizes addressing older adults respectfully and recognizing cultural differences in communication styles, promoting a more dignified and effective care environment.
