Detroit's Pewabic Pottery Thrives by Adhering to Arts and Crafts Traditions

For over 120 years, Pewabic Pottery in Detroit has survived economic turmoil by staying true to its Arts and Crafts movement principles of handcrafted quality over mass production. Despite Detroit's decline, Pewabic continued its traditional methods, using original machinery and hand-pressing tiles.
