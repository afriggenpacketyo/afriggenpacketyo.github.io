NHS to Offer Free Chickenpox Vaccine to All Young Children in England and Wales

2025-08-28 20:01:09 EDT

Starting in January 2026, all young children in England and Wales will be offered a free chickenpox vaccine on the NHS. Health officials hope the program will not only protect children from severe complications of the virus but also reduce the economic impact on families, as chickenpox is estimated to cause £24 million annually in lost income from parents taking time off work. A catch-up campaign is also planned for slightly older children.
