Steps to Take After a Tick Bite

2025-06-16 12:10:25 EDT

The UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA) has issued a warning about ticks due to the current hot weather and advises people to be tick aware when spending time outdoors. Ticks are small, spider-like creatures that feed on blood and can transmit infections such as Lyme disease and tick-borne encephalitis. If bitten, the UKHSA recommends removing the tick promptly with tweezers or a tick removal device, cleaning the area, and monitoring for symptoms like a spreading bullseye rash or flu-like symptoms. Precautions include using insect repellent, wearing long sleeves and trousers, and checking for ticks after being outdoors.
