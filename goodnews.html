Chinese Team Develops World's First Free Flight Control Software for Bamboo Drones

2026-04-04 02:00:10 EDT

A Chinese team has developed and released the world's first free flight control software specifically designed for bamboo drones, published in Heilongjiang Science. Unlike conventional composite airframes, bamboo structures introduce low-frequency vibrations (8–20 hertz) that standard flight controllers struggle to manage, limiting the industrialization of bamboo-based UAVs. The team redesigned control algorithms, leveraging bamboo’s natural vibration-damping and tuning an extended Kalman filter, to reduce control latency from 15-20 milliseconds to as low as 8-10 milliseconds. This innovation significantly improves responsiveness and stability, addressing a key barrier and promoting open-source development for unique material applications.
