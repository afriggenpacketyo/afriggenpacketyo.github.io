China Develops World-First Triad Nuclear Reactor System for Chemical Plant Power

2026-01-20 22:59:48 EST

China is constructing an innovative power system for a world-class petrochemical complex in Lianyungang, Jiangsu province, utilizing an unprecedented three-reactor nuclear setup. This system integrates two third-generation Hualong One pressurized water reactors with one fourth-generation high-temperature gas-cooled reactor (HTGR). The unique design aims to provide both electricity and ultra-high-temperature steam, reaching up to 1,000 degrees Celsius, which is sufficient for industrial-scale molecular breakdown. This marks the first time a single facility has combined multiple generations of nuclear technology to simultaneously deliver power and ultra-high-temperature process heat, signifying a major advancement in nuclear energy application for industrial purposes.
