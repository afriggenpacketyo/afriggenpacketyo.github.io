2025-07-15 16:49:31 EDT

Sotheby's is auctioning a 54-pound meteorite, the largest piece of Mars currently on Earth, with an estimated value between $2 million and $4 million. The meteorite, designated NWA 16788, represents about 6.5% of all known Martian material on our planet and was discovered in Niger's Sahara Desert in 2023. Scientists believe an asteroid strike on Mars dislodged the rock, sending it on a 140-million-mile journey through space before it landed on Earth. Martian meteorites are exceptionally rare, making this auction a significant event for collectors and scientific institutions alike.
