Cambodia Begins First Direct Air Shipments of Durian to China

2025-08-13 00:00:09 EDT

The first direct air shipment of fresh Cambodian durians has arrived in China, marking the launch of a new air corridor for fruit between the two nations. A two-tonne shipment was transported via cold chain logistics in just four hours, allowing the fruit to reach Chinese consumers with maximum freshness. Durian is now the fifth fresh fruit from Cambodia approved for direct import into the Chinese market, a sign of growing agricultural trade and customs cooperation. The move is part of China's broader "durian diplomacy" to diversify its sources for the popular fruit.
