2025-07-09 00:00:08 EDT

This massive ecological project is part of China's long-term effort to combat desertification, often referred to as the "green great wall." The method involves stabilizing sand dunes with straw checkerboards and then planting drought-resistant vegetation. Chinese officials state that this system creates an effective barrier against shifting sands, aiming to protect agricultural land and urban areas from encroachment.
