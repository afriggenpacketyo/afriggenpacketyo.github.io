Essential Reverse Mortgage Options for Retirees to Consider

2025-10-21 15:40:21 EDT

Retirees are increasingly considering reverse mortgages to access home equity without incurring new monthly payments, a crucial option amidst rising expenses and fixed incomes. Three main types exist: Home Equity Conversion Mortgages (HECMs), which are federally insured by the FHA and offer flexible payout options with built-in consumer protections. Proprietary reverse mortgages, often called jumbo reverse mortgages, cater to higher-value homes, exceeding HECM limits but come with varying terms and fewer federal protections. Lastly, Single-purpose reverse mortgages, offered by state/local governments or non-profits, are for specific needs like property taxes or home repairs, typically with minimal fees and lower interest rates. Understanding these distinct options is vital for retirees to make informed financial decisions tailored to their unique circumstances and financial goals. (not investment advice)
