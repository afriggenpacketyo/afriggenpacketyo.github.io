UK Experiences Significant Temperature Swings with Warm Days and Chilly Nights

2026-03-19 22:30:56 EDT

The UK is currently experiencing a "temperature rollercoaster," characterized by warm, sunny spring days followed by significantly chilly nights, a phenomenon scientifically known as diurnal temperature range. High-pressure systems and clear skies are the primary factors contributing to this stark contrast, allowing for efficient daytime warming and rapid overnight cooling. Some regions in England have observed a substantial 21°C difference between their highest and lowest daily temperatures, with frosts occurring after surprisingly warm afternoons. These considerable temperature swings are typical for early spring due to increasing daylight hours combined with still-cold ground conditions.
