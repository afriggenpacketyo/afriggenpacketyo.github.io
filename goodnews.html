Explaining the 'Rule of Thirds' for Retirement Income Strategy

2025-09-26 12:57:13 EDT

A retirement income strategy known as the "rule of thirds" is gaining attention as an alternative to traditional methods like the 4% withdrawal rule. The approach involves converting one-third of retirement savings into a guaranteed lifetime income product, such as a fixed annuity, to cover essential expenses. The remaining two-thirds of the portfolio can then be invested for growth or kept liquid for unexpected costs. This strategy aims to balance financial security with flexibility, providing a stable income floor that may reduce stress during market volatility and allow for more confident spending in retirement.
