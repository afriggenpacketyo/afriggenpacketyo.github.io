China Unveils World's Longest Cable-Stayed Mega Bridge Over Yangtze River

China has opened the record-breaking Changtai Yangtze River Bridge in Jiangsu province, now the world's longest cable-stayed bridge. Its distinctive asymmetrical lower deck, with a railway on one side and a road on the other, marks a pioneering design for large-span bridges.
