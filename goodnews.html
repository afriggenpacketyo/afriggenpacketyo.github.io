Chinese Team Develops and Releases World's First Free Bamboo Drone Flight Control Software

2026-04-04 02:00:10 EDT

A Chinese team has developed and released the world's first bamboo drone flight control software for free, addressing unique challenges posed by bamboo airframes. Conventional flight controllers struggle with bamboo's low-frequency vibrations (8-20 hertz), but the team redesigned control algorithms to match its structural properties. By tuning an extended Kalman filter and leveraging bamboo's natural vibration-damping characteristics, the system reduces control latency from 15-20 milliseconds to as low as 8-10 milliseconds, significantly improving responsiveness while maintaining stability. This innovation aims to overcome limitations in existing commercial and open-source controllers, thereby facilitating the industrialization of bamboo-based unmanned aerial vehicles.
