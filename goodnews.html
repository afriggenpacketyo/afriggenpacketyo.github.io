UK Experiences Significant Diurnal Temperature Swings with Warm Days and Frosty Nights

2026-03-19 22:30:56 EDT

The United Kingdom is currently experiencing a "temperature rollercoaster," characterized by above-average daytime warmth and abundant spring sunshine, which is then dramatically contrasted by significantly chilly, frosty nights. This notable phenomenon, known as the diurnal temperature range, has seen regions like the Midlands and central southern England record temperature differences of approximately 21C between the warmest part of the day and the coldest overnight. Early spring typically features these large swings due to the sun's higher position and lengthening days allowing for efficient daytime warming. Concurrently, the lingering cold in the ground and lower atmosphere, coupled with clear skies, facilitates rapid heat loss overnight, creating stark contrasts.
