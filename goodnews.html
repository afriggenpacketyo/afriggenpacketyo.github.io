2025-07-16 06:00:00 EDT

Researchers are identifying key lifestyle habits that can help mitigate the natural cognitive decline that occurs with age. Neuroscientists emphasize that understanding the mechanisms of brain aging is crucial, and simple, consistent behaviors like waking at the same time daily and staying physically active can significantly improve brain health both structurally and functionally over a lifetime.
