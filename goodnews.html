Debate Arises: Should Lower-Ranked Teams Always Host FA Cup Matches for "Magic"?

2026-03-09 13:29:21 EDT

While some argue that playing away from home creates the "magic" of the cup, offering a unique challenge and atmosphere, others point to the significant financial benefits lower-league clubs miss out on by not hosting higher-profile matches. For instance, Mansfield earned significantly less hosting Arsenal than they would have from an away game at the Emirates.
