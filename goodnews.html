Low-Cost Paper Antenna for 5G on Chinese Warships Developed

2026-04-20 00:00:09 EDT

Chinese researchers have developed a novel, low-cost antenna made from paper for 5G communications on warships. This innovation utilizes commercially available glossy photo paper and screen-printed copper paste, significantly reducing material costs by over 95% compared to traditional substrates. The paper-based antenna is designed to be biodegradable and suitable for large-scale deployment in the challenging maritime environment. This development contrasts with the high-end, expensive solutions being pursued by navies like the U.S. Navy, offering a scalable 'last-mile' communication solution that could facilitate widespread 5G integration into naval operations.
