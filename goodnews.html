2026-07-17 01:29:46 EDT

The changes allow princesses to retain royal status after marrying commoners and permit the adoption of male-line descendants from former imperial branches. However, the law continues to bar women, including Emperor Naruhito's daughter Princess Aiko, from inheriting the throne, despite significant public support for a female emperor.
