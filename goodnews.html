Framework for Assessing AI's Impact on Jobs and Expertise

2026-06-11 12:00:00 EDT

A new framework, based on three key questions, helps assess the impact of AI on jobs and expertise. It examines whether a job is a 'weak' or 'strong' bundle of tasks, considering how easily predictable 'clean' tasks can be separated from complex 'messy' ones. The framework also analyzes whether increased efficiency from AI leads to greater demand (Jevons paradox) or if AI commodifies or enhances human expertise. Applying this to professions like radiology shows how a combination of factors, including strong bundles and increased demand, can lead to job growth despite automation. The analysis suggests that jobs requiring deep expertise and complex human interaction are less susceptible to AI replacement.
