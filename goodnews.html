This approach prioritizes building emotional resilience, trust, and connection through open and honest conversations, setting clear boundaries with empathy, and encouraging independence. Emotionally safe parents accept their child's emotions without dismissing them, avoid shaming, view "bad" behavior as stress signals, and take responsibility after conflicts.
