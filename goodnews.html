Scientists Transplant Crossbred Corals to Bolster Miami's Bleaching-Devastated Reefs

2025-07-04 12:54:01 EDT

The goal is to introduce greater genetic diversity and thermal tolerance, helping the reef ecosystem become more resilient to rising ocean temperatures caused by climate change. This marks the first time internationally crossbred corals have been permitted for planting in the wild.
