Wharton Professor Discusses AI's Impact on the Future of Work

2025-07-25 11:21:19 EDT

Wharton professor Ethan Mollick suggests that while AI is not yet ready to replace most human jobs, it will be a "huge concern" for entry-level workers. He argues that AI's current capabilities make it a good assistant but not a full substitute for human roles, which often involve a bundle of diverse tasks. The primary challenge, he believes, is not learning to use AI, but developing deep expertise and judgment when AI can perform foundational tasks. Mollick stresses that companies and educators must actively shape AI's integration and create new training methods, rather than expecting employees to figure it out individually.
