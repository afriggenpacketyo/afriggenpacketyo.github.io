Study Suggests Vitamin D Could Slow Biological Aging

2025-05-26 19:54:06 EDT

A new study indicates that vitamin D supplements may help slow down biological aging. Researchers at Mass General Brigham and the Medical College of Georgia found that daily vitamin D3 intake could reduce biological wear and tear, equivalent to nearly three years of aging. The study tracked over 1,000 U.S. adults and measured telomere length in white blood cells, with those taking vitamin D3 showing significantly less telomere shortening compared to the placebo group.
