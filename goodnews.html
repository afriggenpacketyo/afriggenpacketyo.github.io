The proposed 10-year plan aims to reduce congestion and air pollution by discouraging the use of oversized and highly polluting cars, including a surcharge for diesel vehicles. As part of the changes, the city would be divided into new parking zones, with resident permits in the city center being phased out. The report also found that most benefactors are ambivalent about whether their heirs retain their advisor.
