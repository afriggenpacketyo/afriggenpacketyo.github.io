2025-07-21 19:01:10 EDT

This acceleration, caused by factors like changes in the planet's liquid core and atmosphere, could eventually require the removal of a second from Coordinated Universal Time (UTC) to keep it aligned with astronomical time. Such a "negative leap second" has never been implemented and raises concerns for critical computer and telecommunication systems that rely on precise time.
