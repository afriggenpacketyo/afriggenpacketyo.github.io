UK GPs to Receive Bonuses for Prescribing Weight Loss Drugs

While obesity experts caution that current NHS eligibility restrictions for these drugs remain tight, the initiative aims to expand access to modern medicine for a wider patient base, with expectations of 220,000 patients on Mounjaro by 2028.
