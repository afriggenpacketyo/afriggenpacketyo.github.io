Hawley Announces Expansion of Nuclear Waste Compensation Program

Senator Josh Hawley has announced a breakthrough in the fight to reauthorize and expand access to the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act (RECA), which provides compensation to individuals exposed to nuclear waste. The Senate's version of the "One Big Beautiful Bill" includes the largest expansion of the program since its inception in 1990. Individuals in regions of Missouri, Tennessee, Kentucky, and Alaska, as well as "downwinders" exposed to nuclear radiation from atomic bomb testing, will be eligible for compensation.
