China develops world's first high-voltage direct-connection synchronous condenser for renewable grids

2026-04-21 00:00:11 EDT

China has achieved a global first by successfully testing a 35-kilovolt direct-connection rotating machine, a type of synchronous condenser, developed by Dongfang Electric Machinery. This advanced technology eliminates the need for intermediate step-up transformers to achieve direct grid connection, a significant development for renewable energy grids. The innovation is driven by the rapid expansion of wind and solar power installations across China, particularly in regions with abundant natural resources. Synchronous condensers, first used in the 1920s, are crucial for managing reactive power in electricity generation, and this breakthrough enhances their efficiency and applicability for modern renewable energy systems.
