Options include Currently Not Collectible (CNC) status, which temporarily halts collection efforts while interest and penalties accrue, and an Offer in Compromise (OIC), allowing qualifying taxpayers to settle their debt for less than the full amount. Penalty abatement is also possible for first-time issues or reasonable cause, such as serious illness or natural disaster.
