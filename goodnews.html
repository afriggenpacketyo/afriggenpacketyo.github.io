Orionid Meteor Shower Peaks Next Week, Offering Celestial Spectacle

2025-10-17 19:58:22 EDT

The Orionid meteor shower is set to peak around October 22nd, offering a dazzling display of bright, fast-moving meteors visible until November 7th. Originating from debris left by Halley's Comet, these meteors burn up in Earth's atmosphere, creating long streaks of light. Viewers can expect around 15 meteors per hour during the peak, with the best viewing conditions occurring between midnight and dawn in dark locations away from city lights. No special equipment is needed, as the shower will be visible to the naked eye by simply looking towards the constellation Orion.
