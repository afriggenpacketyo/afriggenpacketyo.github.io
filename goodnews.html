NASA Prepares for Historic Artemis II Moon Flyby Mission This Week

2026-03-30 17:19:59 EDT

NASA is set to launch the Artemis II mission this week, sending a four-astronaut crew on a nine-day flight around the moon, marking the first human deep space mission beyond Earth orbit in over 50 years. Commander Reid Wiseman, pilot Victor Glover, astronaut Christina Koch, and Canadian astronaut Jeremy Hansen will pilot the Orion capsule, named Integrity, atop the powerful Space Launch System rocket. This test mission aims to thoroughly evaluate the crew ship's systems and is a critical step towards NASA's goal of landing astronauts on the lunar surface by 2028, preceding China's similar ambitions. The crew will fly farther from Earth than any humans before, reaching approximately 252,000 miles.

He was a fixture in DJ rankings, recorded influential BBC Radio 1 Essential Mixes, and his work profoundly shaped the psychedelic-infused sound of West Coast club music. Tributes from fellow artists underscore his lasting legacy in the electronic music community.

Decades-Old Human Remains from Bay Area Beaches Identified as Missing Banker

2026-03-30 17:02:08 EDT

Human remains discovered on Bay Area beaches in both 1999 and 2023 have finally been identified as Walter Karl Kinney, a Santa Rosa banker who disappeared in 1999. The identification was made possible through the efforts of the DNA Doe Project, which developed a DNA profile from a tibia found in 2023 and uploaded it to a genealogy database. This led investigators to Kinney's family and a 2003 inquiry about his disappearance. Using X-ray records, authorities confirmed the partial remains from both discoveries belonged to Kinney, bringing resolution to a decades-old mystery for his family. The cause of death remains unconfirmed.
