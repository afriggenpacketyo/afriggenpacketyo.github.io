Rare 1776 Copy of US Declaration of Independence Discovered in UK Archives

2026-07-02 19:39:29 EDT

A rare 1776 copy of the US Declaration of Independence, originally printed in Exeter, New Hampshire, has been discovered by a volunteer at The National Archives in Kew, UK. This significant document is the only known example of its kind existing outside the United States. It was seized by the Royal Navy on Christmas Eve 1776 from an American ship and was intended for rapid distribution across the American colonies. The discovery is hailed as "extraordinary" by officials, underscoring its immense historical significance as one of only 11 surviving copies from the first printings in New Hampshire. It will now be prominently displayed in an exhibition on the path to American independence.
