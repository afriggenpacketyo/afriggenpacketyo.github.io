2025-09-10 14:35:00 EDT

Discovered in the Jezero Crater, the vivianite and greigite minerals are known on Earth to form through reactions involving organic matter, often driven by microbes. While scientists emphasize this is not definitive proof of life, they consider it the most compelling evidence found to date.
