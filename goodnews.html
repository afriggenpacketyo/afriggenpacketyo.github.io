China Unveils World's First Zero-Emission Coal Fuel Cell for Electricity Production

2026-04-26 06:00:15 EDT

China has unveiled a groundbreaking direct coal power technology: the world's first coal fuel cell capable of producing electricity with zero emissions. This innovative system bypasses combustion entirely, eliminating carbon dioxide emissions typically associated with coal use. Pulverized coal undergoes electrochemical oxidation within the cell, directly yielding electricity without intermediate steam cycles or turbines. Crucially, the high-purity carbon dioxide generated is captured in situ and converted into valuable chemical feedstocks or mineralized into compounds like sodium bicarbonate. This breakthrough challenges traditional perceptions of coal-fired power, offering a cleaner, more efficient pathway for energy generation from coal resources.
