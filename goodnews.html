UK Parliament Approves Generational Smoking Ban and Vaping Restrictions

2026-04-21 05:34:38 EDT

The UK Parliament has approved the landmark Tobacco and Vapes Bill, which will implement a lifelong ban on cigarette sales for anyone born after January 1, 2009, aiming to create a smoke-free generation. This legislation also grants ministers new powers to regulate tobacco, vaping, and nicotine products, including their flavors and packaging. Additionally, vaping will be prohibited in cars carrying children, playgrounds, outside schools, and at hospitals, significantly expanding existing smoke-free laws. This measure is hailed as the biggest public health intervention in a generation, designed to save lives.
