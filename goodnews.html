Researchers from the Chinese Academy of Sciences have identified a new species of dicynodont, Dinanomodon guoi, from a fossil discovered in China's Gansu province. Dicynodonts, extinct non-mammalian therapsids considered "cousins" to modern mammals, were herbivorous and varied greatly in size.
