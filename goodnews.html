Most individual debts do not automatically transfer, typically being paid through the deceased's estate during probate. However, surviving spouses are generally responsible for joint debts they co-signed, or if they reside in community property states where many debts incurred during marriage are considered shared obligations. The article advises requesting full documentation from debt collectors before making any payments or acknowledging responsibility.
