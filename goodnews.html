Understanding Family Liability for Unpaid Nursing Home Bills

Families often wonder about their legal responsibility for a loved one's unpaid nursing home bills, especially given rising long-term care costs. Generally, family members are not personally liable unless they signed a contract accepting financial responsibility, are a spouse under specific state laws, or reside in a state with filial responsibility laws. The resident's estate is typically responsible for such debts after death. It is crucial for family members to carefully review admission documents and seek legal advice if pursued for payment, exploring debt relief options if necessary.
