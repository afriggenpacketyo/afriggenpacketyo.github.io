As the December 31 deadline approaches, financial experts are reminding retirees about the importance of correctly handling their required minimum distributions (RMDs) from pretax retirement accounts. Failing to withdraw the correct amount can result in a significant IRS penalty of up to 25% of the shortfall. Common mistakes include waiting until the last minute, miscalculating the amount, and overlooking old or inherited accounts that are subject to the rules.
