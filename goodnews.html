Study Reveals AI Productivity Can Lead to "Brain Fry" and Burnout

2026-03-08 13:57:54 EDT

A new study published in Harvard Business Review suggests that artificial intelligence, rather than solely easing workloads, can cause a new form of "brain fry" and burnout among workers. Researchers found that constantly juggling multiple AI tools and intensive oversight of AI systems led to increased decision fatigue and errors in about one in seven workers. The paradox is that AI can reduce burnout when offloading repetitive tasks, but it can also expand a worker's capabilities and accountability to overwhelming levels. The article emphasizes the need for businesses to redesign workflows and provide leadership and training to mitigate cognitive overload.
