2025-05-15 07:00:00 EDT

Perimenopause, the transition period leading to menopause, presents a range of symptoms including mood swings, fatigue, and hot flashes, making diagnosis challenging. Unlike menopause, there is no definitive test for perimenopause, requiring women to be persistent in seeking care and consider specialists. Treatment options include hormone therapy, which can be beneficial for some, and lifestyle adjustments such as diet and exercise. It is also important to test for other conditions with similar symptoms, such as thyroid problems and autoimmune diseases, to ensure accurate diagnosis and effective management.
