Northern Lights Expected to Illuminate UK Skies Again Following Intense Solar Activity

2026-03-21 04:57:17 EDT

The spectacular Northern Lights, known as the Aurora Borealis, illuminated UK skies on Friday evening and are predicted to make another appearance on Saturday, offering a second opportunity for widespread viewing. This breathtaking natural phenomenon is a direct consequence of heightened solar activity, specifically a strong geomagnetic storm triggered by a coronal mass ejection from the Sun. These charged particles interact with Earth's atmosphere, creating the vibrant colors observed. The intensity of this storm allowed the aurora to be visible significantly further south than its typical range, reaching areas such as Norfolk. Favorable weather conditions, with clear skies expected in many regions, promise excellent viewing prospects for this rare celestial event.
