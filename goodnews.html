2025-06-28 21:23:14 EDT

This phenomenon is caused by the prevalence of hard, dark surfaces like roads and buildings that absorb and retain the sun's energy, releasing it slowly as heat. The lack of vegetation and water sources, which provide natural cooling in the countryside, exacerbates the effect. With climate change making heatwaves more frequent and intense, the growing populations of urban centers are expected to bear the brunt of extreme heat.
