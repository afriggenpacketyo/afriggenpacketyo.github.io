2026-01-17 16:32:01 EST

Dietitians Michelle Routhenstein and Vandana Sheth compare the nutritional benefits of chicken and tomato soups, emphasizing that ingredients and preparation are key to their healthfulness. Tomato soup is praised for its lycopene, vitamins A and C, which support eye health and immunity, though it generally has less protein.
