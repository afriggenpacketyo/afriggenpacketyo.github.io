Alfie suffers from fibular hemimelia, a rare condition that caused his right leg to be over an inch shorter than his left. Experts at Liverpool's Alder Hey Children's Hospital utilized a new method involving an implantable magnetic lengthening nail, a technique previously considered unsuitable for younger children due to growth plate risks.
