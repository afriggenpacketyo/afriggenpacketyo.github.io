Greece always enters first as the birthplace of the Games, followed by countries in alphabetical order based on the host nation's language, Italian in this case. The host country, Italy, enters last, preceded by the nation hosting the next Winter Games, France. This year, the Olympic Refugee Team is not participating, and "Individual Neutral Athletes" from Russia and Belarus will not be in the parade. The parade, introduced in 1908, symbolizes national pride and peaceful global competition.
