Today, Fairbanks offers an unexpected array of international foods, from crepes and empanadas to Moldovan and Korean, largely due to independent, family-owned restaurants filling niches where larger brands face high transport costs. Local organic produce from greenhouses and innovative fusion dishes further contribute to this unique and thriving food landscape, earning the city a notable reputation.
