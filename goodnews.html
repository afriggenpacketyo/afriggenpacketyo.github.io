Remembering Julie Moore, the civilian who transformed military casualty notification protocol.

2025-05-25 09:13:55 EDT

Julie Moore, an Army wife during the Vietnam War, revolutionized the U.S. military's casualty notification process. Outraged by the impersonal telegrams used to inform families of service member deaths, Moore lobbied for a more dignified approach. She worked with the local Western Union office to personally deliver the news and provide comfort to grieving widows. Now, service members notify families in person and offer ongoing support. In 2023, Ft. Benning was renamed Ft. Moore to honor Julie and her husband, marking the first time a military base was named for a civilian.
