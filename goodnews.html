Saffie's improved vision has allowed her to participate in activities she previously couldn't, such as trick-or-treating, bringing immense joy to her family.\n\nResearch conducted by Great Ormond Street Hospital and University College London indicates that Luxturna can enhance sight and strengthen visual pathways, especially in younger children during critical developmental periods. While not a complete cure, the therapy has shown objective improvements in visual function, as measured by specialized tests. This development offers significant hope for children affected by inherited retinal diseases.\n\nThe success of this treatment highlights the potential of gene therapy in addressing previously untreatable conditions.
