California Offers Fire Relief to Homeowners Affected by Wildfires

2025-06-06 20:08:33 EDT

California is offering up to $20,000 in mortgage relief to homeowners whose homes were severely damaged or destroyed by recent fires. The CalAssist Mortgage Fund provides grants to displaced homeowners whose homes are uninhabitable due to disasters declared between January 2023 and January 2025. The relief can cover three months of mortgage payments, paid directly to the mortgage service provider. Eligible disasters include the Eaton and Palisades fires in Los Angeles, the Park fire in Butte, Tehama and Plumas counties, and the San Diego floods. Applications will be reviewed on a first-come, first-serve basis, with limited funds available.
