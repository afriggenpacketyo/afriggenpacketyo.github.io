Evaluating the Safety of Annuities as an Investment in the Current Economic Climate

2025-08-12 12:55:27 EDT

This article examines the safety of annuities as a retirement investment, especially amid current economic conditions of high interest rates and persistent inflation. The security of an annuity depends heavily on the financial strength of the issuing insurance company, as they are not FDIC-insured. While state guarantee associations offer a limited safety net, the biggest long-term risk is often inflation eroding the purchasing power of fixed payments. Different types of annuities, such as fixed, variable, and indexed, carry vastly different risk profiles that potential investors must carefully consider for their financial goals.
