Understanding Borrower Rights Under the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act

2025-10-07 12:22:47 EDT

This article outlines three crucial rights that borrowers possess when dealing with third-party debt collectors, as established by the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA). These protections include the right to be treated fairly and without harassment, prohibiting tactics like threats, profane language, or public shaming. Borrowers also have the right to demand and verify information, forcing collectors to prove the debt is legitimate before resuming collection efforts. Finally, consumers have the right to control communication, including the ability to demand in writing that a collector cease all contact, providing crucial breathing room to assess their options.
