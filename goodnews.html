UK Parliament Approves Hillsborough Law to Enhance Public Authority Accountability

Named after the 1989 Hillsborough disaster, where 97 lives were lost and police leaders spread false narratives, the Public Office (Accountability) Bill will now proceed to the House of Lords for final scrutiny.
