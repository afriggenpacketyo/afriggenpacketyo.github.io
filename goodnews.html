2025-05-19 01:10:06 EDT

To combat this, Nature Conservation Cymru is feeding them edible, biodegradable glitter to track their movements through their droppings. The glitter, safe for the voles, comes in different colors to distinguish families and their ranges. By understanding their territory, conservationists can remove invasive species or protect riverbanks to aid dispersal and survival. The team is testing this method on captive-bred voles, offering them glitter-covered apples to monitor consumption and tracking success, with hopes of reintroducing more voles into the wild.
