Celebrate Square Root Day

2025-05-05 17:19:14 EDT

Originated in 1981 by Ron Gordon, the day is celebrated with square-shaped foods and root-related activities. This year is especially unique as the year itself is a perfect square, 45 times 45 equaling 2025. Square roots are important in various applications, including cybersecurity, making the day a fun reminder of math's relevance.
