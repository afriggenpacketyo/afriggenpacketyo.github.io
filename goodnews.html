Total Lunar Eclipse Creates 'Blood Moon' Visible to Sky-Watchers Globally

2025-09-07 17:02:33 EDT

A total lunar eclipse captivated observers around the world, creating a phenomenon commonly known as a "Blood Moon." This celestial event occurs when the Moon passes directly through the Earth's darkest shadow, or umbra, causing it to take on a distinct reddish hue. The coloration is the result of sunlight filtering through Earth's atmosphere and refracting onto the lunar surface. The full eclipse was visible in its entirety from numerous countries, providing a striking and widely observed astronomical display for sky-gazers.
