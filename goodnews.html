Eligibility Requirements for Social Security Disability Insurance Explained

2026-06-10 14:08:30 EDT

Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) provides a financial safety net for individuals unable to work due to a qualifying disability. Eligibility hinges on having a medically determinable condition expected to last at least 12 months or result in death, and earning sufficient work credits through employment. Applicants must also be unable to perform substantial gainful activity. To improve chances of approval, comprehensive medical documentation and thorough application completion are crucial, as appeals are common after initial denials.
