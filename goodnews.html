This registry will include offenders' names, dates of birth, photos, and locations of their convictions for those with at least two domestic violence offenses after January 1, though it will not be retroactive or include addresses. The initiative aims to provide crucial information and potentially prevent future tragedies by increasing transparency around repeat offenders.
