US and India Announce Interim Trade Agreement, Strengthening Economic and Defense Ties

2026-02-07 01:25:00 EST

The United States and India have unveiled an interim trade agreement framework, reaffirming their commitment to broader bilateral trade negotiations. Under this deal, India will reduce or eliminate tariffs on various US industrial and agricultural products. Additionally, India plans significant purchases of US energy goods and aircraft, totaling around $500 billion over five years. This agreement follows President Trump's decision to rescind a 25% punitive duty on Indian imports, previously imposed to pressure New Delhi over its Russian oil purchases. The framework also includes a commitment to expand defense cooperation over the next decade, signaling a strategic alignment that deepens investment and technology partnerships between the two nations. Taiwan Integrates Conscripts into High-Intensity Live-Fire Drills to Bolster Reserve Force

2026-02-07 01:00:20 EST

Taiwan is implementing a new policy to significantly enhance its reserve force by integrating conscripts into full battalion-level units for high-intensity Lien Yung three-service live-fire exercises. Conscripts will now experience realistic battlefield conditions alongside professional soldiers, utilizing advanced weapons and integrating air force strike aircraft, army attack helicopters, and naval gunfire into single combat scenarios.
