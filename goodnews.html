UK Government Launches New Unit to Combat Organized Crime Fronting High Street Shops

2026-05-18 17:06:38 EDT

The National Crime Agency (NCA) will spearhead this three-year initiative, receiving £20 million in funding alongside allocations for trading standards and additional police officers in key regions like Greater Manchester and West Midlands. Home Secretary Shabana Mahmood emphasized the unit's goal to shut down these operations, seize illicit funds, and incarcerate organized crime leaders, with further plans to strengthen law enforcement powers.
