2025-10-31 15:55:49 EDT

The mission, focused on scientific research, includes China's youngest-ever taikonaut, Wu Fei, born in 1993. The crew will conduct 27 research projects in fields such as space life sciences and biotechnology, while four mice will be observed for the effects of microgravity. This mission is part of China's ambitious "space dream" under President Xi Jinping, which aims to land taikonauts on the moon by 2030 and eventually build a lunar base. China is also preparing to host its first non-Chinese crewmember from Pakistan.
