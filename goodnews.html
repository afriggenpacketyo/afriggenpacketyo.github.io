Scientists Discover Thriving Ecosystem in Deepest Ocean Trenches

2026-05-27 22:00:06 EDT

An international research team, supported by China's submersible 'Fendouzhe,' has discovered a previously unknown and thriving ecosystem in ocean trenches deeper than 9 kilometers (5.6 miles). This 'garden-like' community feeds on organic debris from above, existing under extreme pressure, perpetual darkness, and near-freezing temperatures. Researchers had previously believed that only limited life forms could survive in such hadal depths. The findings, reported in the journal Science, expand our understanding of life's adaptability in Earth's most extreme environments.
