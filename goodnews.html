AI-Powered System Deployed to Protect Gray Whales from Ship Strikes in San Francisco Bay

2026-05-21 17:03:26 EDT

Scientists are implementing an AI-powered thermal camera monitoring system in San Francisco Bay to mitigate deadly ship strikes on gray whales, whose populations are at a decades-long low. The system, utilizing Flir thermal cameras and WhaleSpotter AI, detects whale heat signatures from up to four nautical miles, subsequently alerting nearby ships to adjust their courses. This innovative approach aims to protect the increasing number of gray whales entering the bay in search of food due to climate change. The initiative, starting with installations on Angel Island and an SF Bay Ferry vessel, plans to expand across the entire bay, working in conjunction with a proposed "whale desk" within the US Coast Guard.
