Chinese Scientists Develop Crocodile Scale-Inspired Armor Technology to Deflect Projectiles

2026-05-11 02:00:08 EDT

Chinese scientists at Ningbo University have developed a novel armor technology inspired by the asymmetric, overlapping scales of crocodile skin, designed to deflect projectiles. Published in Acta Armamentarii, their research demonstrates that this asymmetric structure can indeed cause projectile deflection, a result verified experimentally. Instead of traditional hexagonal ceramic tiles, the team created a mosaic of diamond-shaped alumina ceramic units arranged at 45-degree angles, bonded with epoxy resin onto an aluminum alloy backing plate. This innovative design leverages the hardness, high compressive strength, and low density of ceramic materials to enhance protective barriers.
