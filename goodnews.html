2025-08-10 04:31:05 EDT

Known as programmable chromosome engineering (PCE), the system can precisely manipulate DNA segments ranging from thousands to millions of bases in higher organisms like plants. This breakthrough is expected to open new avenues for crop improvement by altering traits for better yields or resilience.
