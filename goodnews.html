The Origin and History of Welsh Rarebit

The name Welsh rarebit appeared later, with early English cookbooks using terms like Welsh rabbit for similar cheese on toast recipes. Rose Geraedts, who runs the International Welsh Rarebit Centre, believes that the dish became popular due to Wales' connection to cheese-making, despite its name being a sarcastic English joke, and it is a dish that many people enjoy.
