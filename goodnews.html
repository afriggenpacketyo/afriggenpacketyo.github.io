Dog Aging Project Explores Longevity for Canines and Humans

2026-06-07 19:00:18 EDT

The Dog Aging Project, involving over 50,000 dogs, investigates how canines age and develop diseases like dementia, mirroring human conditions. Scientists believe studying dogs, which age faster and share environments with humans, can provide crucial insights into human longevity and age-related illnesses like Alzheimer's. The project analyzes data on diet, exercise, and brain scans, revealing correlations like reduced disease risk in dogs living with others and higher dementia rates in inactive dogs.
