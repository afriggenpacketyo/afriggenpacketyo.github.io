Understanding the Income Requirements for Filing Chapter 7 Bankruptcy

2025-10-21 14:43:58 EDT

This test compares an individual's household income over the last six months to their state's median income for a similar-sized household. If income is below the median, qualification is likely; if above, a more detailed calculation of disposable income after expenses is required. The article explains that even if one doesn't qualify for Chapter 7, alternatives like Chapter 13 bankruptcy, debt settlement programs, or credit counseling are available.
