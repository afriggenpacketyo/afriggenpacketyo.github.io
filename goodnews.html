Experts and health departments confirm there is no scientific evidence for this assertion; in fact, UV radiation is the main cause of melanoma, and sunscreen helps protect against it. The rise in skin cancer diagnoses alongside increased sunscreen sales is attributed to factors like better diagnostics, greater awareness, and people spending more time in the sun. The article clarifies that while countries like Australia have high sunscreen use and high skin cancer rates, this correlation does not imply causation.
