Comparing the Suitability of Annuities and CDs for Retirement Planning

2025-08-06 12:51:59 EDT

This article explores the roles of certificates of deposit (CDs) and annuities in retirement planning, concluding that neither is universally superior but each serves different needs. CDs offer simplicity, predictability, and FDIC-insured safety, making them ideal for short-term goals and preserving capital without risk. However, their returns often fail to outpace inflation, and they do not provide guaranteed lifetime income. Annuities are specifically designed to address longevity risk by converting a lump sum into a guaranteed income stream for life, though they are more complex, less liquid, and can have higher fees. The best choice depends on an individual's specific financial situation, risk tolerance, and need for lifetime income. (not investment advice)
