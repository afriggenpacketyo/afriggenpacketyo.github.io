2025-08-23 18:42:59 EDT

The findings support claims of an independent urban civilization in southern India dating back to 580BC, challenging traditional narratives of Indian urbanization. This scientific endeavor aims to understand the ancestry and lifestyle of Keeladi's literate and skilled inhabitants.
