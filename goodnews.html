Daylight Saving Time to End on November 2nd

2025-11-01 06:56:45 EDT

Daylight saving time for 2025 will conclude on Sunday, November 2nd, at 2 a.m., at which point clocks will "fall back" one hour to 1 a.m. This change provides most Americans with an extra hour of sleep and shifts daylight earlier in the morning, while causing darkness to arrive earlier in the evening. The practice, which is not observed in states like Hawaii and Arizona, has been a part of U.S. law since the Uniform Time Act of 1966. Despite its longevity, the biannual time change remains controversial, with polls showing most Americans would prefer to stop changing clocks, though there is no consensus on whether to make daylight saving or standard time permanent.
