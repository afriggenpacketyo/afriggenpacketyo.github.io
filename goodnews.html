China Shifts Strategy in Africa from Building to Operating Key Infrastructure Projects

2025-12-07 23:00:14 EST

Chinese state-owned companies are altering their investment approach in Africa, moving beyond construction to become long-term financiers and operators of key infrastructure like railways and ports. An example of this new model is a $1.3 billion highway project in Kenya, where Chinese firms will finance, build, and operate the road.
