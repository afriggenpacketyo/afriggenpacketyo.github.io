SETI Institute Finds No Alien Life Signals from Interstellar Comet 3I/ATLAS

2026-06-05 22:42:04 EDT

Scientists from the SETI Institute used a radio telescope to scan Comet 3I/ATLAS, an interstellar object detected last summer, for signs of extraterrestrial technology. Despite wild speculation that the comet might be an alien spacecraft, the seven-hour observation using the Allen Telescope Array yielded no evidence of technosignatures. Researchers identified nearly 74 million narrowband signals, but all were traced back to Earth-based technology or orbiting satellites. The institute emphasizes the importance of continuing such searches as more interstellar objects are discovered, furthering understanding of cosmic phenomena.
