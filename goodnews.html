As gold prices surge, many investors are turning to Gold IRAs for retirement savings, which require strict adherence to IRS rules for storing physical precious metals. Investors can choose between segregated storage, where specific metals are kept separate, or non-segregated (commingled) storage. Reputable depositories provide comprehensive insurance coverage through major global insurers, protecting against theft, loss, or damage, and conduct routine independent audits to verify physical holdings against custody records. Before opening a Gold IRA, investors must verify the depository, storage option, insurance, and audit practices to ensure their retirement assets are properly protected and compliant.
