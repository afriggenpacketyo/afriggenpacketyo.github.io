Debt Settlement vs. Debt Management: Evaluating Speed and Impact

2026-04-22 10:51:00 EDT

Debt management plans, usually through credit counseling agencies, consolidate debts into a single monthly payment with reduced interest rates, typically taking 3-5 years to complete. While slower, debt management provides more predictability and less credit impact, making it a steadier path to becoming debt-free.
