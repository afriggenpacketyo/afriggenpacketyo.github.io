2026-06-19 11:29:59 EDT

This unique fungus preys on the well-known "zombie fungus" (Ophiocordyceps), which typically infects and manipulates insects before killing them. Instead of manipulating the insect, the new species infiltrates and feeds directly on the zombie fungus tissue within the host. This discovery highlights the immense biodiversity and intricate ecological interactions within Borneo's jungles, offering potential for developing new antimicrobial drugs and biocontrol agents, while reassuring that humans are not at risk from these fungi.
