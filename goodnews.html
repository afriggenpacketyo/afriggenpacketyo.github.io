2026-05-26 14:08:00 EDT

Garros achieved fame for his aviation feats, including being the first to shoot down an enemy aircraft with a gun synchronized to fire through the propeller. In 1928, a new tennis stadium in Paris was named in his honor at the suggestion of a former classmate. Garros' legacy is tied to the tournament due to his status as a national hero and his contributions to aerial combat innovation.
