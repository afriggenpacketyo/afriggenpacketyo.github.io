2025-06-26 18:31:57 EDT

The planet, named TWA 7b, is believed to be roughly the mass of Saturn and orbits a young, nearby star. Scientists used high-contrast imaging with a coronagraph to suppress the star's glare and reveal the faint object. While a small possibility remains that the image is of a background galaxy, researchers state that the evidence strongly points to it being a planet, potentially shaping the debris disk of its host star.
