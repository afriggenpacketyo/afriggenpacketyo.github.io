Psychologists Explain Why Sexual Desire Often Fades in Long-Term Relationships

2025-10-19 00:56:00 EDT

It is common for sexual desire to decrease after the initial passionate phase of a long-term relationship, a phenomenon explained by hormonal shifts and psychological factors. According to couple's therapist Andrea Seiferth, the initial hormone cocktail that drives lust is eventually replaced by bonding hormones like oxytocin, which can dampen sex drive.
