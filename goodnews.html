2025-10-25 18:00:14 EDT

China has become a global leader in producing lab-grown diamonds, with companies like Zhongjing Diamond using massive machines to create flawless gems in about a week. The sales of these lab-grown diamonds are rapidly increasing, with market projections showing substantial growth from $25.89 billion in 2024 to $74.45 billion by 2032. Fact Check: Cross-National Comparisons of European Sexual Assault Statistics Are Misleading

2025-10-25 14:58:00 EDT

A viral bar chart claiming the UK is Europe's "sexual assault capital" is misleading, despite using accurate numbers, due to significant differences in legal definitions and reporting practices across countries. DW Fact Check explains that the UK's seemingly high rate reflects its consent-based legal overhaul in 2003 and its "input statistics" counting method, rather than a higher prevalence of sexual assault. Criminologists advocate for victim surveys, not police reports, for accurate comparisons, as crime statistics often only capture a fraction of actual incidents. The article also debunks claims linking increased sexual assault rates to "uncontrolled mass immigration," citing a lack of evidence and contrasting data.
