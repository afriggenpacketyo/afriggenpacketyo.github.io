2025-09-07 17:02:33 EDT

This striking phenomenon occurs when the Moon passes directly through the Earth's shadow, or umbra, causing it to take on a reddish hue. The light from the sun is filtered through Earth's atmosphere, scattering blue light and allowing red light to pass through and illuminate the lunar surface. The captivating display was visible in its totality from numerous countries, offering a spectacular sight in the night sky.
