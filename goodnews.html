Tibet Quartz Discovery Enhances China's Self-Sufficiency in High-Tech Materials

2026-06-07 22:00:10 EDT

A significant discovery of high-purity quartz in Tibet's leucogranites is set to bolster China's drive for self-sufficiency in critical high-tech manufacturing materials. Research, published in the European Journal of Mineralogy, indicates these leucogranites can produce quartz with over 99.995% silica content, exceeding the purity threshold required for advanced applications in industries such as semiconductors, solar panels, and optical fibers. This finding presents a substantial opportunity for China to develop high-end quartz resources domestically, ensuring a stable and strategic supply chain. The development is crucial for reducing reliance on foreign imports and strengthening the nation's technological independence and economic resilience.
