Collection efforts can temporarily intensify after enrollment, particularly if payments are halted for settlement purposes, before negotiations begin. Factors influencing this timeline include the type of debt relief program, the age of the accounts, creditor policies, and the borrower's ability to fund settlements. While bankruptcy or legal intervention might offer faster relief, debt settlement and management plans require patience and understanding of the process.
