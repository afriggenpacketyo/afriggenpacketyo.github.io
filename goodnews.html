While non-biological processes could also be responsible, this discovery marks a compelling step in the search for extraterrestrial life.

China Unveils Record-Breaking Cable-Stayed Mega Bridge Over Yangtze River

2025-09-10 22:00:12 EDT

China has opened the world's longest cable-stayed bridge, the Changtai Yangtze River Bridge, in Jiangsu province, significantly cutting travel time between Changzhou and Taizhou. Stretching 10.3km with a main span of 1,208 meters, this mega-structure is the first river crossing to integrate an expressway, regular road, and intercity railway on the same structure. A distinctive feature is its asymmetrical lower deck, accommodating a 200km/h railway on one side and a road on the other, a pioneering side-by-side traffic layout for large-span bridges. This engineering feat showcases China's advanced infrastructure capabilities.
