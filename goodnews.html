A reverse mortgage is a financial tool for homeowners aged 62 and older, allowing them to convert a portion of their home equity into cash without needing to sell their home or make monthly mortgage payments. Borrowers can remain in their home for as long as it serves as their primary residence and they meet all loan obligations, which include paying property taxes, maintaining homeowners insurance, and keeping the property in good condition.
