2025-07-05 23:36:26 EDT

The UK government has announced a £500 million plan to establish "Best Start" family hubs in every local authority across England by April 2026. These hubs are designed to provide a wide range of support services for families, from midwifery and birth registration to debt advice and youth clubs. The initiative aims to support an additional 500,000 children in disadvantaged areas, building on a previous rollout of 400 hubs in 75 local authorities. The concept echoes the earlier "Sure Start" centers, many of which were closed after 2010 due to funding cuts.
