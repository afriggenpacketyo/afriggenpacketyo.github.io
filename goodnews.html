China Activates World's First Three-Band Optical Fiber System, Boosting AI Network Capacity

2026-06-13 00:00:06 EDT

China has successfully activated the world's first three-band optical fiber communication system, a technological breakthrough expected to significantly enhance the carrying capacity of future AI networks. This 35km link, connecting major computing facilities, uses three transmission windows—S-band, C-band, and L-band—within a conventional optical fiber. This innovation allows a single fiber to carry over five times the traffic of traditional systems and nearly half more transmission capacity per core. The development is likened to expanding a "two-lane motorway into a three-lane highway" without new infrastructure, addressing previous challenges in signal amplification and noise control in the S-band.
