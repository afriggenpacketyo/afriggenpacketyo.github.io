Understanding Spousal Responsibility for Credit Card Debt After Death

2026-07-01 11:47:32 EDT

This article clarifies whether a surviving spouse inherits credit card debt, explaining that it typically does not automatically transfer but is instead paid from the deceased's estate. Key exceptions include jointly owned accounts, debts that were co-signed or guaranteed, and liabilities incurred in community property states. If an estate is insolvent, creditors may only receive partial payment or none at all.
