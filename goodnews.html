Researchers utilized CT scans to examine the specimen's internal anatomy, as only one individual was available. The finding underscores the vastness of unexplored ocean depths and the potential for new discoveries.

2026-05-26 06:20:00 EDT

The article details the remarkable survival of Berlin's Jewish Hospital throughout the Nazi era, making it the only Jewish institution to operate and endure the Holocaust. The narrative highlights the controversial figure of Walter Lustig, the hospital's director, whose wartime actions have been viewed as both heroic and collaborative. Understanding UV radiation: dangers and protective measures

2026-05-26 06:17:03 EDT

UV levels are unusually high across the UK for this time of year, posing potential health risks despite UV radiation's role in vitamin D production. Excessive UV exposure can damage skin cells, leading to skin cancer and premature aging, as well as eye problems like cataracts. The UV Index (UVI) measures these levels, with higher numbers indicating greater potential for harm. The NHS advises using sunscreen with SPF 30 and at least 4-star UVA protection, covering up with clothing, and seeking shade during peak sun hours. Experts emphasize that there is no safe way to tan and that UV rays can penetrate clouds.
