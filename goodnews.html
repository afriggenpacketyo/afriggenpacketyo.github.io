Chinese Medical Team Achieves World First with Combined Pig Organ Transplant into Human

2026-06-09 01:59:25 EDT

A groundbreaking medical procedure in China has seen the first-ever combined transplant of a pig's liver and kidneys into a human patient who had been declared brain dead. The transplanted organs functioned within the 53-year-old recipient for nearly five days before the study was concluded at the family's request. This xenotransplantation, the transfer of organs between species, offers a potential solution to the global shortage of donor organs. Researchers identified early immune and metabolic responses that could guide future clinical applications of such procedures.
