Research Suggests Cows Exhibit Advanced Tool Use, Challenging Perceptions

2026-01-19 11:14:00 EST

Veronika was seen picking up objects like sticks and brushes with her mouth to scratch various parts of her body, even selecting specific ends of a deck brush for different purposes, such as bristles for broad strokes and the handle for delicate areas.
