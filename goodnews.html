Largest Known Piece of Mars on Earth to be Auctioned for up to $4 Million

2025-07-10 21:11:51 EDT

Sotheby's is auctioning the largest known piece of Mars on Earth, a meteorite named NWA 16788, with an expected price of up to $4 million. The 54-pound rock, which represents about 6.5% of all known Martian material on our planet, was discovered in Niger's Sahara Desert in 2023. Scientists believe an asteroid strike dislodged it from Mars, sending it on a 140-million-mile journey through space before it landed on Earth. The rarity of such finds makes this a significant event, offering a massive dataset for scientists hoping to unlock more secrets about the red planet.
