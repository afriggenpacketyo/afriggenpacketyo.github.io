Spain to Witness Rare Trilogy of Solar Eclipses Beginning August 12

2026-07-23 10:20:00 EDT

Spain and North Africa are set to experience a rare and spectacular trilogy of solar eclipses between August 2026 and January 2028. This exceptional astronomical event includes two total solar eclipses, with the first on August 12 traversing Spain from Galicia to the Balearic Islands, and a second in August 2027, dubbed "the eclipse of the century" for its extended duration over Egypt. An annular eclipse will complete the series in January 2028. Scientists emphasize the profound educational impact and valuable research opportunities these eclipses offer, particularly for studying the sun's corona and space weather, engaging students across continents.
