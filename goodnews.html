2026-03-05 22:59:33 EST

Designed to generate energy and transmute long-lived radioactive waste into less hazardous isotopes, this advanced reactor could provide a "green, safe, stable energy source for 1,000 years," offering a sustainable solution to both energy demands and nuclear waste management. While promising, no commercial ADS systems currently operate globally, making China's prototype a significant experimental step in nuclear technology. Researchers aim to complete the installation of the crucial superconducting particle accelerators this year, pushing the boundaries of sustainable energy and waste management.
