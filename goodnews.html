Three Key Steps to Take Before Paying a Debt Collector

2025-10-03 12:38:31 EDT

When contacted by a debt collector, it is crucial to avoid making immediate payments and instead take strategic steps to protect your financial interests. First, you should always validate the debt in writing to confirm it is yours and the amount is accurate, as required by the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act. Second, explore all your options, which may include negotiating a settlement for a lower amount, arranging a payment plan, or checking if the debt is past the statute of limitations. Finally, ensure you get any agreement in writing before sending any money to prevent future disputes.
