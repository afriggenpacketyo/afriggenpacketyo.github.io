Exploring the Benefits and Limitations of Single-Purpose Reverse Mortgages for Retirees

2025-10-08 14:03:45 EDT

For older homeowners on fixed incomes, a single-purpose reverse mortgage can offer a targeted financial solution for essential expenses like property taxes or critical home repairs. Unlike more common HECM reverse mortgages, these loans are restricted to one pre-approved use and are typically offered by state or local agencies. A key advantage is that they usually have much lower fees and costs, and no monthly payments are required until the homeowner moves, sells, or passes away. However, their availability is limited, and they often have strict income qualifications, making them unsuitable for retirees needing flexible funds for general living expenses.
