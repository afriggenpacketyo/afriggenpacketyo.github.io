2025-07-03 17:27:45 EDT

An opinion piece argues for a new approach to make popular anti-obesity GLP-1 drugs like Ozempic affordable through employer-sponsored health insurance. The author suggests eliminating the complex system of rebates and coupons between drug makers and pharmacy benefit managers. Instead, employers could directly access the drugs at the lower, direct-to-consumer price of around $500 per month and then offer cost-sharing plans to employees.
