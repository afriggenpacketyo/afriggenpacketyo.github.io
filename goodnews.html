UK Welcomes First Baby Born from Deceased Donor Womb Transplant

2026-02-23 19:39:50 EST

A "miracle" baby boy, Hugo, has been born in the UK, becoming the first child to result from a womb transplant using an organ from a deceased donor. Grace Bell, who was born with MRKH syndrome and lacked a viable womb, described her son's birth as incredible, expressing deep gratitude to the donor's family for their selfless gift and the dedicated medical teams. This groundbreaking achievement, following a 10-hour transplant operation and IVF, is part of a UK clinical research trial. The successful birth offers profound hope and a new pathway to motherhood for approximately 5,000 women in the UK facing similar challenges.
