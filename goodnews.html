The new assessment criteria will be based on Body Mass Index (BMI), with a normal range of 18-24.9 considered fully acceptable. Crew members with a BMI below 18 or between 25-29.9 will require medical and functional clearances, while those with a BMI over 30 will be immediately grounded and face pay cuts until they meet the acceptable standards.
