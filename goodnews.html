China Develops Hypersonic Drone Carrier Concept with Revolutionary Pivoting Wing Design

2025-08-16 22:00:14 EDT

Chinese researchers have proposed a design for a hypersonic drone carrier featuring a single, centrally mounted wing that can rotate up to 90 degrees. This innovative "scissor wing" concept, once explored by NASA, allows the aircraft to optimize its aerodynamics for different speeds. The wing remains perpendicular for efficient low-speed takeoff and subsonic flight, pivots to 45 degrees to mitigate shock waves at transonic speeds, and aligns parallel to the fuselage at Mach 5, turning the entire vehicle into a high-performance "waverider." This adaptability aims to solve the long-standing challenge of balancing low-speed lift with high-speed drag in aircraft design.
