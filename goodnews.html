J&J anticipates Icotyde becoming a leading first-line systemic treatment, bridging the gap between topical therapies and injections. FAA Strengthens Helicopter and Plane Safety Rules Near Major Airports

2026-03-18 12:21:06 EDT

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is implementing stricter safety regulations for aircraft operating in congested airspace around major airports, specifically by suspending the use of visual separation between helicopters and planes. This change follows a fatal midair collision near Washington D.C. and two recent close calls involving helicopters and commercial aircraft. The FAA cited an "overreliance on pilot 'see and avoid' operations" as a contributing factor to safety events. Henceforth, air traffic controllers will be required to rely on radar for active aircraft management to ensure separation, enhancing safety protocols in high-traffic areas.
