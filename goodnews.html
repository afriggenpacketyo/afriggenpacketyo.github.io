China Explores 'Scissor Wing' Design for Hypersonic Drone Carrier Aircraft

2025-08-16 22:00:14 EDT

Chinese researchers are developing a hypersonic drone carrier based on a novel "scissor wing" or oblique wing design, an idea once explored by NASA. Unlike conventional aircraft, this design features a single, centrally mounted wing that rotates up to 90 degrees to optimize performance across different speeds. At low speeds, the wing is perpendicular to the fuselage for maximum lift, while at hypersonic speeds like Mach 5, it aligns parallel to the fuselage, turning the entire aircraft into an efficient "waverider." This innovative approach aims to solve the aerodynamic compromises between low-speed take-off and high-speed flight.
