2026-03-07 16:19:40 EST

The recall, announced this week, states that the zipper head on the sleepsuits can detach, posing a danger to infants. The affected products, with specific batch codes PO30592, PO30641, and PO30685, were sold between September 2025 and February 2026 at major retailers including Amazon.com, Walmart.com, and Target.com, as well as the company's website. Although there have been at least 15 reports of zipper heads detaching, fortunately, no injuries have been reported. Consumers are urged to participate in the recall for store credit before discarding the product.
