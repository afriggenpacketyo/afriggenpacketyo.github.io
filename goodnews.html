Arthur Brooks Explains How Embracing Boredom Can Lead to a More Meaningful Life

2026-03-22 10:15:25 EDT

CBS News contributor Arthur C. Brooks argues that the constant distraction provided by modern devices, which eliminates boredom, inadvertently creates a major crisis of meaning and purpose. He explains that boredom activates the brain's "Default Mode Network," essential for mind-wandering, abstract thinking, and contemplating life's "why" questions. By constantly engaging with phones, this network remains inactive, leading to emptiness, anxiety, and depression.
