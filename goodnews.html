November's 'Beaver Moon' to Be the Largest and Brightest Supermoon of 2025

2025-11-02 00:52:27 EST

Stargazers are set to witness the largest and brightest full moon of 2025 on November 5th, an event known as a supermoon. This month's full moon, nicknamed the 'Beaver Moon,' will coincide with its closest point to Earth in its elliptical orbit, known as the perigee. This proximity will make it appear up to 14% larger and 30% brighter than a typical full moon. The term 'supermoon' was coined to describe a full moon that occurs when it is within 90% of its perigee. This will be the second of three consecutive supermoons this year, with the final one occurring in December.
