'Blood Moon' Total Lunar Eclipse to Be Visible Across the UK

2025-09-07 04:09:09 EDT

Sky-watchers in the United Kingdom are preparing to witness a total lunar eclipse, which will cause the full moon to take on a reddish hue, an event commonly known as a "Blood Moon." The spectacle occurs when the Earth passes directly between the Sun and the Moon, casting a shadow on the lunar surface. The reddish color is a result of sunlight refracting through Earth's atmosphere, a phenomenon called Rayleigh scattering. Visibility will vary across the UK, with eastern regions getting the best and longest views shortly after moonrise.

A team of researchers from the U.S. and China has created a novel method for launching tiny robots by harnessing the energy released from collapsing vapor bubbles. When these bubbles violently collapse, they generate a powerful force capable of launching millimeter-scale objects high into the air.
