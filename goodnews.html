The government is set to unveil a 10-year industrial strategy aimed at boosting growth by lowering energy costs for UK businesses. It also aims to speed up energy grid connections for new factories and projects. Prime Minister Starmer described the strategy as a "turning point" for the UK economy, supporting key industries and creating over one million new jobs.
