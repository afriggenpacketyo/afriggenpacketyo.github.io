2025-10-08 08:15:01 EDT

Financial experts are emphasizing the benefits of qualified charitable distributions (QCDs) as a highly effective tax-saving strategy for retirees aged 70½ and older. This move allows for a direct, tax-free transfer of up to $108,000 in 2025 from a traditional IRA to an eligible charity. While not a deduction, the amount is excluded from the donor's adjusted gross income (AGI), which can help lower Medicare premiums and prevent phaseouts of other tax breaks. Additionally, QCDs can be used to satisfy annual required minimum distributions (RMDs), providing a way for philanthropic retirees to give to charity while managing their tax burden.
