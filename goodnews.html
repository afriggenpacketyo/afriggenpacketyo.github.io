Egg Producers Settle Federal and State Price-Fixing Allegations

2026-07-13 11:11:37 EDT

Three leading U.S. egg producers, Cal-Maine Food, Versova/Centrum, and Hickman's Egg Ranch, have agreed to a proposed settlement with the Department of Justice and over a dozen states regarding allegations of price-fixing. The investigation claimed these companies colluded to artificially inflate daily egg price quotations, leading to higher prices for consumers between June 2022 and March 2025. Under the agreement, the companies are required to provide 53 million eggs to food banks and community organizations in the affected states, in addition to paying $3.3 million. The article notes that while the companies deny wrongdoing, this settlement aims to prevent future anti-competitive practices and ensure fair egg prices for American shoppers.
