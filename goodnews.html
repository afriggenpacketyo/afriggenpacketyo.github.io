Understanding Pentecost: The Christian Holiday Rooted in Jewish Tradition

Its roots lie in the Jewish harvest festival of Shavuot. The event inspired the disciples to spread Christianity, leading to the baptism of thousands and the formation of the early church. The holiday is observed in many countries as a public holiday, often symbolized by the color red and practices like scattering rose petals, representing the tongues of flame.
