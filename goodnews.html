Understanding Single-Purpose Reverse Mortgages for Senior Homeowners

2025-10-08 14:03:45 EDT

A single-purpose reverse mortgage is a lesser-known financial product that allows homeowners aged 62 and older to borrow against their home equity for one specific, lender-approved expense, such as property taxes or essential home repairs. Unlike more common reverse mortgages like HECMs, these loans are often offered by state or local agencies to lower-income seniors and typically have lower costs and fees. Borrowers make no monthly payments, and the loan is repaid when the homeowner sells the property or moves out. However, their availability is limited and funds can only be used for the designated purpose.
