This decision reverses two decades of rejections of similar state efforts and is expected to pave the way for more states to implement such restrictions. Nebraska will begin the program in January 2026, affecting approximately 150,000 food stamp recipients, with plans to evaluate changes in spending habits and provide assistance to retailers during the transition.
