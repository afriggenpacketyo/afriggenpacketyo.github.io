For straightforward cases where the debt is valid, individuals can respond to the lawsuit themselves and negotiate a settlement or payment plan directly with the creditor. Ignoring the lawsuit is the worst option, as it can lead to a default judgment allowing for wage garnishment or frozen bank accounts. For complex cases or substantial debt amounts, professional help from a consumer protection lawyer, credit counseling agency, or even bankruptcy proceedings can provide a path to resolution.
