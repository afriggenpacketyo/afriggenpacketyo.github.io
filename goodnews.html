Chinese Team Develops World's First Free Bamboo Drone Flight Control Software

2026-04-04 02:00:10 EDT

A Chinese research team has developed and released the world's first free flight control software specifically designed for bamboo drones, overcoming significant engineering challenges. Bamboo airframes introduce low-frequency vibrations, typically in the 8–20 hertz range, that conventional flight controllers cannot effectively manage. The team's innovation involves redesigned control algorithms that tune an extended Kalman filter and leverage bamboo's natural vibration-damping characteristics. This advancement reduces control latency from 15-20 milliseconds to a mere 8-10 milliseconds, enhancing responsiveness while maintaining stability. This open-source solution aims to accelerate the industrialization of bamboo-based UAVs by providing a flexible and adapted control system.
