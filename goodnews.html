Borrowers Have Key Protections During Debt Collection Process Under Federal Law

2025-10-07 12:22:47 EDT

Amid rising consumer debt and default rates, it is crucial for borrowers to understand their rights under the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA). This federal law provides significant protections, including the right to be treated fairly and without harassment, prohibiting abusive language or threats from collectors. Borrowers also have the right to demand and dispute information, requiring collectors to provide proof of the debt before continuing collection efforts. Furthermore, consumers can control how and when they are contacted, and can even demand that communication cease entirely, offering them space to manage their financial situation without undue pressure.
