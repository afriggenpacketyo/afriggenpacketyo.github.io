A lesser-known financial product, the single-purpose reverse mortgage, offers a targeted way for homeowners aged 62 and older to access home equity for a specific, pre-approved need, such as paying property taxes or making essential home repairs. Unlike more common reverse mortgages, these loans are typically offered by state or local agencies and nonprofits, often to lower-income seniors. They generally have lower costs and fees because their use is restricted.
