2025-08-16 02:00:20 EDT

China has achieved a significant milestone in its lunar exploration program with the successful completion of a key engine test for its Long March-10 rocket. This new-generation superheavy launcher is central to China's ambitions of landing astronauts on the moon by the year 2030. The test verified that the 21 YF-100K engines, which will power the rocket's first stage and two boosters, can operate in unison.
