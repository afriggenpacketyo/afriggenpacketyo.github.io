Massive Hydrothermal Vent Field Discovered in Pacific Ocean

2025-08-30 15:00:28 EDT

Researchers have discovered a colossal hydrothermal vent field in the Pacific Ocean, named the Kunlun field, which is over a hundred times larger than the famous 'Lost City' in the Atlantic. This deep-sea 'metropolis' features a system of large craters and dolomite walls, releasing hydrogen-rich fluids at temperatures cooler than typical 'black smokers.' Scientists believe this environment, which supports a diverse ecosystem of deep-sea life, could offer crucial insights into how life may have first originated on Earth. The discovery also challenges previous assumptions about where such large-scale hydrogen generation can occur on the seafloor.
