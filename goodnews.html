Shark tracking relies on dorsal fin devices breaking the surface to transmit data to satellites, with multiple messages needed for accurate location confirmation.

Germany Proposes New Measures to Tackle Soaring Rents

2026-02-12 10:08:00 EST

Germany's Justice Minister, Stephanie Hubig, has proposed new plans to combat spiraling rents, particularly addressing the exploitation of short-term, furnished rentals. The draft proposals include requiring landlords to disclose furnishing surcharges, capping them at 5% of net rent, and limiting rent control exemptions for short-term leases to a maximum of six months. Additionally, annual rent increases tied to inflation would be capped at 3.5%. These measures aim to curb a trend where furnished apartments in major cities can cost twice as much as regular ones, with professional agencies increasingly dominating the market. Tenant associations welcome the changes, while property owners express skepticism, fearing increased bureaucracy.
