Administered just six times a year, this innovative injection offers a convenient and discreet alternative to daily oral pills for pre-exposure prophylaxis (PrEP). Experts hail this "game-changing" development as a vital tool to help achieve the UK's ambitious goal of ending new HIV cases by 2030. The treatment is expected to particularly benefit vulnerable individuals who face difficulties with daily pill adherence, enhancing public health efforts significantly.
