2026-07-17 07:00:00 EDT

This method provides a gentle and rapid alternative to traditional techniques like harsh chemicals or boiling, which can damage delicate bones, or dermestid beetles, which pose containment risks. The superworms, which remain in their larval stage when kept together, are capable of meticulously stripping flesh from various animal sizes, from fish to wild cats. This innovative biological solution is gaining attention for its efficiency and safety, potentially transforming specimen preparation in natural history collections worldwide.
