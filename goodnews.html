Missing a tax deadline can trigger a cascade of penalties and interest, making an already difficult financial situation more stressful. The IRS imposes a failure-to-file penalty of 5% per month on unpaid taxes, capped at 25%, and a separate failure-to-pay penalty of 0.5% per month, also capped at 25%. Additionally, daily interest accrues on the unpaid balance, compounding without limit. The IRS will issue notices that can escalate to collection actions like liens or levies if ignored.
