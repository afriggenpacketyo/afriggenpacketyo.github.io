2026-07-09 18:48:10 EDT

Despite this setback, she continued her career in aviation, becoming a flight instructor and FAA inspector. In 2021, at 82, she finally realized her dream of space travel aboard a Blue Origin flight, becoming the oldest woman to journey into space. Her life was marked by unwavering determination and a passion for flight, inspiring generations.
