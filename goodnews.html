2025-07-13 04:50:04 EDT

The remarkable stone is believed to have been blasted off the Martian surface by a massive asteroid impact before it traveled through space and crashed into the Sahara desert. A meteorite hunter discovered the fragment in Niger in November 2023. Scientific analysis has since confirmed its Martian origin by comparing its distinct chemical composition to data gathered by the Viking space probe in 1976, identifying it as a rare olivine-microgabbroic shergottite.
