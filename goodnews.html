North America Anticipates Spectacular Aurora Borealis Display This Weekend

2026-05-16 14:07:59 EDT

North America is preparing for a vibrant display of the aurora borealis, or Northern Lights, this Saturday and Sunday night, as energized space particles interact with Earth's atmosphere. The phenomenon, characterized by green, purple, and pink streaks, will be most visible in the northern U.S. and much of Canada. The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) expects the geomagnetic storm to be strongest on Saturday, offering the best viewing opportunities just after sunset or before sunrise. Auroras are typically most visible in March and November in northern latitudes, making this a rare treat for many.
