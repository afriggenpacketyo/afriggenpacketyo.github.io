This flight will be the first time humans travel beyond Earth's orbit since the final Apollo mission in 1972, aiming to test critical life-support systems of the Orion crew capsule. The mission, launching from Florida's Kennedy Space Center, represents a crucial step toward NASA's long-term plans for a permanent moon base and a human landing near the lunar south pole by 2028.
