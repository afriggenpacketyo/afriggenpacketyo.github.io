The law aims to address two key injustices exposed during the long fight for truth: the lack of a legal duty for public bodies to cooperate transparently with inquiries and the imbalance in legal funding between victims and state institutions. Drafted by barristers who represented the families, the bill seeks to create a "duty of candour" and ensure parity of legal funding. The legislation is seen as a vital legacy for the victims.
