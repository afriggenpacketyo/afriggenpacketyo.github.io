2026-07-02 19:39:29 EDT

A rare and remarkably preserved copy of the US Declaration of Independence, originally printed in Exeter, New Hampshire, in July 1776, has been discovered by a volunteer within The National Archives in Kew, UK. This document stands as the sole known example of its kind found outside the United States. It was seized by the Royal Navy on Christmas Eve 1776 and was initially intended for rapid distribution throughout the American colonies. Described as an "extraordinary discovery," this find is particularly significant because such ephemeral copies were not designed for long-term preservation. The article underscores the immense historical importance of this artifact, which is now slated for display in an upcoming exhibition focused on American independence.
