The Department of Education found that the policy was discriminatory because it prohibited Native American-inspired mascots while allowing others that appeared to be derived from other racial or ethnic groups. Proposed resolutions include rescinding the ban, issuing a memorandum to public schools, and issuing apology letters to Indigenous tribes.
