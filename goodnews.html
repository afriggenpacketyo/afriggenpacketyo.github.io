The landmark legislation permits mentally sound adult citizens suffering from terminal, irreversible diseases to choose assisted death, to be performed by a qualified healthcare professional. This progressive legislative achievement aligns with Uruguay's long-standing tradition of socially liberal laws, having previously pioneered the legalization of cannabis, same-sex marriage, and abortion in the region.
