2026-06-12 10:42:19 EDT

Nitenpyram, previously approved for fleas, is suitable for pets weighing at least two pounds and aged four weeks or older. The New World screwworm, once thought eradicated in the U.S., has recently resurfaced in Texas cattle and a New Mexico dog, posing a renewed threat to warm-blooded animals.
