Shockwave Therapy Offers Relief for Persistent Elbow and Heel Pain

2026-03-02 05:00:00 EST

For active adults suffering from persistent elbow or heel pain due to conditions like plantar fasciitis, Achilles tendinopathy, or tennis elbow, extracorporeal shockwave therapy (ESWT) presents a viable alternative when conservative treatments fail. This FDA-approved method utilizes a wand-like device to deliver high-intensity pressure pulses to the affected area, effectively enhancing blood flow and stimulating the body's natural tissue repair mechanisms. Patients generally find the treatment uncomfortable but tolerable, with many reporting significant and lasting pain relief after just a few weekly sessions. Although often not covered by insurance, its ability to help individuals remain active and potentially avoid surgery makes it a valuable option.
