These lab-reared male mosquitoes don't bite and, when they mate with wild females, their eggs do not hatch, reducing the overall mosquito population. The warming climate allows mosquitoes to climb to higher elevations where the last remaining honeycreepers live, and this is a race against time to save these endangered birds.
