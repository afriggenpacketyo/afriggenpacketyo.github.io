China Unveils World's First Zero-Emission Direct Coal Fuel Cell

2026-04-26 06:00:15 EDT

China has unveiled a pioneering direct coal power technology, presenting the world's first coal fuel cell capable of generating electricity with zero emissions. This innovative system bypasses traditional combustion processes entirely, instead using pulverized and purified coal in an electrochemical oxidation reaction across an oxide membrane. A key aspect of this breakthrough is the in-situ capture of the resulting high-purity carbon dioxide, which is then catalytically converted into valuable chemical feedstocks like synthesis gas or mineralized into compounds such as sodium bicarbonate. This development offers a significant redefinition of coal's role in energy production, potentially transforming it into a clean and efficient power source.

They demand meticulous care, including precise "fishless cycling" for their tanks and specific dietary requirements, and can live for up to two decades. Animal welfare organizations, including the RSPCA, strongly advise potential owners to conduct extensive research and fully grasp the substantial long-term commitment involved, as many axolotls are unfortunately re-homed when owners underestimate their specialized needs.
