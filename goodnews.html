Understanding Grace Periods for Credit Card Payments

2025-07-22 11:26:03 EDT

This article explains the nuances of credit card grace periods, which are often misunderstood by consumers. While most cards offer a grace period on new purchases, allowing cardholders to avoid interest if they pay their balance in full each month, this benefit typically does not extend to late payments. Missing a payment due date can result in immediate consequences such as late fees, penalty APRs, and potential damage to one's credit score if the payment is over 30 days late. The article advises proactive communication with card issuers and exploring debt relief options if consistently struggling to make timely payments.
