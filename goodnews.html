Understanding How Written-Off Debt Impacts Credit Reports and Potential Removal Strategies

This negative mark can significantly depress credit scores and typically stays on a report for up to seven years from the initial missed payment. While difficult, removing written-off debt is possible under specific conditions: after the seven-year reporting period concludes, if the reported information is inaccurate or incomplete and successfully disputed with credit bureaus, or if the debt is a result of identity theft and genuinely not the borrower's.
