China-US Study Reveals Mars Has Solid Inner Core

2025-09-07 22:00:09 EDT

A joint China-US study analyzing seismic waves from marsquakes has discovered that Mars has a solid inner core, despite expectations that it should not. The team, using data from NASA's retired InSight lander, found the core to be approximately 600km in radius, about 18% of Mars' radius. This finding could revive the idea that Earth was not the only planet to have once developed plate tectonics. The discovery suggests that Mars, Earth, and the moon all possess solid inner cores, challenging previous assumptions about planetary cooling and core formation.
