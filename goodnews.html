Medicare to Begin Covering GLP-1 Drugs for Weight Loss Through New Pilot Program

2026-06-29 17:31:36 EDT

Effective July 1, a new Medicare GLP-1 Bridge pilot program will enable some Medicare patients to access GLP-1 medications for weight loss with a $50 monthly copay. This initiative represents a significant expansion, as it is the first time Medicare will contribute to the cost of drugs prescribed exclusively for obesity, rather than limiting coverage to conditions like diabetes. The program, scheduled to run through December 2027, includes drugs from Eli Lilly and Novo Nordisk. Eligibility is contingent on meeting specific BMI and health criteria, alongside prior authorization. While improving affordability, the copay does not apply to deductibles or annual out-of-pocket limits, and the program's continuation beyond 2027 remains undecided.
