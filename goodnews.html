China Constructs Pioneering Triad Nuclear Reactor System for Industrial Petrochemical Complex

2026-01-20 22:59:48 EST

China is developing an innovative nuclear system to power a new world-class petrochemical complex in Lianyungang, Jiangsu province. This groundbreaking project integrates two third-generation Hualong One pressurized water reactors with a fourth-generation high-temperature gas-cooled reactor (HTGR). The system is designed to generate ultra-high-temperature steam, reaching up to 1,000 degrees Celsius, capable of breaking molecules apart for industrial processes, alongside electricity generation.
