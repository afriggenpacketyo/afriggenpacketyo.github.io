Jack Dorsey Launches Bitchat, a Decentralized Messaging App Using Bluetooth

2025-07-07 13:26:10 EDT

Block CEO and Twitter co-founder Jack Dorsey has launched a beta version of Bitchat, a new peer-to-peer messaging app that operates without the internet. The app uses Bluetooth mesh networks to allow nearby devices to communicate directly, forming local clusters to pass encrypted, ephemeral messages from phone to phone. Designed for privacy and censorship resistance, Bitchat requires no phone numbers or emails and does not use central servers, ensuring communications can continue even during internet shutdowns. This experiment reflects Dorsey's ongoing interest in developing decentralized technologies for communication and social media.
