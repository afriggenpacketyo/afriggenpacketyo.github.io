What to Know About Normal Pressure Hydrocephalus, the Brain Disorder Billy Joel Has

2025-05-23 17:51:08 EDT

Billy Joel canceled upcoming concerts due to normal pressure hydrocephalus (NPH), a brain disorder affecting hearing, vision, and balance. NPH involves excess cerebrospinal fluid in the brain, causing difficulty walking, bladder control issues, and memory problems. It is diagnosed through CT scans or MRIs, followed by a spinal tap to confirm symptom alleviation. Treatment typically involves ventricular shunting to drain the excess fluid. Prognosis depends on early diagnosis and treatment, with gait and bladder issues responding better than cognitive symptoms.
