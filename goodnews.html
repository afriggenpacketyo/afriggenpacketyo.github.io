European Union Explores Homegrown Social Media Alternatives to Big Tech Platforms

2026-06-05 00:50:00 EDT

The European Union is actively promoting and developing alternatives to dominant social media platforms like Meta's Instagram and ByteDance's TikTok, aiming to curtail Big Tech's immense market power and address mounting criticisms.
