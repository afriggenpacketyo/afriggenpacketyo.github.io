New Galleri Blood Test Shows Promising Results for Early Cancer Detection

2025-10-17 18:02:31 EDT

A new study reveals "exciting results" for the Galleri blood test, capable of detecting over 50 types of cancer, many of which currently lack existing screening programs. Trials in North America involving 25,000 adults demonstrated that the test identified more than half of confirmed cancers at an early, more treatable stage, and correctly ruled out cancer in over 99% of negative cases.
