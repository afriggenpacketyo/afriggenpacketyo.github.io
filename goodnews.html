2025-10-17 05:38:14 EDT

As the head of the Socialist Party, he led a coalition government from 1994 to 1996. His most enduring legacy is the "Murayama statement," which expressed "deep remorse" and a "heartfelt apology" to Asian nations for the suffering caused by Japan's colonial rule and aggression during World War II. The treatment is intended for those at high risk for whom daily oral medication is not a practical or viable option.
