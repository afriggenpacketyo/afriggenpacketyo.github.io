Understanding and Protecting Against High UV Radiation Levels

2026-05-26 06:17:03 EDT

UV levels are currently unusually high across the UK, necessitating awareness and protective measures. Ultraviolet (UV) radiation from the sun is essential for vitamin D production but can cause significant harm, including DNA damage in skin cells leading to skin cancer, as well as skin aging and eye problems. The UV Index (UVI) measures radiation levels, with higher numbers indicating greater risk. Experts advise using sunscreen with SPF 30 or higher, wearing protective clothing and sunglasses, and seeking shade during peak sun hours. There is no safe way to tan, and UV rays can penetrate clouds, making protection crucial even on overcast days.

2026-05-26 05:16:00 EDT

Sonny Rollins, a towering figure in jazz and a legendary tenor saxophonist, has died at the age of 95. Known for his improvisational brilliance and relentless experimentation, Rollins recorded over 60 albums throughout his seven-decade career. He rose to prominence during the bebop era alongside other jazz greats and was revered for his bold tone and extended solos. Rollins' career included significant periods of retreat and practice, notably his time on the Williamsburg Bridge, which inspired his landmark album "The Bridge." His contributions also extended to popular music, and his 1958 album "Freedom Suite" addressed civil rights issues. Rollins is remembered as one of the last living greats of his generation.
