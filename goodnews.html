Japan's Departure Tax Revenue Reaches Record High in Fiscal Year 2024

2025-06-02 08:33:00 EDT

Japan's revenue from the departure tax, levied on travelers leaving the country, reached a record high in fiscal year 2024, according to the Finance Ministry. The total revenue eclipsed the previous record of 44.3 billion yen in fiscal 2019, reaching 48.1 billion yen ($34 billion). The tax, known as the International Tourist Tax, charges 1,000 yen to each traveler departing Japan, regardless of nationality. The Cloning Industry
