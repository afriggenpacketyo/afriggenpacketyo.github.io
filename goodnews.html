2025-07-11 06:00:00 EDT

In fact, the short cooking time of microwaves can help food retain more nutrients compared to methods like boiling. The primary risks associated with microwaves are burns from hot food and containers, and the potential for chemicals to leach from unsafe plastic containers when heated.
