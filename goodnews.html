China Tests "Octopus Tentacle" Robotic Arm for Satellite Refueling and De-Orbiting

2026-03-19 21:00:13 EDT

China is testing a commercial satellite equipped with an innovative "octopus tentacle" robotic arm designed for in-orbit refueling and a deployable device to mitigate space debris. The Hukeda-2 satellite, a joint project by Hunan University and Suzhou Sanyuan Aerospace Technology, will simulate refueling processes, from approach to docking. It also carries an ultralight sphere that can inflate to significantly increase atmospheric drag, aiming to reduce the de-orbit time for defunct satellites from decades to less than a year. These advancements are critical for extending satellite lifespans and managing orbital congestion.

"Tailgating" Becomes Criminal Offence at Football Matches in England and Wales

2026-03-19 20:01:16 EDT

"Tailgating" into football matches without a ticket will become a criminal offence in England and Wales under new laws taking effect before Sunday's Carabao Cup final. Offenders face up to a five-year banning order and a £1,000 fine. This legislation, the Unauthorised Entry to Football Matches Act, was introduced in response to the severe disorder at the Euro 2020 final in 2021, where thousands forced entry, and aims to enhance spectator safety. It also criminalizes the use of forged documents or impersonating staff for stadium entry.
