2025-08-09 13:13:29 EDT

A small meteorite that crashed through the roof of a home in McDonough, Georgia, has been analyzed by a planetary geologist and found to be approximately 4.56 billion years old. This makes the space rock about 20 million years older than our own planet. The meteorite is believed to have originated from the breakup of a much larger asteroid in the main asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter around 470 million years ago. Scientists plan to officially name the rock the "McDonough Meteorite," marking it as the 27th meteorite ever recovered in Georgia and a rare witnessed fall.
