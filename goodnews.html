2025-08-12 10:55:07 EDT

As the Federal Reserve shifts towards potential rate cuts, savers are evaluating money market accounts (MMAs) and high-yield savings accounts (HYSAs). Both offer higher variable interest rates than standard savings, but MMAs typically include debit card and check access, often with higher minimum balances or tiered APYs. HYSAs generally have lower entry barriers and are ideal for emergency funds or short-term goals, while MMAs suit larger nest eggs needing occasional access.
