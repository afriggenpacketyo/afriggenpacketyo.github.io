UK Experiences Significant Diurnal Temperature Swings Amid Spring Sunshine

2026-03-20 05:04:13 EDT

The UK is currently experiencing a "temperature rollercoaster," with several days of above-average warmth and spring sunshine followed by significantly chilly nights. High pressure systems are responsible for the clear skies, which allow the sun to rapidly warm the ground during the day, but also permit heat to escape quickly after sunset. This phenomenon, known as the diurnal temperature range, has led to differences of around 21°C between day and night temperatures in some regions. The article explains that longer nights and cool surface temperatures in spring contribute to these stark variations, with local geography and humidity also playing crucial roles.
