West Indies Cricket Icon Sir Garfield Sobers Passes Away at 89

Sir Garfield Sobers, a legendary West Indies all-rounder widely regarded as one of cricket's greatest players, has passed away at the age of 89. His illustrious career spanned from 1954 to 1974, during which he played 93 Tests, scoring an impressive 8,032 runs and taking 235 wickets. A highlight of his career was his record-setting 365 not out against Pakistan in 1958, a feat that stood for 36 years.
