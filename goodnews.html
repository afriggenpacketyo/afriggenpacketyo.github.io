Chinese Scientists Develop Balm to Boost Sperm Quality Amid Global Decline

2025-12-15 01:31:34 EST

Chinese scientists have developed a novel balm designed for external application to testicles, which they claim can significantly boost sperm quality and activity. Published in the peer-reviewed journal The Innovation, the study details successful outcomes in both animal trials and in vitro studies, demonstrating improvements regardless of whether sperm function decline stemmed from exposure to heavy metals, plasticizers, heat stress, or natural aging.
