Chinese Researchers Develop Low-Cost Paper-Based 5G Antenna for Warships

2026-04-20 00:00:09 EDT

Chinese researchers have developed an innovative, low-cost 5G antenna using commercially available glossy photo paper and conductive copper paste, specifically engineered for shipborne communications. This flexible and biodegradable design, detailed in the Chinese Journal of Ship Research, significantly reduces material costs by over 95% compared to traditional microwave substrates. The breakthrough enables large-scale deployment of 5G on Chinese warships, offering a scalable "last-mile" communication solution. This development presents a cost-effective alternative to the high-end satellite-based 5G solutions being pursued by other navies, such as the US Navy.
