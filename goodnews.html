Artemis II Astronauts Discuss Views of Earth and Mission Progress from Space

2026-04-03 03:42:59 EDT

Commander Reid Wiseman described seeing the entire globe from pole to pole, a moment that paused the entire crew. Mission Specialist Christina Koch addressed a minor toilet malfunction, humorously calling herself the "space plumber" and noting its resolution. Pilot Victor Glover emphasized the unity of humanity visible from space, stating that Earth looks like "one thing." Asia-Pacific Markets Mostly Higher on Hopes for Strait of Hormuz Reopening

2026-04-03 03:35:00 EDT

This comes as disruptions in the Middle East and the weakening Indian rupee have contributed to soaring fuel prices. The summit, convened by the UK, aimed to discuss securing safe passage through the Strait of Hormuz, though no concrete agreement was reached.
