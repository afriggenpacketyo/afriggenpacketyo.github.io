"God of Chaos" Asteroid Apophis to Pass Close to Earth in 2029

2026-04-15 15:43:16 EDT

NASA announced that the colossal asteroid Apophis, named after the ancient Egyptian deity of chaos, will safely pass within 20,000 miles of Earth on April 13, 2029. This rare event, occurring roughly once every few thousand years for an asteroid of its size (three football fields wide), presents an unprecedented opportunity for scientists to study a near-Earth asteroid up close with modern technology. Although there is no risk of impact for at least 100 years, Earth's gravity could potentially cause surface changes on the 1,115-foot-diameter rock, aiding understanding of its composition. People in the Eastern Hemisphere may even observe it with the naked eye.
