Understanding Credit Card Debt Relief Options

2025-06-13 10:33:59 EDT

Debt relief companies offer services like debt settlement and consolidation to help consumers manage overwhelming credit card debt. Debt settlement involves negotiating with creditors to accept a lump sum payment that is less than the total amount owed, potentially reducing the balance by 30% to 50%. Debt consolidation programs roll multiple debts into one loan with a lower interest rate, making it easier to manage debt. However, these companies cannot guarantee settlements, stop credit damage during the program, or prevent legal action by creditors, and they also can't charge upfront fees for settlement services.
