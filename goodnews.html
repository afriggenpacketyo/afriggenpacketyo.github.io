Understanding Aortic Dissections Following Senator Graham's Death

2026-07-12 22:44:55 EDT

The recent death of Senator Lindsey Graham from an aortic dissection has brought crucial attention to this life-threatening medical emergency. An aortic dissection involves a tear in the inner layer of the aorta, the body's main artery, which allows blood to flow between its layers and can severely impede blood flow to vital organs. Symptoms, often sudden and mimicking a heart attack with "ripping" chest pain, necessitate rapid diagnosis and emergent surgery for improved outcomes. Managing blood pressure and avoiding smoking are key preventive measures against this rare but serious condition.
