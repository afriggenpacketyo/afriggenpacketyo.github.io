New Non-Invasive Tests for Endometriosis Recommended for General Practice Use in UK

2026-07-07 05:16:09 EDT

New draft guidance for the NHS in England and Wales recommends two non-invasive tests for endometriosis, a painful condition affecting one in 10 women, aiming to reduce long diagnosis waits. These include a spit test for genetic material, Endotest, and a gut electrical signal test, Endosure, using abdominal sensor pads. Experts hope these tests will significantly shorten the current nine-year average diagnosis time, offering earlier identification and treatment for patients. While not standalone diagnostic tools, they should enable faster referrals and an end to symptoms not being recognized, according to Endometriosis UK.
