2026-02-27 14:06:41 EST

These lead "pigs," used to produce tools and weapons, date to AD 87 during Emperor Domitian's reign, underscoring the area's historical importance for its rich mineral deposits. The discovery offers new insights into Roman-era mining and trade in Wales.
