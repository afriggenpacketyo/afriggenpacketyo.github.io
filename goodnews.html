Rare Tang Dynasty 'Golden' Armor Restored from Tibetan Tomb

2026-01-19 01:52:53 EST

A Chinese research team has achieved a remarkable feat by meticulously restoring the only known physical example of Tang dynasty "golden" armor, unearthed from a royal tomb on the Tibetan plateau. This gilded bronze armor, found among other artifacts in the Tuyuhun royal tombs, was painstakingly reassembled using a "disassembling the whole into parts and reassembling the parts into a whole" strategy. The restoration process, spanning from 2022 to 2025, involved layered cleaning, extraction, and protection of each armor plate. A video reconstruction now vividly portrays the likely original appearance of this legendary armor, offering invaluable historical and cultural insights into the Tang dynasty.
