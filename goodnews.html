Dietary Adjustments Recommended for UK Heatwave Survival

2026-07-08 05:05:56 EDT

As the UK experiences temperatures exceeding 30C, experts suggest dietary adjustments to manage hunger and maintain hydration during a heatwave. Senior lecturer Aisling Daly advises against increasing protein intake, noting that protein digestion generates more body heat. Instead, she recommends focusing on water-rich fruits and vegetables like cucumber and watermelon, as well as pre-cooked protein sources such as bean salads and Greek yogurt. Warm or room-temperature drinks are suggested to be as effective as cold ones for cooling down, as they can stimulate more rapid sweating.
