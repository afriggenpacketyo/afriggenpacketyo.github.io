Donald E. Newhouse, Influential Newspaper Publisher and Media Heir, Dies at 96

2026-05-27 01:01:09 EDT

Donald E. Newhouse, the influential president of Advance Publications and a former board chairman of the Associated Press, has passed away at the age of 96. Renowned for his discreet nature and sharp business acumen, Newhouse oversaw 35 newspapers, successfully guiding them through the transformative early years of the internet age. He was celebrated for his hands-off editorial policy, which fostered journalistic independence and led to numerous Pulitzer Prizes for his publications. Despite his significant contributions, the media empire eventually confronted financial difficulties, necessitating the withdrawal of a long-standing job security pledge and the reduction of daily print editions for several papers.
