Guide to Buying Physical Gold for Retirement in 2026

2026-02-23 18:01:37 EST

As economic uncertainty persists with market swings and persistent inflation, many individuals approaching retirement are exploring physical gold as a means of diversifying their portfolios. Gold tends to behave differently than traditional assets like stocks and bonds, offering a potential hedge. The most direct approach involves a self-directed gold IRA, which permits holding IRS-approved physical gold in a secure, approved depository. Alternatively, physical gold coins or bars can be purchased directly from licensed dealers or banks, with coins offering more liquidity and bars being more cost-efficient for larger investments. Financial experts generally suggest limiting gold to 5-10% of a total portfolio, emphasizing the importance of understanding associated fees, storage considerations, and being vigilant against potential scams in the industry.
