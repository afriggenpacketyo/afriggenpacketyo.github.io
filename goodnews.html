Axel Springer Acquires Telegraph Media Group in £575 Million Deal

2026-03-06 13:00:47 EST

German media giant Axel Springer has agreed to acquire Telegraph Media Group (TMG) for £575 million, surpassing a previous £500 million bid by Daily Mail and General Trust. This acquisition, Axel Springer's largest since 2021, aims to preserve TMG's "distinctive character and legacy" while expanding its reach into the United States. TMG, which owns the Daily and Sunday Telegraph, has been without a stable owner since 2023 due to the Barclay brothers' debts. The deal follows a government investigation into the Mail's proposed takeover due to concerns about media plurality.
