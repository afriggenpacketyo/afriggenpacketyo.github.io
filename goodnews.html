Experts Warn Against Underestimating Care Needs of Axolotl Pets

2026-04-26 02:18:06 EDT

The growing popularity of axolotls as pets, partly fueled by their appearance in games like Minecraft, has led to a surge in ownership, but experts warn they are surprisingly difficult to care for. These critically endangered amphibians, which can live up to 20 years, require specific tank conditions and meticulous water testing, a process called a "fishless cycle." Many new owners underestimate the commitment required, leading to animals becoming poorly or being re-homed. Rescue centers and experienced owners emphasize thorough research and proper setup to ensure these unique creatures receive adequate, long-term care, highlighting the significant responsibilities involved.
