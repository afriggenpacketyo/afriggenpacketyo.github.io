The sinoatrial node, often referred to as the heart's "master conductor," regulates the precise electrical signals required for rhythmic contractions, and its failure can lead to life-threatening conditions. This engineered biological pacemaker offers a novel and promising approach to controlling abnormal heart rhythms, potentially revolutionizing treatment options.
