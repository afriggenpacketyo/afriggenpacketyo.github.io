Explainer: How Debt Resolution Affects Your Credit Report

2025-12-05 14:05:44 EST

For consumers overwhelmed by debt, debt resolution can offer a path forward by negotiating lower balances, but it comes with significant credit implications. The process of stopping payments to creditors while building funds for a settlement results in delinquencies that remain on a credit report for seven years. Once a debt is settled for less than the full amount, this notation also stays on the report for the same period. While the credit score damage is considerable, for those facing financial hardship, it can be a faster route to becoming debt-free and eventually rebuilding credit compared to decades of minimum payments.
