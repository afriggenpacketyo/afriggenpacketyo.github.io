Chinese Study Develops Low-Cost Paper-Based 5G Antennas for Warships

2026-04-20 00:00:09 EDT

A Chinese study has unveiled a low-cost, flexible paper-based multiple input multiple output (MIMO) antenna designed specifically for shipborne 5G communications, potentially paving the way for large-scale upgrades on Chinese warships. This innovation replaces expensive, rigid conventional foundation boards with commercially available glossy photo paper and screen-printed conductive copper paste, significantly cutting material costs by over 95%. The article highlights that this biodegradable solution is suitable for widespread deployment, contrasting with the US Navy's focus on high-end satellite connectivity programs. This development positions China's approach as a scalable and economical "last-mile" communication solution for complex maritime environments.
