Comparing Annuities and CDs for Retirement Planning

2025-08-06 12:51:59 EDT

When planning for retirement, both certificates of deposit (CDs) and annuities offer safe, predictable options, but they serve distinct purposes. CDs are simple, government-insured products ideal for short-term savings and principal protection, though their returns may not outpace inflation. Annuities are insurance contracts designed to provide a guaranteed income stream for life, addressing the risk of outliving one's savings, but they are more complex and less liquid. The best choice depends on an individual's specific needs, such as their desire for lifetime income versus liquidity and their overall portfolio composition. (not investment advice)
