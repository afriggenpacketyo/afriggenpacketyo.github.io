George Mills Sets British 5,000m Record at Diamond League Oslo

2025-06-12 16:49:15 EDT

George Mills set a British record in the men's 5,000m at the Diamond League meeting in Oslo, finishing fourth with a time of 12:46.59. Mills surpassed Sir Mo Farah's previous British best of 12:53.11, which had stood since 2011, taking 12 seconds off his own personal best. Nico Young of the United States won the race in a personal best of 12:45.27, with the event seen as an assault on the world record. Mills stated that achieving the national record was a key goal, and this performance indicates he is on track for a global medal this season.
