NASA's Perseverance Rover Discovers Strongest Hints of Ancient Life on Mars

2025-09-10 22:48:54 EDT

NASA's Mars rover Perseverance has uncovered compelling evidence in a dry river channel that may indicate ancient microscopic life, scientists reported. The rover collected samples from clay-rich mudstones in Neretva Vallis, revealing organic carbon and minuscule specks enriched with iron phosphate and iron sulfide. On Earth, these compounds are byproducts of microorganisms consuming organic matter, making this the most promising candidate yet in the search for past Martian life. While definitive conclusions await in-depth analysis of samples returned to Earth, this discovery marks a significant step towards answering fundamental questions about life beyond our planet.

China Unveils Record-Breaking Multi-Modal Cable-Stayed Bridge Over Yangtze River

2025-09-10 22:00:12 EDT

China has opened the world's longest cable-stayed bridge, the Changtai Yangtze River Bridge, in Jiangsu province, significantly reducing travel time between Changzhou and Taizhou. Stretching 10.3 kilometers with a main span of 1,208 meters, this mega-structure is the first Yangtze River crossing to integrate an expressway, regular road, and intercity railway on the same deck. Its distinctive asymmetrical lower deck, with a railway on one side and a road on the other, represents an innovative engineering feat. This design, accommodating the triple weight of rail systems compared to roads, showcases China's advanced infrastructure capabilities.
