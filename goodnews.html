Beluga Whales Relocated to Texas in International Rescue Effort

2026-07-22 17:17:53 EDT

Two beluga whales, Bertie Bott and Frankie, have successfully arrived at SeaWorld San Antonio, Texas, marking the initial phase of an international rescue mission from Canada's now-shuttered Marineland. This complex operation involved transporting the whales over 1,500 miles, with four additional belugas relocating to the Shedd Aquarium in Chicago. The effort, which took nearly two years of coordination, aims to rehome 28 whales that Marineland could no longer care for, ensuring they receive necessary medical treatment and a more dynamic environment. Caretakers report the whales are thriving and exceeding expectations in their new home.
