NASA Rover Discovers Rock Sample with Potential Biosignatures on Mars

2025-09-10 16:14:56 EDT

The sample, named Sapphire Canyon, was taken from an ancient river valley in Jezero Crater and exhibits textures and mineral compositions, including iron, phosphorus, and sulfur, that on Earth are often byproducts of microbial life. While scientists cannot yet rule out non-biological origins, they are excited by the finding. The next critical step is the proposed Mars Sample Return mission, which would bring the rock to Earth for detailed analysis with advanced instruments.
