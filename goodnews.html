Consumer Experts Reveal Common Marketing Tactics to Avoid During Holiday Shopping

2025-11-26 13:25:25 EST

Consumer experts are advising shoppers to be aware of common marketing tricks designed to encourage overspending during the holiday season. These tactics include creating a false sense of urgency with 'flash deals,' which pressures consumers into making hasty decisions. Another technique is 'price anchoring,' where an inflated 'original price' makes the sale price seem like a better deal than it is. Retailers may also use 'high-low pricing' by raising a product's base price before offering a large discount. To counter these strategies, experts recommend pausing before buying, comparing prices, and focusing on the actual value of an item rather than the perceived discount.
