Developed by psychologist Marsha Linehan, DBT techniques are now widely recognized for helping individuals manage difficult emotions and navigate stressful situations. The TIP skills, such as plunging one's face into ice water to trigger the mammalian dive reflex or engaging in intense physical activity, aim to lower emotional intensity from an extreme to a more manageable level.
