As the December 31 deadline approaches, financial advisors are warning retirees about common mistakes associated with required minimum distributions (RMDs) from pretax retirement accounts. Another frequent mistake is overlooking accounts like old 401(k)s or inherited IRAs when calculating the total withdrawal amount.
