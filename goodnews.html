Chinese Fossil Discovery Reveals Gradual Evolution of Bird Tails from Dinosaurs

2026-07-18 00:00:07 EDT

A team of Chinese researchers has published findings in Science Advances detailing how birds gradually shed their dinosaur tails, a significant evolutionary transformation. The study focuses on the development of the pygostyle, a fused skeletal structure in modern birds that anchors tail feathers and muscles, crucial for flight. Understanding this transition has been challenging due to the scarcity of early-diverging bird and birdlike dinosaur fossils. This discovery provides new insights into one of the most profound morphological changes in terrestrial vertebrate history.
