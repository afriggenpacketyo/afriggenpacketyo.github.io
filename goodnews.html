The Linguistic Function and Social Perception of the Filler Word 'Um'

2025-09-03 05:00:00 EDT

The filler word "um" serves multiple crucial functions in conversation, such as holding a speaker's turn, filling pauses while thinking, and softening one's tone. Its simple vowel sound makes it easy to produce, and similar filler words exist across many languages, reflecting neutral sounds in different dialects. While historically absent from formal writing, "um" is increasingly used in informal digital text to convey humor or confusion. Despite its utility, the word is often stigmatized in professional settings, where its use can be perceived as a sign of indecisiveness, though linguists view it as a natural and important part of spontaneous speech.
