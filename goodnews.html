Estonia Leads Europe in Digital Government by Prioritizing User-Friendly Services

2025-07-18 05:03:00 EDT

Estonia has become a global model for digital governance, with over 90% of its population using e-ID to access a vast range of public and private services, from filing taxes to initiating a divorce online.
