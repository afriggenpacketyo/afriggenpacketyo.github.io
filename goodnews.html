2025-07-17 10:25:21 EDT

A groundbreaking trial in the U.K. has resulted in the birth of eight healthy babies using a novel IVF technique that combines DNA from three people. The results, published in the New England Journal of Medicine, showed the technique was highly effective in reducing the transmission of mutated DNA. While hailed as a breakthrough, the method remains controversial and raises ethical concerns about genetic modification and the destruction of embryos.
