The discovery is significant due to the limited knowledge of deep-sea octopuses in the tropical Pacific. The specimen was preserved and studied using advanced techniques like micro CT scans to create a 3D model, allowing for detailed analysis without dissection. This finding highlights the unexplored biodiversity of the Galapagos marine environment, famously studied by Charles Darwin.
