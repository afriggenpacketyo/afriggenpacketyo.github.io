Android Phones Form Global Network for Earthquake Early Warnings

2025-07-29 21:15:01 EDT

Google has transformed its Android operating system into the world's largest earthquake detection network by utilizing the accelerometers built into billions of smartphones. When multiple phones in an area detect the initial, subtle P-waves of an earthquake, Google's system sends alerts to users who are about to experience the more destructive S-waves. This provides a crucial warning of 15 to 60 seconds, allowing people time to seek safety. The system has successfully detected over 11,000 earthquakes since 2021 with very few false alarms, while iPhone users can access similar alerts through third-party apps.
