2026-01-20 22:59:48 EST

China is constructing a pioneering three-reactor nuclear system to power a new world-class petrochemical complex in Lianyungang, Jiangsu province. This innovative project integrates two third-generation Hualong One pressurized water reactors with one fourth-generation high-temperature gas-cooled reactor (HTGR) to provide both electricity and ultra-high-temperature steam. The steam can reach up to 1,000 degrees Celsius, capable of breaking molecules apart for industrial processes. This marks the first time a single facility combines multiple generations of nuclear technology for simultaneous power and industrial process heat at such a scale.

While total exercise volume remains crucial, combining activities with complementary benefits, such as resistance training and aerobic exercise, is highly recommended.
