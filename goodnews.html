2026-07-13 02:29:00 EDT

These new rules, set to take effect in mid-2027, will ensure children under 14 and passengers with reduced mobility can sit with companions free of charge, and correct name spellings on tickets without fees. Airlines will also be required to display all-inclusive fares, provide printed boarding passes, and offer automatic refunds for class downgrades.
