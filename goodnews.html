EFL Championship Approves Expanded Six-Team Play-Off Format

2026-03-05 06:05:16 EST

English Football League (EFL) clubs have voted to expand the Championship play-offs from four to six teams, a significant change set to be implemented for the 2026-27 season. This new format will mirror the National League structure, allowing teams finishing from third to eighth place to compete for promotion to the Premier League. The third and fourth-placed teams will advance directly to the semi-finals, while fifth will host eighth and sixth will host seventh in one-legged quarter-finals. The decision aims to increase competitive fixtures during the closing stages of the campaign and offer two additional clubs the opportunity to reach the play-off final at Wembley and potentially gain Premier League promotion.
