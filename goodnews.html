California Desalination Plant Explores Water Sales to Arizona and Nevada Amid River Shortages

2026-03-03 13:07:45 EST

The revenue generated would help reduce costs for San Diego ratepayers, and the plant's output could increase if out-of-state agencies purchase water. This initiative, supported by California's governor and former US Bureau of Reclamation head, signifies a new approach to managing critical water resources in the drought-stricken West.
