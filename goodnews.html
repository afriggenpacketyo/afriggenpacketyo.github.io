Study Suggests Nitrate-Rich Beetroot Juice May Help Lower Blood Pressure in Older Adults

2025-09-01 11:26:11 EDT

A recent study from the University of Exeter suggests that daily consumption of nitrate-rich beetroot juice may help lower blood pressure in older adults. The research found that the juice altered the oral microbiome, increasing helpful bacteria and reducing microbes linked to inflammation. Nitrates are converted by the body into nitric oxide, which helps dilate blood vessels and improve vascular health. While researchers note more study is needed, the findings support the established belief that a diet rich in vegetables can help manage chronic health conditions.

Documentary Highlights Democratic Structure of Germany's Kammerphilharmonie Bremen

2025-09-01 11:23:00 EDT

A new documentary, "The Haydn Expedition," explores the unique democratic ethos of the Deutsche Kammerphilharmonie Bremen (DKB), a world-renowned orchestra. Founded by music students in 1980, the DKB operates with a grassroots structure where all musicians have a say in artistic and operational decisions. This collaborative approach, combined with their financial independence and the artistic direction of conductor Paavo Järvi, is credited for their success. The film parallels the orchestra's pursuit of freedom with composer Joseph Haydn's own journey as a freelance artist.
