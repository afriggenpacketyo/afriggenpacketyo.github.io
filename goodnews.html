Massive Hydrothermal Vent Field Discovered in Pacific Ocean Dwarfs 'Lost City'

2025-08-30 15:00:28 EDT

A deep-sea exploration vehicle has discovered a colossal hydrothermal vent field in the Pacific Ocean, named the Kunlun field, which is over a hundred times larger than the famous 'Lost City' vent field in the Atlantic. Located northeast of Papua New Guinea, this 'undersea metropolis' features deep craters and dolomite walls, releasing hydrogen-rich fluids at relatively low temperatures. Scientists are particularly excited by this discovery because its conditions may resemble those on early Earth, offering a prime location to study the origins of life. The field also supports a diverse ecosystem of deep-sea life, potentially reliant on hydrogen-driven chemosynthesis, and challenges previous assumptions about where such systems can form.
