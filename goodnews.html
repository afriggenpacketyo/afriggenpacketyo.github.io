EBV infects B cells, and in those with HLA-DR15, the virus can manipulate these cells to present myelin protein, tricking the immune system into attacking nerve fibers. This mechanism explains why only a fraction of EBV-infected individuals develop MS. Scientists are exploring vaccinations and targeted treatments to prevent or manage the disease.
