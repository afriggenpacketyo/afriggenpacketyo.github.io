2025-07-12 05:00:08 EDT

Located near the city of Yinchuan, the 40-square-kilometer area includes nine imperial mausoleums and over 270 associated tombs belonging to the Tangut people. The World Heritage Committee noted that the site serves as a significant testament to the cultural fusion and interaction among diverse traditions during that historical period. The complex also features ancient flood control structures and a large architectural site used for worship and management.
