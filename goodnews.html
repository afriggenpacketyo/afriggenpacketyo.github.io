Eligibility is limited to individuals with terminal illnesses causing unbearable suffering, with a life expectancy of six months (or 12 months for neurodegenerative conditions). UK and EU Finalize Post-Brexit Border Agreement for Gibraltar

2026-02-26 06:30:22 EST

The UK and EU have finalized a new treaty outlining a jointly-managed border deal for Gibraltar, aimed at removing the land border with Spain post-Brexit. Under the agreement, British travelers to Gibraltar will present passports to Spanish officials, and Schengen-style checks will occur at the territory's port and airport without Gibraltar formally joining the Schengen zone. The deal, likened to the Eurostar system at London's St Pancras, also ensures most goods for Gibraltar are cleared by EU customs in Spain, avoiding land border checks. This treaty seeks to bring clarity to Gibraltar's status, nearly a decade after the Brexit vote created uncertainty.
