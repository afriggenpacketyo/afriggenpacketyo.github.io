UK to Allow Foreign States to Own 15% Stake in Newspapers

2025-05-14 21:24:39 EDT

The new legislation allows State Owned Investors (SOIs) to take minority stakes, aiming to protect media plurality while helping publishers secure funding. Culture Secretary Lisa Nandy stated the changes will safeguard news media from foreign state control while recognizing the need for organizations to raise capital.
