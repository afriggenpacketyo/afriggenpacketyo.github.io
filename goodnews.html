Busy feeders in warmer months can become disease hotspots, significantly impacting bird populations, particularly greenfinches. The parasite causing trichomonosis contaminates food and water sources, making it difficult for birds to eat, drink, or breathe. The RSPB emphasizes the importance of hygiene and suggests offering small, daily amounts of food if feeding continues, along with regular cleaning of feeders and bird baths.
