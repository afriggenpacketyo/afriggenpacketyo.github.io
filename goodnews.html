Researchers Uncover Details of Uniquely Armored Dinosaur with Fused Spikes

2025-09-03 16:05:07 EDT

Paleontologists have gained new insights into a bizarre, heavily armored dinosaur named Spicomellus that lived over 165 million years ago in what is now Morocco. Recently unearthed remains reveal that the car-sized herbivore possessed an elaborate defense system, including three-foot-long neck spikes and ribs with spikes fused directly to the bone, a feature never before seen in any vertebrate. This discovery challenges previous understandings of ankylosaur evolution, suggesting their armor was more complex and developed earlier than previously thought. Scientists believe the elaborate spikes served a dual purpose for both defense against predators and attracting mates.
