Tax Relief Programs Available for Individuals Facing Medical Crisis Debt

The Internal Revenue Service (IRS) offers a range of structured relief programs specifically designed to assist taxpayers who are facing severe financial hardship as a direct result of a medical crisis. These programs include flexible installment agreements for managing payments over time, Offers in Compromise which allow eligible taxpayers to settle their debt for a reduced amount, and the "Currently Not Collectible" status to temporarily pause collection efforts.
