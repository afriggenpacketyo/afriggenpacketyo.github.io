2026-03-24 00:02:40 EDT

The practice, more common among affluent families and boys, may even lead to potential disadvantages such as a higher risk of dropping out of high school and a lost year in the workforce. While individual circumstances like developmental delays can still benefit from extra time, the article advises parents to carefully consider the rapid development that occurs before school starts and the implications of California's transitional kindergarten program.
