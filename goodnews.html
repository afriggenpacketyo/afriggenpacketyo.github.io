High Income Does Not Disqualify Americans from Medicare Eligibility

2025-09-09 12:50:31 EDT

Eligibility for Medicare is determined by age or disability status, not by income level, meaning you cannot make too much money to qualify for the program. However, higher earners are subject to an Income-Related Monthly Adjustment Amount (IRMAA), which increases the monthly premiums for Medicare Part B (medical insurance) and Part D (prescription drug coverage). The article explains that these surcharges are based on tax returns from two years prior and suggests financial planning strategies, such as strategic retirement withdrawals and Roth conversions, to help manage these additional costs.
