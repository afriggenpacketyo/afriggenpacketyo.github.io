While initial observations suggest the planet lacks a primary hydrogen-helium atmosphere, it may have developed a heavier secondary atmosphere, similar to Earth's. Understanding Medicare Eligibility and Costs for High-Income Earners

2025-09-09 12:50:31 EDT

This article clarifies that high income does not disqualify individuals from Medicare eligibility, which is based on age or disability status. However, higher earners are subject to the Income-Related Monthly Adjustment Amount (IRMAA), which increases premiums for Medicare Part B (medical insurance) and Part D (prescription drug coverage). The adjustment is based on modified adjusted gross income from two years prior, which can impact recent retirees. The article suggests strategies to manage these costs, such as planning retirement account withdrawals, considering Roth conversions, and appealing the IRMAA if income drops significantly due to life events like retirement.
