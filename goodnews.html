Cooper Lutkenhaus Becomes Youngest World Indoor Athletics Champion with 800m Gold

2026-03-22 16:08:59 EDT

US teenager Cooper Lutkenhaus made significant history on Sunday by winning the 800m gold medal at the World Indoor Athletics Championships, thereby becoming the youngest ever champion in the event at just 17 years old. The high school student from Northwest High School in Texas, who notably took time off from his classes to compete at the prestigious championships, secured his gold with a time of 1 minute, 44.24 seconds, narrowly finishing ahead of Belgium’s Eliott Crestan.
