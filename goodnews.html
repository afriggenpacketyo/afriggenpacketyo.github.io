2026-02-23 02:06:38 EST

This innovative approach was inspired by the market success of fragrant rice, whose signature scent the engineered tomatoes now mimic. The team, comprising Chinese and Australian researchers, published their findings in the Journal of Integrative Agriculture, highlighting significant advancements in genetic engineering for agriculture and potentially opening new avenues for novel food product characteristics.
