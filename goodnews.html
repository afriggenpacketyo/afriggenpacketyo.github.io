UK Home Office Unveils New Capped Refugee Sponsorship Routes

2026-06-27 03:04:04 EDT

The UK Home Office announced new "capped safe and legal" routes for refugees, inspired by Canada's system, allowing organizations like universities and businesses to sponsor applicants from later this year.
