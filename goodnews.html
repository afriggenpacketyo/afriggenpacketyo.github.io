Chinese Study Develops Low-Cost Paper Antenna for Warship 5G Upgrades

2026-04-20 00:00:09 EDT

Chinese researchers have successfully developed a groundbreaking low-cost, paper-based flexible antenna specifically engineered for 5G communications on warships. This innovative design replaces expensive, rigid conventional foundation boards with commercially available glossy photo paper combined with screen-printed conductive copper paste, slashing material costs by over 95%. The biodegradable nature of this antenna also makes it suitable for large-scale deployment, offering a highly scalable "last-mile" communication solution for naval forces. This technological advancement could enable China to integrate advanced 5G capabilities into its maritime operations more economically, contrasting with the multi-million dollar high-end satellite-based solutions being explored by navies in other nations, such as the U.S.
