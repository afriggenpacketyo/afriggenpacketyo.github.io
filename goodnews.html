2026-05-24 03:00:07 EDT

This milestone follows a government ban on raw mineral exports, implemented to curb widespread smuggling and maximize value from the country's rich resources. The Arcadia plant, operated by China’s Zhejiang Huayou Cobalt, can produce 50,000 metric tonnes of lithium sulphate annually, which can be further refined into battery-manufacturing materials like lithium hydroxide and carbonate.
