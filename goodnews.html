2026-03-22 02:00:18 EDT

The project, led by Hunan Hongshuo Biotechnology Co. and supported by scientific institutes, aims to address "weak links" in China's supply chains and reduce its heavy reliance on food imports. This development is a strategic step towards national food security, positioning key commodities like food and energy on the same strategic footing to prevent and mitigate major risks in systemically important areas.
