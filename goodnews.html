Rose Parade Float Sets Guinness World Record for Largest Animatronic Fish

2026-01-03 07:00:00 EST

The UPS Store float at this year's Rose Parade achieved a Guinness World Record for the world's largest animatronic fish, featuring a towering 34-foot-tall mechanical seahorse. Designed by Charles T. Meier and engineered by Kyle Amerine, the 55-foot-long float also received the "Director Award" for design. Despite rainy weather posing challenges for glues and hydraulics, the massive seahorse, which coached fin-flapping baby sea creatures, completed its route intact. This remarkable achievement, a first world record for the float-building team Artistic Entertainment Services in 18 years, celebrates exceptional mechanics and creative engineering.
