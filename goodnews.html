FedEx Founder Fred Smith Dies at 80

2025-06-22 09:13:25 EDT

Fred Smith, the founder of FedEx Corp., passed away at the age of 80, leaving behind a legacy of revolutionizing the express delivery industry. Starting in 1973, FedEx transformed package delivery by offering faster service than the postal service, and Smith's leadership turned the company into a global transportation and logistics giant. A Marine Corps veteran, Smith attributed his business acumen to his military experience and created a delivery system based on coordinated air cargo flights centered on a main hub. His company also played a major role in the shift by American business and industry to a greater use of time-sensitive deliveries and less dependence on large inventories and warehouses.
