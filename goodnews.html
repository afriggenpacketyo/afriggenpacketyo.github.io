2026-03-08 02:30:08 EDT

This innovation, unveiled in Nature Materials, involves quantum dots designed to emit precisely two photons at a time, a challenging feat in quantum computing. Under pulsed excitation, their new emitter achieved an impressive 98.3% paired photon emission and a 29.9% generation efficiency. Led by Yuan Zhiliang from the Beijing Academy of Quantum Information Sciences, this advance is lauded as "international best-in-class" and represents a crucial step forward for quantum information science and related applications.
