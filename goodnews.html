The Science and Craft of Creating Olympic Ice for Winter Games

Different sports require varying ice conditions; curling needs a pebbled texture, ice hockey a harder surface for quick stops, and long track speed skating a thin, cold, hard surface. Technicians at the Milan-Cortina Games face the challenge of adapting venues to these diverse requirements.
