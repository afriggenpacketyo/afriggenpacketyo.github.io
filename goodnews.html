New Daily Pill Shows Promise in Sustaining Weight Loss Post-Obesity Injections

2026-05-12 19:06:23 EDT

A new daily pill, orforglipron, shows significant promise in helping patients maintain weight loss after discontinuing GLP-1 injections, potentially offering a more convenient and affordable alternative. Trials funded by manufacturer Eli Lilly found that participants who took orforglipron daily for a year kept over 70% of their prior weight loss off, compared to 38-50% for a placebo group. This oral medication works similarly to existing obesity jabs by mimicking a natural hormone that effectively reduces appetite and promotes a feeling of fullness. While more research is needed to determine the optimal duration of treatment, this development could revolutionize weight management by providing a highly effective, non-injectable option.
