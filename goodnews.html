TSA to Implement $45 Fee for Air Travelers Without REAL ID Starting in February

2025-12-02 01:38:34 EST

The Transportation Security Administration has announced that starting in February, U.S. air travelers without a federally compliant REAL ID will be required to pay a $45 fee. This fee applies to passengers 18 and older who lack a REAL ID or another accepted form of identification, such as a passport. The charge is for using the TSA's alternative "Confirm.ID" verification system, a process that could take up to 30 minutes at the airport. Officials state that 94% of travelers are already compliant and the fee is meant to encourage the remaining passengers to obtain the updated identification.
