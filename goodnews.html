Chinese Scientists Restore Ancient Calligraphic Masterpiece Using AI Technology

Only about a quarter of its verses had survived through time, leaving much of its beauty lost. This allowed them to replicate and expand upon the original style and form, culminating in a complete rendition of 919 characters presented in its full scroll form. This groundbreaking endeavor vividly illuminates the immense fusion potential of 'AI plus culture,' demonstrating how traditional calligraphic art can gain fresh and vibrant modes of expression in the digital age.
