India's Inflation Eases to 2.82% in May Due to Sliding Food Prices

2025-06-12 07:06:02 EDT

India's consumer price index (CPI) eased to 2.82% in May, lower than expected, primarily due to a decline in food prices. This marks the lowest level since February 2019, with food inflation dropping to 0.99% and vegetable inflation decreasing by 13.7%. The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has been relaxing monetary policy in response to these easing inflationary pressures, recently cutting its benchmark repo rate by 50 basis points to 5.50%. Nomura economists anticipate further rate cuts, projecting a subdued CPI of 3.3% for fiscal year 2026. (not investment advice)

Trump Administration and GOP Congress Overturn California's EPA Authority

2025-06-12 07:00:30 EDT

President Donald Trump and congressional Republicans have reversed California's authority to dictate national emissions standards for new vehicles. This action overturns a Biden administration decision that allowed California to set its own emissions regulations, including a requirement for nearly all vehicles sold in the state to be electric by 2035. Republicans argue that the Clean Air Act was not intended to grant one state the power to control emissions standards for entire industries nationwide.
