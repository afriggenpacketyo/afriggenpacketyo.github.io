2026-03-13 08:57:02 EDT

This change recognizes the blurring line between gaming and gambling associated with in-game purchases of random items. Games with time-limited systems like battle passes will receive a PEGI 12 rating, and those with NFTs will be rated PEGI 18. While considered a step in the right direction, some advocate for retrospective application to existing titles.
