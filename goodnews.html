NASA Confirms Discovery of Third Known Interstellar Object Passing Through Solar System

2025-07-03 17:56:06 EDT

NASA has announced the discovery of a new interstellar comet, designated 3I/ATLAS, which is the third known object from another star system to visit our solar system. The comet was detected using a telescope in Chile and is currently located about 420 million miles from Earth, traveling at approximately 37 miles per second. Scientists are now monitoring the object to determine its size and composition as it makes its closest approach to the sun in late October. This visitor, which poses no threat to Earth, offers another rare opportunity for astronomers to study material that originated from outside our own stellar neighborhood.
