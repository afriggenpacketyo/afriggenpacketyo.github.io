Mars Rover Captures Evidence of Ancient Water on the Red Planet

2025-06-25 12:59:22 EDT

NASA's Curiosity rover has captured detailed images of ridges on Mars, providing compelling evidence of the planet's watery past. The images reveal a "boxwork pattern" of low ridges believed to have formed from groundwater trickling through rock, leaving behind hardened mineral deposits. These spiderweb-like patterns, previously only seen from orbit, contain small fractures filled with calcium sulfate, a substance not previously found in this part of Mount Sharp. Scientists are intrigued by the unique hardening of these ridges and the reappearance of calcium sulfate, aiming to uncover more about Mars's history and potential for past life as the rover continues its exploration.

Understanding Credit Card Grace Periods and Their Importance

2025-06-25 12:51:58 EDT

A credit card's grace period is the time between the end of a billing cycle and the payment due date during which no interest is charged on new purchases, provided the full statement balance is paid by the due date. This interest-free loan typically lasts 21 to 25 days, allowing users to avoid interest charges by consistently paying their balance in full each month. Carrying a balance from month to month usually results in losing the grace period on new purchases, causing interest to accrue immediately. Utilizing the grace period effectively involves automating payments, tracking billing cycles, avoiding cash advances, and ensuring the card offers a grace period.
