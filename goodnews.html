2026-06-20 04:59:40 EDT

Paraguay's Almiron Receives First Red Card for New World Cup Rule

2026-06-20 00:45:25 EDT

Paraguay's Miguel Almiron made an unusual mark in World Cup history by becoming the first player to be shown a red card for the specific offense of covering his mouth while speaking to an opponent. This incident unfolded during Paraguay's group match against Turkey, where Almiron's gesture of obscuring his mouth with his hand was immediately noted by an official, who then informed the referee. Following a review by the video assistant referee (VAR), the red card was issued, enforcing a new rule introduced by the International Football Association Board (Ifab) to curb unsporting conduct and potential abusive language.
