The Department of Health and Social Care estimates that chickenpox causes approximately £24 million in lost income and productivity annually in the UK, and the vaccine will dramatically reduce the number of chickenpox cases overall. Scotland and Northern Ireland are also expected to offer the vaccine on the NHS, though a date has not been set yet.
