Communication Expert Identifies Five Common Phrases That May Inadvertently Sound Judgmental

2025-07-06 09:00:01 EDT

A communication expert and executive coach highlights five common phrases that, despite good intentions, can make a person sound judgmental and erode trust. Phrases such as "Why don't you just..." and "Actually..." can come across as dismissive or condescending, implying the other person is wrong or illogical. Similarly, using preambles like "To be honest..." can suggest prior dishonesty and put others on the defensive.
