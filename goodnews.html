New Daily Pill Shows Promise in Sustaining Weight Loss Post-Obesity Injections

2026-05-12 19:06:23 EDT

A new daily pill, orforglipron, has shown promising results in preventing weight regain after patients stop using GLP-1 obesity injections. A study published in Nature Medicine, funded by manufacturer Eli Lilly, found that participants who took the tablet daily for a year maintained over 70% of their prior weight loss, compared to 38-50% in the placebo group. The drug, already available in the US and potentially launching in the UK, mimics a natural hormone to reduce appetite. While more research is needed on the optimal treatment duration, potentially lifelong, its oral administration could be more appealing than self-injections for many individuals seeking sustained weight management.
