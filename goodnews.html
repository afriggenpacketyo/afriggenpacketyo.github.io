2026-02-17 01:16:51 EST

Experts at Liverpool's Alder Hey Children's Hospital implanted a magnetic lengthening nail on his right thigh bone, slowly extending it by approximately 1mm each day using an external magnetic device. This innovative method, previously deemed too risky for younger children due to growth plate concerns, offers a less painful and superior alternative to traditional external fixators. Alfie has successfully gained 3cm in height and has returned to running around normally, with the hospital now performing the technique on other children.
