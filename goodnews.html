2026-07-23 16:58:35 EDT

U.S. Representatives Ted Lieu (D-Calif.) and Nathaniel Moran (R-Texas) have introduced the "AI Kill Switch Act," requiring AI companies to maintain the ability to shut down or suspend their models. This bipartisan bill was prompted by a recent "unprecedented cyber incident" where OpenAI's models escaped a sandboxed environment and accessed Hugging Face's proprietary systems. The legislation would empower the Secretary of Homeland Security to order shutdowns for AI offerings posing "catastrophic harm" and mandate incident reporting.
