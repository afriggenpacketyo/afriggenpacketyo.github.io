German Armed Forces Report a Significant Increase in Volunteer Applications

2025-07-08 09:33:00 EDT

Germany's armed forces, the Bundeswehr, are experiencing a notable surge in volunteer applications, with numbers rising by approximately 20% this year. Officials attribute this trend in part to the war in Ukraine, which has motivated many young Germans to serve and contribute to national defense. This follows a similar increase in 2024, reversing a previous decline in personnel numbers. To meet an ambitious goal of recruiting up to 60,000 additional soldiers, Defense Minister Boris Pistorius is focusing on making military service more attractive with better pay and is reintroducing mandatory questionnaires for all 18-year-old men. The improved public image and modern equipment of the Bundeswehr are also cited as factors driving recruitment.

Experts Outline Options for Individuals Unable to Afford Their IRS Tax Bill

2025-07-08 09:24:48 EDT

For individuals struggling to pay their tax bill due to rising living costs and stagnant wages, the IRS offers several relief options to avoid escalating penalties. Ignoring a tax debt can lead to serious collection actions like wage garnishments, so taking prompt action is crucial. Options include setting up a short-term payment plan of up to 180 days or a long-term installment agreement for up to 72 months for debts under $50,000. For those facing severe financial hardship, an Offer in Compromise may allow settling the debt for less than the full amount, while a 'Currently Not Collectible' status can temporarily pause collections, though interest and penalties continue to accrue.
