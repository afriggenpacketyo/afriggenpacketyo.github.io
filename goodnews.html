RMDs are mandatory annual withdrawals from accounts such as traditional IRAs and 401(k)s, generally commencing at age 73, with the amount determined by the account balance and IRS life expectancy factors. For instance, a $400,000 account would require withdrawals of about $15,094 at age 73, increasing with age.
