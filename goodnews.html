California Startup Develops Technology to Clean Water and Remove CO2 at Reduced Cost

2025-06-04 13:59:55 EDT

According to CNBC, Capture6, a startup based in Berkeley, California, has developed a technology that cleans water and removes carbon dioxide from the atmosphere while reducing costs. The technology repurposes industrial and water treatment waste, generating clean water and capturing CO2 from the atmosphere. Capture6's process involves extracting fresh water and separating salt into sodium and chlorine from brine waste. The company's CEO says that the sodium is then turned into lye, which bonds with CO2 from the air, capturing it and improving the environment, all while operating on renewable energy, thus allowing the company to sell carbon credits. This approach is particularly attractive in areas facing water scarcity and environmental challenges.
