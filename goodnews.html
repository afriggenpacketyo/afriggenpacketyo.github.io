Comparing Annuities and CDs for Retirement Planning

2025-08-06 12:51:59 EDT

When planning for retirement, both certificates of deposit (CDs) and annuities offer low-risk options, but they serve different purposes. CDs are simple, safe, and FDIC-insured, making them ideal for short-term goals and capital preservation, though their returns often fail to outpace inflation. Annuities, however, are specifically designed to provide a guaranteed income stream for life, addressing the risk of outliving one's savings, a primary concern for many retirees. While more complex and less liquid, annuities can offer higher periodic payments by pooling longevity risk. The best choice depends on an individual's specific needs. (not investment advice)
