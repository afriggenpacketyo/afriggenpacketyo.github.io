2026-03-04 02:05:45 EST

2026-03-04 02:02:00 EST

A vast plume of Saharan dust is drifting over the UK, promising spectacular fiery sunrises and sunsets, particularly on Thursday and Friday in England and Wales. This phenomenon, where fine sand and mineral particles travel thousands of miles from North Africa on warm southerly air currents, will create deep gold, amber, and burnt orange skies. However, the dust mixing with rain will also result in "blood rain," leaving thin, gritty, reddish-brown deposits on surfaces like cars and windows.
