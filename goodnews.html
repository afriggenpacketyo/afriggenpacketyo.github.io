2026-07-16 19:30:06 EDT

Chinese scientists have discovered that early-stage human reproductive cells face significant challenges in growth and development within a space environment, primarily due to microgravity and cosmic radiation. Despite these negative implications for long-term human reproduction in space, the study marks a world-first: the successful differentiation of human embryonic stem cells into germ cells in space.
