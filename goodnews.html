Annuities vs. CDs: A Retirement Planning Comparison

2025-08-06 12:51:59 EDT

The article compares annuities and Certificates of Deposit (CDs) as low-risk options for retirement planning, emphasizing that neither is universally "better" but rather suited to different needs. CDs offer simplicity, predictability, and FDIC insurance, ideal for short-term goals or as a conservative portfolio anchor, though their returns often struggle against inflation and they don't provide lifetime income. Annuities, conversely, are specifically designed for retirement income, offering guaranteed lifetime payments and addressing longevity risk, but they are more complex with varying fees and limited liquidity. The choice depends on individual priorities, such as maximizing safety and flexibility with CDs versus prioritizing guaranteed lifetime income with annuities, with many retirees finding a combination of both offers the best approach.
