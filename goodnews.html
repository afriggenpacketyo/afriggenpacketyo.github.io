China Explores Scissor-Wing Design for Hypersonic Drone Carrier

2025-08-16 22:00:14 EDT

A proposed Chinese aircraft design for a hypersonic drone carrier revives NASA's 'scissor wing' concept, featuring a single, centrally mounted wing that rotates up to 90 degrees. This innovative design aims to optimize performance across different speeds, from subsonic to hypersonic. At low speeds, the wing is perpendicular to the fuselage for maximum lift, pivots to 45 degrees at transonic speeds to reduce drag, and aligns parallel to the body at Mach 5, turning the entire aircraft into an efficient 'waverider'. This adaptability could overcome traditional aerodynamic compromises in aircraft design.
