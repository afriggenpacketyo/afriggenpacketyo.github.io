2025-07-03 17:56:06 EDT

The object was spotted using a telescope in Chile and is currently about 420 million miles from Earth, posing no threat. Traveling at 37 miles per second, the comet will make its closest approach to the sun in late October, passing between the orbits of Mars and Earth.
