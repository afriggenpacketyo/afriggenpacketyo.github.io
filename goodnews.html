Chinese Researchers Propose Hypersonic Drone Carrier with Novel Pivoting 'Scissor Wing'

2025-08-16 22:00:14 EDT

Chinese scientists have designed a hypersonic drone carrier concept featuring a unique, single rotating wing, reviving a design once explored by NASA. The "scissor wing" sits perpendicular to the fuselage for efficient lift during takeoff and subsonic flight. As the aircraft accelerates, the wing pivots, swinging to a 45-degree angle to mitigate shock waves at transonic speeds and eventually aligning parallel with the fuselage at Mach 5. In this final configuration, the entire vehicle functions as a "waverider," generating high pressure for lift in hypersonic flight.
