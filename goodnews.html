The ruling reverses the 5th Circuit Court of Appeals' decision. The current ruling enables Interim Storage Partners to store nuclear waste in Andrews County, Texas. Optimism regarding the long-term solution to nuclear waste storage remains tempered by the ongoing political and logistical challenges.
