FedEx Founder Fred Smith Dies at 80

2025-06-22 09:13:25 EDT

Fred Smith, the founder of FedEx Corp., has passed away at the age of 80. Smith revolutionized the express delivery industry, starting FedEx in 1973 to deliver small parcels and documents more quickly than the postal service. Over the next half-century, the Marine Corps veteran oversaw the growth of FedEx into a global transportation and logistics company. Smith stepped down as CEO in 2022 but remained executive chairman, leaving behind a legacy of innovation and economic impact.
