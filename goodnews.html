Weighing the Pros and Cons of Using a HELOC to Pay Off Your Mortgage

2025-06-05 16:28:03 EDT

Homeowners might consider using a home equity line of credit (HELOC) to pay off their mortgage faster, but this strategy has both advantages and disadvantages. HELOCs offer flexibility, controlled costs, and competitive rates, with minimal closing costs and interest-only payments during the draw period. However, variable interest rates, potential prepayment penalties, and the risk of foreclosure are drawbacks to consider. Alternatives to using a HELOC include refinancing, home equity loans, mortgage recasts, extra mortgage payments, and reverse mortgages, each with their own set of implications.
