2025-10-22 22:28:42 EDT

The Westminster City Council previously approved a comprehensive redevelopment plan in 2022 to transform the 100-acre site into a mixed-use complex, which will include 3,000 housing units and 600,000 square feet of upscale retail space. Community members have gathered for nostalgic farewell events, reflecting on the mall's past as a vibrant social center. While many stores, including JCPenney, are closing, Best Buy and Target are anticipated to remain open for several years as the extensive property undergoes its phased redevelopment.
