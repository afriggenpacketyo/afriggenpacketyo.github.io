Financial Advisor Highlights Qualified Charitable Distributions as a Key Tax Strategy for Retirees

2025-10-08 08:15:01 EDT

For retirees looking to make charitable donations, financial advisors point to qualified charitable distributions (QCDs) as a highly effective tax-saving strategy. This move allows individuals aged 70½ or older to transfer up to $108,000 directly from their pretax IRA to an eligible charity in 2025. While there is no deduction, the distributed amount is excluded from the taxpayer's adjusted gross income (AGI). This can prevent an increase in Medicare premiums and also satisfy the annual required minimum distribution (RMD) obligation without adding to taxable income, making it more beneficial than a standard deduction for most retirees.
