March Madness Tournaments to Expand to 76 Teams Each, Including Alcohol Sponsorships

2026-05-08 01:31:58 EDT

This expansion is partly enabled by allowing alcohol sponsorships, a previously restricted category. The 'First Four' will become the 'March Madness Opening Round,' featuring 12 teams advancing to the main 64-team bracket. This expansion is expected to generate significant additional revenue, with a substantial portion distributed to participating schools. While some coaches express concerns about the financial motivations, the move is seen as a response to the evolving landscape of college sports.
