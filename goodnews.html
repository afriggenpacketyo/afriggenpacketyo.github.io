Innovative Artificial Reefs Aid Restoration of Arctic Kelp Forests and Marine Ecosystems

2026-01-20 05:31:00 EST

Norwegian researchers have achieved a significant breakthrough in restoring vital kelp forests in the Arctic, an ecosystem severely depleted over the past four decades by sea urchins and human activity. These kelp forests are crucial for supporting marine life, purifying water, absorbing carbon, and protecting coastlines.
