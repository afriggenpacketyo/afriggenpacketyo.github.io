Comparing Gold, CDs, and Stocks for Retirement Savings in a Volatile Economy

2026-03-18 16:00:05 EDT

Retirement planning in the current volatile economic climate requires carefully balancing growth and protection, prompting many to re-evaluate traditional investment strategies. Gold has surged past record highs as a defensive asset, offering protection against inflation and systemic risks, though it does not generate income. Certificates of Deposit (CDs) provide principal safety and predictable fixed returns, appealing to those prioritizing stability, but offer limited growth potential against long-term inflation. Stocks historically offer the strongest long-term growth but come with the highest short-term volatility.
