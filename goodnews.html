The new rules, set to be introduced in October, include a mandatory six-month waiting period for learner drivers before taking a practical test, along with restrictions on night-time driving and passenger numbers for newly qualified drivers. Infrastructure Minister Liz Kimmins emphasized that these changes specifically target the age group statistically most likely to be killed or seriously injured on the roads.
