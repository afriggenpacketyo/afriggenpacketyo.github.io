Chinese Team Develops and Releases World's First Free Bamboo Drone Flight Control Software

2026-04-04 02:00:10 EDT

A Chinese research team has developed and released the world's first free flight control software specifically designed for bamboo drones, addressing unique challenges posed by their unconventional airframes. Unlike composite materials, bamboo structures introduce low-frequency vibrations (8-20 hertz) that standard flight controllers struggle to manage. The team redesigned control algorithms, utilizing an extended Kalman filter and bamboo's natural vibration-damping properties, to reduce control latency from 15-20 milliseconds to 8-10 milliseconds. This innovation significantly improves responsiveness and stability, aiming to overcome limitations of existing commercial and open-source controllers and facilitate the industrialization of bamboo-based unmanned aerial vehicles.
