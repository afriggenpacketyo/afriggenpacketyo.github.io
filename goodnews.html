UK Government to Legally Extend Bereavement Leave for Early-Stage Miscarriages

2025-07-07 06:30:26 EDT

The UK government plans to change the law to grant parents who experience a miscarriage at any stage of pregnancy the legal right to bereavement leave. Currently, this leave is only available for pregnancy loss after 24 weeks, but the amendment to the Employment Rights Bill will extend this protection. Deputy Prime Minister Angela Rayner stated the change will give people necessary time to grieve without having to use sick leave.
