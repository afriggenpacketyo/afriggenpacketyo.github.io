2026-06-11 05:00:00 EDT

The word 'algorithm,' now often associated with complex digital systems and potential dangers, originates from the Latinized name of the 9th-century Persian mathematician Muhammad ibn Musa al-Khwarizmi. His influential book on calculation introduced algorithmic methods and popularized Hindu-Arabic numerals in the West, laying groundwork for algebra. The term evolved through algorismus and algorisme before reaching its modern form.
