Understanding Annuities: Can They Run Out of Money?

2025-05-22 14:49:21 EDT

The possibility of an annuity running out of money depends on the type of annuity. Immediate and lifetime annuities are designed to provide a steady income stream for life, while fixed-term annuities pay out for a set number of years. Variable or indexed annuities can be tied to investment performance, potentially depleting the cash value. Strategies to safeguard annuity payments include choosing a lifetime income annuity, adding an income rider, diversifying retirement income, researching the insurance company, and monitoring withdrawal habits.
