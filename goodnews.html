NASA Discovers Third Known Interstellar Object, a Comet, Passing Through Solar System

2025-07-03 17:56:06 EDT

NASA has identified a new interstellar comet, named 3I/ATLAS, making it the third known object from another star system to visit our solar system. The comet was discovered using a telescope in Chile and is currently located near Jupiter's orbit, traveling at 37 miles per second. It poses no threat to Earth and will make its closest approach to the sun in late October, passing between the orbits of Mars and Earth. Astronomers are now monitoring the object to determine its size, shape, and composition, providing a rare opportunity to study material from outside our solar system.
