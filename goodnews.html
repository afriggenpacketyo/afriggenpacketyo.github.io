Scientists Trace Evolutionary Origins of Kissing Back 21 Million Years

2025-11-18 19:08:59 EST

The study, published in Evolution and Human Behaviour, also concluded that Neanderthals likely engaged in kissing, potentially even with modern humans, given shared oral microbes. While the exact evolutionary purpose remains a puzzle, this article highlights the deep historical roots of this intimate behavior.
