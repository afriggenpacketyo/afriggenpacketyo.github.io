Historic Swedish Church Begins 5km Relocation Due to Mining Expansion

Sweden's iconic 113-year-old Kiruna Church has embarked on a monumental 5-kilometer relocation to a new city center, a necessity driven by ground subsidence from over a century of iron ore mining.
