Letters of Credit Help Chinese Exporters Focus on Domestic Market Amid Trade War

2025-06-13 02:00:17 EDT

In response to the United States imposing reciprocal tariffs on imports from China, Beijing has taken measures to help Chinese exporters shift their focus to the domestic market. One of these measures is encouraging the increased use of letters of credit (L/Cs) to ensure payment security and enhance credit for businesses. Domestic L/Cs function similarly to international L/Cs, guaranteeing payment to the seller, but can also be used in the trade of services, unlike international L/Cs which are limited to physical goods. This strategy aims to mitigate the impact of trade tensions and support Chinese businesses.
