2026-04-02 17:51:57 EDT

This pivotal maneuver will propel the spacecraft to approximately 25,000 mph, enabling it to escape Earth's gravitational pull for a four-day trek around the lunar far side. The mission is a vital step in validating procedures for upcoming moon landing missions, signifying a significant advancement in human deep space exploration.

CFTC Chairman Michael S. These lawsuits emerge amid increasing scrutiny of prediction markets, with some Congressional Democrats recently proposing legislation to ban certain types of bets.
