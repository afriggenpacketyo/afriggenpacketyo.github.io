Australia to Include YouTube in Social Media Ban for Individuals Under 16

2025-07-29 21:24:49 EDT

Australia's government has reversed its previous decision and will now include YouTube in its landmark social media ban for children under 16. The law, set to begin in December, will prevent teenagers from having accounts, which are needed for uploading content or interacting, though viewing videos will still be permitted. The decision follows a recommendation from the eSafety Commissioner, who noted that YouTube was the most cited platform where children saw harmful content. Tech companies face fines up to A$50 million for non-compliance with the age restrictions.
