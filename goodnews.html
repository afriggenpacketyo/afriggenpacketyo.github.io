Finally, applicants must be willing to commit to the program's rules, which often includes closing credit accounts and participating in financial counseling.

Independent undergraduates have higher limits, up to $12,500 annually and a $57,500 aggregate cap. Graduate students can borrow up to $20,500 per year.
