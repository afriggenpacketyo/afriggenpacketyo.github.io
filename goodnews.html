2025-07-03 22:00:13 EDT

A research team in China has achieved a major scientific breakthrough by cultivating a human-cell-based heart structure within a pig embryo. The structure, developed by scientists at the Guangzhou Institutes of Biomedicine and Health, reportedly continued to beat unaided for 21 days. To achieve this, the team reprogrammed human stem cells to improve their survival rate before implanting them into pig embryos at an early stage.
