Mental health experts are highlighting the unique challenges of diagnosing and treating depression in older adults, who may be more stigmatized or misdiagnosed than younger generations. While symptoms like sadness and loss of interest are similar across age groups, depression in older adults often presents with more physical symptoms like fatigue, which can be mistakenly attributed to normal aging.
