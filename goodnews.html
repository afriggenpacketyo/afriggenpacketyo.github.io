China Develops Innovative Driverless Mining Truck with Advanced Maneuverability

2026-05-05 00:00:06 EDT

Developed by Shuanglin Group and Tsinghua University, this advanced vehicle can move sideways and pivot on the spot, enabling operation in narrow pits and sloped roadways. The K7 is the world's first driverless mining truck to utilize a distributed electric drive-by-wire corner module technology.
