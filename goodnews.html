Ford Recalls 119,000 Vehicles Due to Engine Block Heater Fire Risk

2026-01-21 17:36:20 EST

Ford is recalling approximately 119,000 vehicles due to a defect in their engine block heaters that poses an increased risk of fire. The recall affects certain 2016-2018 Focus, 2019 Explorer, 2024 Explorer, 2013-2018 Focus, 2013-2019 Escape, and 2015-2016 MKC models equipped with 2.0L engines. The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) states that the heaters may crack and leak coolant, leading to a short circuit and potential under-hood fires when plugged in. Owners are advised to stop using the heaters immediately and can receive a free replacement or removal service from Ford dealers.
