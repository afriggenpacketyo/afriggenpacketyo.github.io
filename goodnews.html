Scientific Study Reveals Mars Possesses a Solid Inner Core Similar to Earth's

2025-09-07 22:00:09 EDT

A joint China-US research team has discovered that Mars has a solid inner core, a finding that challenges previous models of the planet's formation and thermal history. By analyzing seismic data from marsquakes recorded by NASA's InSight lander, scientists determined the solid core has a radius of about 600km. Previously, it was thought that Mars had not cooled fast enough to "freeze" its liquid metal center into a solid state. This discovery places Mars alongside Earth and the Moon as the only bodies in the solar system currently known to have a solid inner core.
