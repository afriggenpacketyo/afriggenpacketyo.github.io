Understanding Gold IRAs: Mechanics, Rules, and Investor Suitability

2026-03-05 17:04:20 EST

A Gold IRA is a self-directed retirement account allowing investors to hold IRS-approved physical gold and other precious metals, offering diversification beyond traditional stocks and bonds. It operates through specialized custodians, with funding via rollovers, transfers, or contributions, but requires physical metals to be stored in IRS-approved depositories, incurring ongoing fees. While beneficial for investors seeking diversification, a hedge against economic turbulence, or concerned about geopolitical instability, it may not suit early-career investors or those sensitive to fees due to its price-driven returns and higher costs.
