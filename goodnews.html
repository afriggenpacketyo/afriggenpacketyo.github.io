2026-03-21 05:10:00 EDT

2026-03-21 04:57:17 EDT

This natural phenomenon, typically seen closer to the Arctic Circle, was visible as far south as Norfolk due to a powerful geomagnetic storm caused by a coronal mass ejection from the Sun. Forecasters anticipate similar activity, potentially allowing views across Scotland, Northern Ireland, and northern England and Wales, with a small chance for further south. While clouds might obscure parts of the view, clear skies are expected in other areas, offering another opportunity to witness this rare display.
