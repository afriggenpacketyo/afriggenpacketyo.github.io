Understanding Phantom Energy: The Hidden Drain on Home Electricity and How to Combat It

2025-09-03 11:04:07 EDT

Phantom energy, also known as vampire energy, refers to electricity silently consumed by devices left plugged in but not actively in use, accounting for 5% to 10% of household energy. This wasted power contributes to climate change by increasing demand for emissions-producing electricity sources. Experts recommend simple actions like unplugging chargers and unused appliances, or disabling smart features on devices like internet-connected TVs, to reduce this drain. Such individual efforts, when multiplied across the country, can significantly decrease U.S. carbon emissions and save money on utility bills.
