Medigap, which supplements Original Medicare, offers broader access to any doctor or specialist accepting Medicare nationwide, without restrictive networks or referral requirements. It also provides more predictable out-of-pocket costs, covering deductibles and copayments, which is crucial for managing chronic conditions. Furthermore, Medigap plans offer stable benefits year after year, unlike Medicare Advantage plans which can change annually.
