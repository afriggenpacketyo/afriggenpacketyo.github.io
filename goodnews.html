Gold IRA storage requirements include approved depositories, insurance, and audits

2026-03-12 14:39:37 EDT

Investors considering gold IRAs must adhere to strict IRS rules for storing physical precious metals. Gold must be held in IRS-approved depositories, which offer secure vaults with advanced security measures and 24-hour monitoring. Investors can choose between segregated storage, where their specific metals are kept separate, or non-segregated storage, where metals are commingled. Reputable depositories maintain comprehensive insurance policies to protect stored metals against theft or damage. Independent third-party audits are also conducted to verify the existence of the metals against custody records, ensuring transparency and accountability for investors.
