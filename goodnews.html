Australia and European Union Finalize Comprehensive Free Trade Deal

2026-03-23 21:28:00 EDT

Australia and the European Union have successfully concluded a new free trade agreement after eight years of negotiations, announced by EU chief Ursula von der Leyen and Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese. Key concessions were made on access to the EU market for Australian beef and sheep meat, and Australia will be allowed to continue using some protected geographical names for products like feta for a transitional period.
