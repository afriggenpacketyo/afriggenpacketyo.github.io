Understanding Gold IRAs: A Guide to Including Physical Gold in Retirement Plans

2026-03-05 17:04:20 EST

With gold prices soaring, many retirement savers are considering Gold Individual Retirement Accounts (IRAs) as a diversification strategy. This article explains that a gold IRA is a self-directed account allowing physical gold and other precious metals, held by an IRS-approved custodian in a depository, not at home. While offering tax advantages similar to traditional IRAs, they incur higher fees due to custodianship and storage. Gold IRAs are best suited for investors seeking diversification and a hedge against economic turbulence, particularly those already maximizing conventional retirement contributions, rather than for aggressive growth.
