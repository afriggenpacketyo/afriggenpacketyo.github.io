China Team Releases First Bamboo Drone Flight Control Software for Free

2026-04-04 02:00:10 EDT

A Chinese research team has developed and released the world's first flight control software specifically designed for bamboo drones, making it freely available. Conventional flight controllers struggle with the low-frequency vibrations inherent in bamboo airframes, typically in the 8-20 hertz range. The team redesigned control algorithms to match bamboo's structural properties, reducing control latency from 15-20 milliseconds to 8-10 milliseconds while maintaining stability. This innovation aims to overcome limitations of existing commercial and open-source controllers, potentially fostering the industrialization of bamboo-based unmanned aerial vehicles by adapting to local development needs.
