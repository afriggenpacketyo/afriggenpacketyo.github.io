Ticketless Entry to Football Matches in England and Wales Now a Criminal Offense

2026-03-19 20:01:16 EDT

Entering a football match in England and Wales without a valid ticket has officially become a criminal offense under new legislation enacted before Sunday's Carabao Cup final. This measure specifically targets "tailgating" and the use of fraudulent entry documents, practices that led to severe disorder at the Euro 2020 final at Wembley in 2021. Individuals caught violating the new law face a potential football banning order for up to five years and a fine of up to £1,000. Policing minister Sarah Jones stated the legislation provides police with necessary tools to ensure fan safety and prevent a recurrence of past chaos, reinforcing stadium security.

2026-03-19 18:32:45 EDT
