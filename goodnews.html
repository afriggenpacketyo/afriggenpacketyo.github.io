Hardy went on to serve with distinction in the Korean and Vietnam Wars, retiring in 1971. The Tuskegee Airmen were the first Black military pilots in the U.S. Armed Forces, and Hardy became a champion of their legacy, helping accept the Congressional Gold Medal on the group's behalf in 2007 for their patriotism in the face of discrimination.
