Young Chinese Embrace Early Retirement by Purchasing Affordable Homes Amid Economic Slowdown

2026-03-01 22:06:33 EST

This trend, echoing the Western FIRE movement, is a stark departure from previous generations' pursuit of upward mobility in megacities, driven by disillusionment with the "996" work schedule and high youth unemployment.
