2025-07-10 21:11:51 EDT

Weighing over 54 pounds, the rock represents about 6.5% of all known Martian material on our planet. It was likely ejected from Mars by an asteroid impact and traveled 140 million miles before landing in the Sahara Desert, where it was discovered in 2023. The auction house highlights the meteorite's rarity and its scientific value for understanding the red planet.
