2026-06-05 22:42:04 EDT

Scientists at the SETI Institute utilized a radio telescope to search for signs of extraterrestrial technology on Comet 3I/ATLAS, an interstellar object that recently traversed our solar system. This cosmic visitor, the third known object from another star system, had sparked widespread speculation about potential alien spacecraft. After seven hours of scanning a broad radio spectrum, the team identified nearly 74 million narrowband signals, but all were ultimately traced back to terrestrial technology or orbiting satellites.
