Debt resolution, or debt settlement, can offer significant relief for individuals overwhelmed by debt, but its consequences can remain on a credit report for up to seven years. The process involves stopping payments to creditors while funds for a settlement are accumulated, leading to delinquency reports. Once an account is settled for less than the full amount, it is marked as such, impacting credit scores for years. However, for those already facing financial hardship and falling behind on payments, debt resolution can be a faster path to financial recovery compared to decades of interest payments, despite the temporary credit damage.
