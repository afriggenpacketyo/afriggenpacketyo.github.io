Twin Spacecraft Offer New Perspectives on the Sun's Corona

2025-06-16 11:25:00 EDT

The European Space Agency's Proba-3 mission has achieved a new milestone by using two spacecraft to create an artificial solar eclipse, providing unprecedented views of the sun's corona. Consisting of the Occulter and Coronagraph spacecraft, the mission blocks out the sun's light, allowing scientists to observe the corona from a close proximity. This mission aims to address the mystery of the solar corona's high temperature compared to the sun's surface, and it also tests new space technology with autonomous precision flying. Combined with data from NASA's Parker Solar Probe and the Solar Orbiter, astronomers are gaining new insights into solar weather and the sun's magnetic field.
