2026-03-31 05:47:58 EDT

The Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) has published rules anticipating average payouts of £829 per mis-sold agreement, with the total cost potentially reaching £9.1 billion. The mis-selling often involved "discretionary commission arrangements" where dealers earned more by charging higher interest rates, a practice banned by the FCA in 2021.
