China's Jiuzhang 4.0 Quantum Computer Demonstrates 'Quantum Supremacy'

2026-05-14 21:00:08 EDT

China's Jiuzhang 4.0 quantum computer has achieved a significant scientific breakthrough, successfully completing a Gaussian boson sampling task in an astonishing 25 microseconds. This feat, which researchers estimate would require the world's most powerful classical supercomputer, El Capitan, over 10^42 years to accomplish, demonstrates a concept known as "quantum supremacy." Gaussian boson sampling is a specialized quantum computing task specifically designed to be computationally intractable for conventional machines.
