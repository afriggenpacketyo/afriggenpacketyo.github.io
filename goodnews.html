Understanding VA Debt Relief Programs for Veterans

2026-03-03 14:32:39 EST

This article clarifies that the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) does not offer a broad debt relief program for general consumer debts like credit cards or personal loans. However, it provides specific relief options for debts owed directly to the VA, such as benefit overpayments, allowing for repayment plans, compromise offers, or debt waivers. Veterans often face higher credit card debt than civilians and irregular incomes, making external debt relief options crucial. The article outlines other available strategies like Servicemembers Civil Relief Act protections, military-focused credit counseling, debt settlement, and bankruptcy.
