Researchers hypothesize that this mechanism is vital for flushing out protein waste and other unnecessary material from the brain by moving cerebrospinal fluid. The findings clarify the long-observed cerebral movement and open significant avenues for future research into how physical conditions like obesity might affect this belly-brain choreography and overall brain health.
