These include bank account levies, placing liens on property, and accounts receivable garnishment, where third parties owing money to the self-employed individual can be compelled to pay the creditor directly. The article advises prompt action, suggesting negotiation, repayment plans, challenging judgments, or bankruptcy, to effectively manage debt and prevent further financial complications.
