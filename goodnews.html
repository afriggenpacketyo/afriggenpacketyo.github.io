2026-01-09 23:00:20 EST

This significant scientific breakthrough, detailed in the peer-reviewed journal Cell, addresses long-standing ethical constraints and limited access to human embryos that previously impeded comprehensive understanding of this crucial stage. The innovative 3D in-chip model utilizes human blastoids or blastocysts co-cultured with bioengineered human endometrial tissue.
