Doctors Advise Against Consuming Grapefruit with Certain Common Medications

2025-07-13 13:50:00 EDT

Medical experts are warning that grapefruit and grapefruit juice can have dangerous interactions with a number of common medications, including statins, certain blood pressure drugs, and immunosuppressants. Compounds in grapefruit called furanocoumarins can block a key enzyme in the liver and intestines responsible for metabolizing these drugs. This can lead to the medication building up to toxic levels in the body, potentially causing serious side effects like muscle breakdown or irregular heart rhythms. Patients are advised to consult their doctor or pharmacist about potential interactions before consuming grapefruit.
