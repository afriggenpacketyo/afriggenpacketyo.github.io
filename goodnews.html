China Advances Space-Based Solar Power as Next Frontier for Green Energy

2026-03-27 17:30:08 EDT

China is actively pursuing space-based solar power, a concept utilizing orbital satellites equipped with enormous solar panels to collect continuous solar energy. This energy is then converted into microwaves or laser beams and wirelessly transmitted to ground-based rectennas, feeding electricity into the grid. The system offers significant advantages over terrestrial solar, operating uninterrupted by weather, seasons, or nighttime. Historically considered economically unviable, recent breakthroughs in robotics, wireless power transmission, and reduced launch expenses are now bridging the gap between concept and reality, positioning it as a key green energy frontier.
