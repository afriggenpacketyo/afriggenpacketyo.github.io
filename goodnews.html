2025-07-06 02:00:16 EDT

The study, published in the Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences, established a link from the genetic level to brain function across various scales, from specific regions to large networks. These findings identify brain criticality as a biological phenotype, creating new avenues for research into brain function and potential neurological or mental dysfunctions.

UK Government to Expand 'Best Start' Family Hubs to Every Council in England

2025-07-05 23:36:26 EDT

The UK government has announced a £500 million plan to establish "Best Start" family hubs in every local authority across England by April 2026. These hubs are designed to provide a wide range of support for families, from midwifery and birth registration to debt advice and youth services, aiming to help 500,000 more children in disadvantaged areas. The initiative builds on the concept of the previous "Sure Start" centres, many of which were closed due to funding cuts after 2010.
