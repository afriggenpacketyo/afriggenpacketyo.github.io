Longtime Congressman Charles Rangel Dies at 94

2025-05-26 17:59:23 EDT

Charles Rangel, a longtime U.S. congressman from New York City, has died at the age of 94. He was known as a champion for justice, equity, and opportunity, fighting for affordable housing, urban revitalization, fair tax policies, and equal opportunities for all Americans.
