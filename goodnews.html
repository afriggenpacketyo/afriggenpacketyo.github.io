Homeowners Can Hold Both a HELOC and a Home Equity Loan Simultaneously

2025-12-09 15:52:48 EST

Lenders typically cap the combined loan-to-value ratio at 80-90% and assess a borrower's credit and income to ensure they can manage both payments. This strategy can balance the predictability of a fixed-rate loan with the flexibility of a revolving credit line. Borrowers often use the loan for a large, one-time expense while keeping the HELOC for ongoing or unexpected needs.
