UK Driving Test Booking System Overhauled to Combat Long Waits and Reselling

2026-05-11 19:08:21 EDT

The UK's driving test booking system is undergoing significant changes, effective May 12, to reduce extensive waiting lists and prevent the bulk-buying and reselling of slots. Under the new rules, only learner drivers can book, change, or swap their own tests, eliminating the ability of instructors or third parties to do so. This reform addresses issues like touts selling test slots at inflated prices, as revealed by a BBC investigation. Learners will need their instructor's reference number to book, ensuring availability. Additionally, from June 9, test date movements will be restricted to the three closest test centers, streamlining the process and limiting speculative bookings.
