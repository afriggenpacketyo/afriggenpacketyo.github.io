Rare US Declaration of Independence Copy Discovered in UK National Archives

A rare surviving copy of the U.S. Declaration of Independence, the only known example of its kind outside the U.S., has been discovered at The National Archives in Kew, UK. A volunteer uncovered the document in February while cataloging papers from Royal Navy captains from the American Revolutionary War. This copy is one of 11 printed in Exeter, New Hampshire, in July 1776 to disseminate news of independence throughout the colonies, and was seized by British forces on Christmas Eve 1776. This "extraordinary discovery" will be displayed in an exhibition, offering unique insight into the rapid spread and historical context of the Declaration.
