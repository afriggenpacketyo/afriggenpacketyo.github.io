However, the article clarifies that need-based programs like Supplemental Security Income (SSI) or Medicaid can be affected if large, unspent reverse mortgage proceeds exceed asset limits. Consulting a financial advisor is crucial for seniors relying on these supplementary benefits to structure payouts carefully and avoid unintended consequences.
