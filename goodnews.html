Expert Advice on Protecting Pets During Cold Weather Conditions

2026-01-04 00:26:07 EST

As cold and snowy weather affects many parts of the UK, pet owners are receiving essential guidance on ensuring their animals' safety and warmth. The RSPCA and PDSA advise providing extra blankets and elevated beds for indoor pets to guard against hypothermia, alongside encouraging increased indoor playtime and ensuring constant access to fresh drinking water. For dogs, shorter, more frequent walks are recommended, with suitable winter coats for short-haired breeds, and careful attention to rinsing paws. Smaller outdoor pets like rabbits, guinea pigs, and ferrets should ideally be moved inside to a cooler room or have their outdoor living quarters heavily insulated to protect them from the elements.
