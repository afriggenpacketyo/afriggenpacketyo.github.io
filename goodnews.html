Trump Expected to Reclassify Marijuana to Schedule III Drug

2025-12-17 17:13:23 EST

President Trump is expected to sign an executive order reclassifying marijuana from a Schedule I to a Schedule III drug, marking a significant shift in federal drug policy. Currently, Schedule I denotes substances with "no currently accepted medical use and a high potential for abuse," while Schedule III is for those with "moderate to low potential for physical and psychological dependence." This reclassification would not federally legalize recreational use but could significantly facilitate more research into marijuana, reduce its stigma, attract new investment, and ease tax burdens for state-licensed dispensaries. The move follows recommendations from the Biden-era Department of Health and Human Services and has bipartisan support.
