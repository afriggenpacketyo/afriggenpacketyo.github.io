While elite athletes benefit from them during intense, prolonged workouts in hot conditions, most people obtain sufficient electrolytes like sodium, potassium, and magnesium from a regular, well-balanced diet. For moderate activities like gym workouts or 5k runs, plain water is adequate, and expensive electrolyte products offer no proven performance gains.
