2025-10-31 06:30:00 EDT

In fact, modern urban populations, particularly in the U.S., consume far more meat than most pre-industrial societies did. The romanticization of a prehistoric, meat-centric lifestyle serves more as a political and cultural signifier today than an accurate reflection of the past.
