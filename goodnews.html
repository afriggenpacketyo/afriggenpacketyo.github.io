As the youngest Red Tail fighter pilot, he completed 21 missions in WWII, followed by 45 in Korea and 70 in Vietnam. Despite receiving numerous military honors, including the Distinguished Flying Cross, Hardy often cited his educational achievements as his proudest accomplishment. His death signifies the end of an era for these pioneering Black aviators who courageously challenged and overcame racial barriers within the U.S. Armed Forces.
