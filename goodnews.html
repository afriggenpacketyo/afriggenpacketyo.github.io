She advises consumers to prioritize ingredients over brand names, recommending lip oils with broad-spectrum SPF, linoleic acids, squalane, glycerin, and hyaluronic acid. Conversely, she warns against fragrances and oleic acids, emphasizing that safe lip oils can be beneficial for hydration and shine. Consulting a medical professional for severe symptoms is also recommended.
