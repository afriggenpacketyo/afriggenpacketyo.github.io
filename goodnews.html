Rare 1776 U.S. Declaration of Independence Copy Discovered in UK Archives

2026-07-02 19:01:12 EDT

A "vanishingly rare" 1776 Exeter printing of the U.S. Declaration of Independence has been discovered by Michael Scurr, a volunteer in the UK's National Archives, just weeks before its 250th anniversary. This copy, one of only 11 known to exist and the sole one found outside the U.S., was uncovered among Royal Navy correspondence seized from an American privateer vessel, the Dalton, off the coast of Spain in December 1776. The document's remarkable provenance offers a unique glimpse into how news of the declaration spread rapidly across the Atlantic, reaching ordinary citizens and sailors, including a free Black man identified in the privateer's crew. This significant transatlantic discovery underscores the global impact and historical context of America's founding document, providing a powerful reminder of the American Revolution's reach.
