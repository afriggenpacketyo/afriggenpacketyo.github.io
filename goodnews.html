This new regulation, enacted under the Ecodesign for Sustainable Products framework, now prohibits large companies with over 250 employees and substantial turnover from destroying their stock. Instead, these firms are mandated to find alternative sales channels, such as offering discounts, exploring alternative markets, or donating items to charity.
