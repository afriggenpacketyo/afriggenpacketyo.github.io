China Develops Jam-Proof Satellite Navigation Using Coded Light Signals

2026-03-29 22:01:05 EDT

Led by Professor Xing Fei, this innovative system involves deploying powerful light sources on satellites to transmit highly precise coded signals to Earth. Ground-based receivers then detect these light signals, using their direction and the satellites' known positions to calculate extremely accurate location data. This advanced optical approach renders interference significantly more challenging compared to radio signals, offering a robust and resilient alternative for guiding a wide array of vehicles and spacecraft in the future.
