Joint China-US Study Finds Mars Has a Solid Inner Core

2025-09-07 22:00:09 EDT

A collaborative study between Chinese and American scientists has revealed that Mars possesses a solid inner core, a feature previously unconfirmed. By analyzing seismic data from NASA's InSight lander, the research team determined the solid core has a radius of about 600km, proportionally similar to Earth's. This discovery is significant because most bodies in the solar system have not cooled sufficiently to form a solid inner core from their liquid metal centers. The finding places Mars alongside Earth and the Moon as one of the few celestial bodies known to have this feature, potentially reviving theories about Mars' geological history.
