China Develops World's First Zero-Emission Coal Fuel Cell Technology

2026-04-26 06:00:15 EDT

China has unveiled a groundbreaking direct coal fuel cell technology capable of generating electricity with zero carbon dioxide emissions, fundamentally challenging conventional perceptions of coal power. This innovative system bypasses combustion, instead using electrochemical oxidation of pulverized coal across an oxide membrane to produce electricity directly, without intermediate steam cycles or mechanical turbines. Crucially, the high-purity CO2 generated is captured in situ and catalytically converted into valuable chemical feedstocks or mineralized into compounds like sodium bicarbonate. This development represents a significant advancement in clean energy, potentially transforming coal's role and offering a viable path to decarbonization for heavy industries globally.
