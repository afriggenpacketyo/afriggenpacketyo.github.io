Germany Establishes National Security Council to Streamline Crisis Decision-Making

2025-08-16 03:34:00 EDT

Germany's coalition government has agreed to create a National Security Council (NSR) to centralize information and enable more decisive action during crises. The move, ending decades of debate, is a response to the need for faster decision-making, highlighted by events like the withdrawal from Afghanistan and Russia's invasion of Ukraine. China Successfully Tests Engines for Long March-10 Rocket in Lunar Mission Milestone

2025-08-16 02:00:20 EDT

China's space agency has successfully completed a key ignition test of the engines for its new-generation Long March-10 rocket, a critical step toward its goal of landing astronauts on the moon by 2030. The test confirmed that the 21 YF-100K engines, which will power the rocket's first stage and two boosters, can operate in sync under various conditions. The superheavy Long March-10 is designed to deliver a 27-tonne payload into a trans-lunar orbit. China's lunar mission plan involves two separate launches of this rocket to send the Mengzhou crewed spacecraft and the Lanyue lander into orbit for a rendezvous.
