Experts suggest HELOCs are ideal for short-term needs and homeowners with stable income and significant equity, provided they can repay quickly. They offer lower borrowing costs and potential tax deductibility compared to personal loans. However, HELOCs carry risks, especially with variable rates and the potential to shift unsecured debt onto a home.
