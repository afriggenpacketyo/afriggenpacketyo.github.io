Inheritances and gifts are typically considered separate property, but they can become marital assets if they are commingled with joint finances. To ensure the funds remain separate, it is advisable to deposit the money into a brokerage account held solely in the recipient's name, rather than adding it to joint accounts or even some retirement funds.
