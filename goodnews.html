The presence of these materials, particularly the quartz which likely acted as gastroliths or "stomach stones" to help grind tough food, strongly suggests that this species was herbivorous. This finding provides new insight into the diverse ecosystems of the dinosaur era and the dietary habits of the creatures that once ruled the skies.
