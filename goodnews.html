By analyzing the stylistic traits of the remaining 250 characters, the team used an artificial intelligence model to replicate the artist's unique style and generate the missing verses. The project resulted in a full scroll of 919 characters, showcasing how modern AI can be fused with cultural preservation to revive ancient artistic treasures.
