Chinese Scientists Develop Hair-Thin Fiber Integrated Circuit for Advanced Electronic Textiles

2026-01-23 17:00:11 EST

Chinese scientists have achieved a significant breakthrough by developing a novel fiber integrated circuit (FIC), shrinking a semiconductor chip into a threadlike form as thin as a human hair. This innovation enables fibers to perform complex computations and display information, paving the way for machine-washable smart textiles that function like computers or televisions. The team fabricated functional circuits on an elastic substrate, then rolled it into a fiber, achieving a transistor density of 100,000 per centimeter. This development meets industry standards for very large-scale integration while maintaining full flexibility, promising a new era for wearable electronics.
