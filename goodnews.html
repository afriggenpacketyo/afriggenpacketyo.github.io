Clinical trials suggest a significant reduction in bone strength loss in the spine for users compared to a placebo group. While experts acknowledge potential benefits for targeted areas, they caution that osteopenia is a systemic disease and the belt should be an adjunct to established bone health practices like exercise and nutrition.
