The program, developed by researchers at the University of Iowa and the University of Kansas, includes videos demonstrating appropriate and inappropriate communication techniques. Studies have shown that reducing elderspeak can decrease resistance to care and lower the use of antipsychotic drugs in nursing homes. The training emphasizes addressing older adults respectfully, avoiding terms of endearment, and using appropriate language and tone.
