Level 2 BTec Firsts are equivalent to GCSEs, while Level 3 BTec Nationals can be taken alongside or instead of A-levels. These qualifications are graded from Pass to Starred Distinction and serve as a pathway to university or direct employment, particularly popular among white working-class students. Recent government decisions have preserved funding for many key BTec courses, preventing their earlier planned scrapping.
