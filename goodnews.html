This coloration is caused by sunlight filtering through Earth's atmosphere. Unlike a solar eclipse, a lunar eclipse is safe to view with the naked eye. The event provides a spectacular astronomical sight, occurring when the moon's orbit aligns perfectly with the sun and Earth.
