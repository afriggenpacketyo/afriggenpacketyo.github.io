2025-08-04 05:00:00 EDT

Electrolytes, which are minerals like sodium and potassium lost in sweat, are crucial for bodily functions but are typically replenished through a balanced diet. Experts point out that the body is highly effective at regulating its own electrolyte levels. Unless a person is engaged in prolonged, intense exercise or experiencing rapid fluid loss from illness, drinking plain water and eating a varied diet is generally sufficient for staying properly hydrated.
