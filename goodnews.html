Following Greece, nations enter in alphabetical sequence according to the language of the host country, which for the Milano Cortina Games is Italian. Key exceptions to this alphabetical rule include the penultimate country, reserved for the host of the subsequent Winter Games (France for 2030), and the final country, which is always the current host, Italy. Notably, athletes from Russia and Belarus, competing as Individual Neutral Athletes (AIN), will not participate in this traditional parade, reflecting decisions made by the International Olympic Committee.
