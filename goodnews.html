China Activates Powerful Particle Accelerator for Atomic Research

2026-07-22 21:00:07 EDT

China has launched the High Intensity Heavy-ion Accelerator Facility (HIAF), a record-breaking particle accelerator in Huizhou, Guangdong province. This facility can generate the world's most intense beams of charged atoms, offering scientists a new instrument to create previously unknown atomic nuclei. The HIAF, developed over 16 years, will advance research in areas like cancer treatment and spacecraft materials, surpassing previous global records for atomic pulse intensity.
