Chinese Team Discovers Thriving 'Garden-Like' Ecosystem in Deepest Ocean Trenches

2026-05-27 22:00:06 EDT

An international research team, supported by China's crewed submersible Fendouzhe, has discovered a surprisingly rich and previously unknown "garden-like" ecosystem thriving in the deepest ocean trenches. Contrary to prior beliefs that only minimal life could exist under extreme pressures, perpetual darkness, and near-freezing temperatures at depths exceeding 9 kilometers, scientists found a diverse community living on rocks. Led by Professor Peng Xiaotong, the team investigated seven hadal trenches in the Indo-Pacific region between 2020 and 2024, uncovering these unexpected faunas that subsist on organic debris descending from shallower waters. This discovery significantly expands understanding of deep-sea biodiversity.
