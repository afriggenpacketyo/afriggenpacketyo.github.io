Perseid Meteor Shower Begins, Offering Celestial Spectacle

2025-07-17 12:40:35 EDT

Stargazers in the UK can anticipate the Perseid meteor shower, one of the brightest and fastest annual displays, active from July 17 to August 24. This spectacular celestial event occurs as Earth passes through debris from Comet 109P/Swift-Tuttle, causing tiny particles to vaporize and create visible streaks of light in the atmosphere. Optimal viewing conditions require a dark, clear sky away from light pollution, with the best time typically between midnight and an hour before sunrise in the northern hemisphere. Viewers are advised to check local weather forecasts for clear skies and allow their eyes time to adjust to the low light for the best chance to spot the meteors.
