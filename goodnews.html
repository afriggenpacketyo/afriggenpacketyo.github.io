Treasury Department Plans to Phase Out the Penny

Ending production of the penny will save the Mint about $56 million in annual savings, the Treasury said on Thursday. The penny, which dates from the early days of the U.S. Mint after its establishment in 1792, now costs more to manufacture than the coin is worth.
