2026-06-03 07:52:33 EDT

New research suggests that engaging in 90 minutes to two hours of weight training per week can significantly reduce the risk of premature death. Experts emphasize that strength-based training, especially when combined with aerobic exercise, offers substantial long-term health benefits, including improved cardiovascular health, muscle strength, and bone density. However, the study also found that exceeding two hours of weekly strength training did not provide additional benefits.
