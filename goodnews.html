Northern Lights Anticipated to Be Visible Across UK Due to Potential Geomagnetic Storm

2025-11-11 13:02:49 EST

The Northern Lights are expected to be visible across many parts of the UK this week, particularly in the early hours of Wednesday and Wednesday night. This celestial display is attributed to an active sun experiencing multiple Coronal Mass Ejections (CMEs) sending solar particles towards Earth. The interaction of these particles with Earth's atmosphere creates the aurora, with a Strong G3 or even Severe G4 geomagnetic storm possible. While not harmful to humans, such storms can disrupt satellites and electronic devices, but offer a rare opportunity for widespread aurora viewing.
