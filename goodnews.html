Tropical Storm Bertha Makes Landfall East of New Orleans, Weakening Expected

2026-07-22 17:02:34 EDT

Tropical Storm Bertha made landfall Wednesday afternoon in St. Bernard Parish, Louisiana, approximately 40 miles east of New Orleans, with maximum sustained winds of 45 mph. While there were no immediate reports of damage, the storm is expected to move along the Louisiana and upper Texas coastlines, bringing gusting winds, bands of rain, and a storm surge of up to 3 feet. Louisiana Governor Jeff Landry described the forecast as "extremely encouraging" due to expected weakening, but cautioned that even a weaker storm can cause localized flooding and hazardous travel conditions. Residents are advised to remain vigilant.

Former Google Recruiter Shares Key Interview Questions for Job Seekers

2026-07-22 16:25:44 EDT

A former recruiter from Google, Uber, and The New York Times advises job candidates to ask insightful questions about the company's problems and how the new hire will solve them. Instead of predictable inquiries, asking "What's actually broken right now that you want this person to fix?" demonstrates a proactive mindset and a desire to tackle challenges. Other recommended questions include "A year from now, what will make you say that this hire worked out?" and "What's the part of this job people underestimate?" These questions help candidates understand success metrics, identify with the role, and reveal their analytical capabilities.
