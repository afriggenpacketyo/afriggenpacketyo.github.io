Colvin was arrested and charged, with a charge of assaulting a police officer remaining on her record for over 60 years until its expungement in 2021. She later became one of four plaintiffs in the landmark Supreme Court case Browder v. Gayle, which challenged bus segregation. Colvin's story gained national recognition in recent years, inspiring an upcoming movie and underscoring her early stand for equal rights.
