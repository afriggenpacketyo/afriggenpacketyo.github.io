Iconic Cerne Abbas Giant Undergoes Restoration in UK

2026-05-28 08:54:00 EDT

Specialists have begun the process of re-chalking the Cerne Abbas Giant, Britain's largest chalk hill figure, located in Dorset. This meticulous work, requiring approximately 17 metric tons of fresh chalk, is performed roughly once a decade to maintain the visibility of the 55-meter-tall figure. The National Trust is undertaking this renewal to combat algae growth and erosion caused by weather conditions.
