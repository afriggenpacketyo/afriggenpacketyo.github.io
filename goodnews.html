However, qualifying for a HELOC in the current economic climate involves navigating a more cautious approval process. Lenders typically look for sufficient home equity (at least 15% to 20%), a strong credit score (620 or higher), a low debt-to-income ratio (ideally below 43%), and stable income and employment history. Borrowers can improve their chances of approval by checking their credit report, lowering their DTI ratio, shopping around for lenders, and considering a co-applicant.
