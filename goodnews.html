Australia Expands Under-16 Social Media Ban to Include YouTube

2025-07-29 23:49:00 EDT

The Australian government has reversed an earlier decision and will now include YouTube in its landmark social media ban for children under 16, set to begin in December. The move follows a recommendation from the eSafety Commissioner, who noted YouTube was the most frequently cited platform where children encountered harmful content. While users under 16 can still view videos, they will be prohibited from having an account for uploading or interacting. The policy, which also covers platforms like TikTok and Instagram, has been praised by ministers as a necessary step to protect children from predatory algorithms.
