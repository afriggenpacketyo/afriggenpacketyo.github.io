Upcoming Total Lunar Eclipse to Create 'Blood Moon' Phenomenon

2025-09-05 08:22:00 EDT

A total lunar eclipse is set to occur on September 7, 2025, and will be visible across Europe, Africa, Asia, and Australia, creating a spectacular "blood moon" effect. This phenomenon happens when the Earth passes directly between the sun and the moon, casting a shadow that envelops the lunar surface. Sunlight filtering through Earth's atmosphere gives the moon a distinct reddish-orange hue, with the intensity of the color indicating the amount of dust and clouds present. Unlike a solar eclipse, a lunar eclipse is safe to view with the naked eye and provides a prolonged astronomical event for skywatchers.
