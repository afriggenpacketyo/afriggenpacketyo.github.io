Experts Advise on Safe Use of AI Chatbots for Mental Health Support

2026-03-07 09:05:01 EST

As loneliness rises, more people turn to AI chatbots for emotional support, prompting mental health experts to issue guidelines on their safe use. While chatbots can be useful for learning about mental health, generating journaling prompts, or researching coping strategies, professionals like Leanna Fortunato and Esin Pinarli caution against using them for diagnosis or during a mental health crisis. Concerns include AI's historical inability to recognize severe crises, potential for eroding real-life social skills, and lack of confidentiality. Experts strongly advise against sharing personal medical information and emphasize that AI cannot replace human interaction for genuine emotional challenge and reciprocity.
