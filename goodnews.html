This "graveyard" provides crucial insights into whale-fall ecosystems and cetacean evolution over geological time. The study, published in Nature, highlights how whale carcasses sinking to the ocean floor create unique deep-sea environments that support specialized scavenger communities. The findings significantly advance our understanding of these rare natural phenomena and their role as fossil archives.
