2026-05-27 22:00:06 EDT

Scientists have discovered a vibrant and previously unknown ecosystem flourishing in the planet's deepest ocean trenches, thriving on organic matter from above. Using China's crewed submersible Fendouzhe, researchers explored seven hadal trenches in the Indo-Pacific region, uncovering a rich community of life on rocks at depths exceeding 9 kilometers. This finding challenges previous assumptions that only a limited number of organisms could survive under such extreme conditions of immense pressure, perpetual darkness, and near-freezing temperatures. The discovery highlights the vast unexplored biodiversity of the deep ocean.
