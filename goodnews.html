Renowned German Artist Georg Baselitz Dies at 88

2026-05-04 07:26:00 EDT

Georg Baselitz, the internationally acclaimed East German-born artist renowned for his expressive, figurative works and revolutionary upside-down paintings, has died at 88. Baselitz, born Hans-Georg Bruno Kern, defied convention throughout his career, from being expelled from art academy for refusing industrial work to creating controversial pieces that led to legal challenges. He gained global fame for his "Heroes" series and later for inverting his subjects, a technique that challenged public perception and viewing habits. Despite his significant impact on Neo-Expressionist painters worldwide, Baselitz often downplayed his influence on society, maintaining an image as an unfaltering rebel.
