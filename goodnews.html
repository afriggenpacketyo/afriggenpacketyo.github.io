Coastal Rail Service Between San Diego and Orange Counties to Resume After Landslide Repairs

2025-05-29 21:20:44 EDT

Crews have placed thousands of tons of boulders along the rail line to absorb water flow and stabilize potential runoff, and are preparing to construct a 1,400-foot-long catchment beside the rail.
