Hummingbird Feeders Can Be Deadly If Not Properly Maintained

2025-06-10 07:30:00 EDT

Hummingbird feeders can be harmful or even deadly to hummingbirds if they are not cleaned regularly. Dirty feeders can harbor harmful microbes, leading to infections in the birds, such as candida, which causes tongue swelling and starvation. Wildlife rehabbers frequently rescue hummingbirds suffering from feeder-related infections. To maintain a healthy feeder, it is crucial to use the correct sugar-water recipe and clean the feeder every one to three days, depending on the weather.
