Students Recreate Ancient Rice Dish with Surprisingly Healthy Outcome

The manuscript contains recipes from the Han Dynasty era, including steamed fish and chicken soup. Using wooden steamers and clay pots, the students prepared a non-glutinous rice dish that turned out to be "loose yet elastic" in texture. The ancient rice had a pleasing texture and a lower sugar content than modern rice, leading researchers to describe it as a "comfort food." The project deepened the students' appreciation for traditional Chinese culture and ancient culinary practices.
