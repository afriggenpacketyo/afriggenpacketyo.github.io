Largest Supermoon of 2025, the Beaver Moon, to Rise This Week

2025-11-05 04:34:58 EST

The largest and brightest full Moon of 2025, known as the Beaver supermoon, is set to appear in the night sky this week. A supermoon occurs when a full Moon coincides with its perigee, the point in its elliptical orbit where it is closest to Earth, making it appear up to 14% larger and 30% brighter than usual. This event will be the second of three supermoons this year and will be the closest the full Moon comes to Earth in 2025. The name "Beaver Moon" is a traditional nickname for November's full Moon, reflecting the time of year when beavers are actively preparing their dams for winter.
