Experts Highlight Signs and Treatment Challenges of Depression in Older Adults

2025-07-25 09:40:00 EDT

Mental health experts emphasize that depression in older adults often presents with more physical, or somatic, symptoms compared to younger populations, leading to frequent misdiagnosis as normal aging. Stigma and a lack of awareness among both patients and doctors can cause delayed recognition, allowing the condition to become more chronic. Treatment is complicated by the fact that older adults are often on other medications, increasing the risk of adverse drug interactions. Experts stress the importance of professional diagnosis and note that older adults are frequently excluded from clinical trials, slowing progress in treating late-life depression.
