2026-02-25 04:49:24 EST

This reduction stems from scrapping the Energy Company Obligation (Eco) scheme and reallocating some costs to general taxation. However, the benefit is partially offset by rising costs for maintaining energy networks, meaning the total annual saving for a typical household will be £117, bringing the average bill to £1,641. Despite this welcome drop, prices remain significantly higher than pre-Ukraine war levels, urging consumers to seek further savings.
