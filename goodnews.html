China Develops First Commercial '3-Lane Highway' in Optical Fiber

2026-06-13 00:00:06 EDT

Chinese researchers have activated the world's first commercial optical fiber communication system utilizing three transmission bands (S, C, and L), significantly increasing data-carrying capacity. This advancement, deployed on a 35km link, is described as expanding existing infrastructure like a 'two-lane motorway into a three-lane highway.' The technology overcomes previous limitations in the S-band, which was too unstable for commercial use, by combining it with multi-core fiber technology. Developers suggest this innovation could be crucial for future AI networks, potentially boosting transmission capacity per core by nearly half and allowing a single fiber to carry over five times the traffic of conventional systems.
