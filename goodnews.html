Sir Andreas Whittam Smith, Co-founder of The Independent Newspaper, Dies at 88

2025-12-01 00:48:34 EST

Sir Andreas Whittam Smith, the distinguished co-founder of The Independent newspaper, has passed away at the age of 88, his family announced. He was instrumental in launching the publication in 1986 alongside colleagues Stephen Glover and Matthew Symonds, pioneering a groundbreaking non-partisan editorial stance that profoundly transformed British journalism. Beyond his impactful media career, Sir Andreas dedicated significant service as the First Estates Church Commissioner from 2002 to 2017, overseeing the Church of England's multi-billion-pound investment portfolio. He was also formerly President of the British Board of Film Classification and was knighted in 2015 for his extensive public contributions.
