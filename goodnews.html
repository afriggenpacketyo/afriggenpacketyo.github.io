2025-08-17 00:00:15 EDT

Researchers in China have created a new method for making biological tissues, including brains, transparent for imaging, allowing for an exceptionally clear view of their internal structures. The technique produces vivid and highly accurate three-dimensional images by preserving the tissue's original morphology while enhancing the brightness of fluorescent dyes used for highlighting cells. China Develops Concept for Hypersonic Drone Carrier with Pivoting 'Scissor Wing'

2025-08-16 22:00:14 EDT

Chinese researchers are exploring a novel aircraft design for a hypersonic drone carrier that utilizes a single, rotating 'scissor wing' to optimize performance across different speeds. The concept, inspired by a NASA design from the 1970s, features a wing that sits perpendicular to the fuselage for efficient lift at low speeds. As the aircraft accelerates toward Mach 5, the wing pivots to become parallel with the fuselage, transforming the entire vehicle into a 'waverider' for high-speed flight. This innovative approach aims to solve the aerodynamic challenges of transitioning between subsonic, transonic, and hypersonic speeds, potentially creating a highly versatile military aircraft.
