Taxpayers must still estimate their tax liability and pay it by the original April 15 deadline to avoid penalties and interest. The late payment penalty is typically 0.5% of the unpaid amount per month, and interest accrues daily on the outstanding balance. For those unable to pay their full bill, the IRS offers options such as installment agreements and, in cases of severe financial hardship, an Offer in Compromise.
