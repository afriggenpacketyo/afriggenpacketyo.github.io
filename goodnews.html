2026-02-05 14:00:10 EST

Chinese researchers have achieved a breakthrough in secure data transmission, successfully demonstrating tamper-proof quantum communication over 100 kilometers using single atoms. The team established quantum links between these atoms with individual light particles, or photons, enabling the generation of identical, shared secret keys for encryption at both ends. This innovative method, termed device-independent quantum key distribution (DI-QKD), guarantees security even if the communication devices are inherently flawed or have been tampered with, drawing its robust protection from the principles of quantum mechanics.
