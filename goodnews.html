2026-06-22 21:00:06 EDT

The development of artificial intelligence (AI) has deep historical connections, notably through the work of Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz and the ancient Chinese text, the I Ching. Leibniz's development of the binary numeral system, crucial for modern computing, found a surprising parallel in the I Ching's 64 hexagrams, which represent a similar binary concept developed thousands of years earlier. Jesuit missionary Father Joachim Bouvet recognized this connection and informed Leibniz, who was greatly inspired by this discovery. This historical exchange highlights a profound, often overlooked, intellectual link between Chinese and Western thought that laid foundational principles for contemporary technology.
