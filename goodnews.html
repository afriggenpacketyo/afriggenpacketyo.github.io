Treasury yields decline as Federal Reserve begins monetary policy meeting

2026-06-16 14:02:05 EDT

The yield on the 10-year Treasury note, a key benchmark, fell to 4.422%, while the 2-year Treasury note yield also dropped. This movement in yields is partly attributed to a provisional peace agreement between Washington and Tehran, which could lead to an extension of the U.S.-Iran ceasefire and the reopening of the Strait of Hormuz to shipping. Investors are also anticipating the Federal Reserve's policy decisions, which are expected to maintain the benchmark lending rate.

Yum! Brands to sell Pizza Hut for $2.7 billion to private equity and Yum China

2026-06-16 13:57:55 EDT

Yum! Brands is divesting its Pizza Hut chain for $2.7 billion, with private equity firm LongRange Capital acquiring operations outside mainland China and Yum China Holdings purchasing the mainland China segment. This sale comes after a period of significant challenges for Pizza Hut in the U.S. market, driven by intense competition and aggressive discounting from rivals like Domino's and Papa John's. The move aims to streamline Yum! Brands' focus on its core brands, including KFC and Taco Bell, while positioning Pizza Hut for future growth under new ownership.
