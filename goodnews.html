Understanding HELOC Minimum Draw Requirements

2025-05-08 14:38:57 EDT

Home equity lines of credit (HELOCs) can be a cost-effective borrowing option for homeowners, but they often come with minimum draw requirements that can impact their affordability. Lenders may require an upfront draw, ongoing minimum usage during the draw period, and minimum balance requirements. These rules can affect interest costs and repayment plans, potentially leading to higher expenses if borrowers are forced to draw more funds than they need. It is important for borrowers to understand these requirements before taking out a HELOC and to compare options to ensure the terms align with their borrowing needs.
