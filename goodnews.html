For decades, civil society groups have campaigned to change the name "Mohrenstrasse," as the term "Mohr" (Moor) is a derogatory historical term for Black people. The renaming is seen as a significant step in Germany's efforts to decolonize public spaces and acknowledge the contributions and historical presence of Black people in the country.
