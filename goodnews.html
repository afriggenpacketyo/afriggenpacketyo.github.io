Rare 1776 Copy of US Declaration of Independence Discovered in UK Archives

2026-07-02 19:39:29 EDT

A rare, surviving copy of the US Declaration of Independence has been discovered at The National Archives in Kew, UK, marking the only known example of its kind outside the United States. A volunteer uncovered the 1776 document in February while cataloging papers from Royal Navy captains during the American Revolutionary War, believing it to be one of 11 copies printed in Exeter, New Hampshire. This particular copy was seized by the Royal Navy on Christmas Eve 1776 from an American ship off the coast of Portugal, intended for rapid distribution and not long-term preservation. Following restoration, the document will be displayed in the archives' exhibition on the path to American independence, hailed as an extraordinary and vanishingly rare historical find.
