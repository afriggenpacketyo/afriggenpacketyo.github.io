EFL Clubs Vote to Expand Championship Play-Offs to Six Teams

2026-03-05 06:05:16 EST

English Football League (EFL) clubs have approved a proposal to expand the Championship play-offs from four to six teams, starting in the 2026-27 season. This new format, mirroring the National League, will allow teams finishing from third to eighth to compete for promotion to the Premier League. Third and fourth-placed teams will directly enter the semi-finals, while fifth will play eighth and sixth will play seventh in one-legged quarter-finals. The decision aims to increase competitive fixtures and offer more clubs a chance at promotion, enhancing the drama of the football calendar.
