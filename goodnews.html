2025-10-17 11:02:00 EDT

This cross-cultural exchange aimed to transcend musical boundaries, fostering deep connections and expanding participants' global perspectives. The initiative highlighted music's potent ability to inspire, provoke, and build bridges between diverse cultures, concluding with a strong sense of community and shared artistic achievement.

Mortgage Refinancing: Experts Question Relevance of 1% Rule Amid Falling Rates

2025-10-17 09:59:24 EDT

As mortgage rates decline, financial experts are reassessing the conventional "1% rule" for refinancing, suggesting its universal applicability may be limited this fall. While a one-percentage-point rate reduction is generally considered sufficient to offset refinancing closing costs, the optimal timing for refinancing now hinges on individual financial circumstances, the remaining loan balance, and the current stage of the loan term. Borrowers with substantial balances or those who secured loans at recent peak rates could realize significant savings from even smaller rate reductions. Furthermore, refinancing to consolidate high-interest debt or eliminate private mortgage insurance can be financially prudent, even without achieving a full 1% rate decrease. (not investment advice)
