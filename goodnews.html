NASA's Perseverance Rover Finds Strongest Hints of Ancient Life on Mars

2025-09-10 22:48:54 EDT

NASA's Mars rover Perseverance has uncovered rocks in a dry river channel that present the strongest hints yet of potential ancient microscopic life, scientists reported. While emphasizing that in-depth analysis on Earth is needed for confirmation, the findings include organic carbon and minuscule specks enriched with iron phosphate and iron sulfide. On Earth, these compounds are byproducts of microorganisms consuming organic matter. This discovery, from the Neretva Vallis river channel in Jezero Crater, marks the 25th sample gathered by Perseverance and is considered the most compelling candidate in the rover's search for long-ago life, despite the complex challenges of sample retrieval.

2025-09-10 22:00:12 EDT

China has inaugurated the Changtai Yangtze River Bridge, now the world's longest cable-stayed bridge, connecting Changzhou and Taizhou in Jiangsu province. Spanning 10.3 kilometers with a main span of 1,208 meters, this mega-structure uniquely accommodates an expressway, regular road, and intercity railway on the same deck, significantly cutting travel times. A distinctive feature is its asymmetrical lower deck design, a first for large-span bridges, necessitated by the weight disparity between rail and road systems.
