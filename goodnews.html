UK Braces for Potential Northern Lights Display Amid Severe Geomagnetic Storm Warning

2025-11-12 02:40:53 EST

The UK is anticipating a spectacular Northern Lights display this week, particularly across its northern half, due to intense solar activity. The Sun is undergoing an active phase, releasing multiple Coronal Mass Ejections (CMEs) that send charged particles towards Earth, potentially triggering a "Strong G3 or Severe G4" geomagnetic storm.
