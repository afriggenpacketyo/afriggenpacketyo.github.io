Historically, Tule fog has caused numerous multi-vehicle pileups on highways, though none major have been reported recently. Despite its hazards, the fog is beneficial for agriculture, aiding dormancy requirements for crops like almonds and pistachios, crucial for high yields. Authorities advise extreme caution for drivers, recommending postponed travel or specific safety measures when driving in dense fog.
