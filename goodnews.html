New Research Indicates Fertility Decline in Older Women Even with Young Donor Eggs

2026-07-06 19:33:47 EDT

New research suggests that women aged 49 and over face significantly lower fertility treatment success rates, even when utilizing young donor eggs. The study of 1,774 women found that those 49 and older had double the miscarriage risk and lower pregnancy chances compared to women aged 35-40.
