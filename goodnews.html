FDA Announces Reforms to Expedite Approval of Generic Biologic Drugs

2025-10-29 14:56:46 EDT

The Food and Drug Administration is implementing new measures to accelerate the development and approval of biosimilars, which are generic versions of complex biological medicines. This initiative is part of the Trump administration's effort to lower prescription drug costs by increasing market competition. The reforms aim to cut the typical five-to-eight-year development timeline in half, partly by suggesting that direct human comparison studies between the biosimilar and the brand-name product may no longer be necessary. Health officials noted that while biologics represent only 5% of prescriptions, they account for over half of total drug spending in the U.S.
