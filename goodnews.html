Birds in tropical forests, for instance, often utilize simpler motifs to effectively penetrate dense soundscapes, whereas those in temperate regions tend to employ more complex songs for closer-range communication. This study, leveraging AI and a vast global database, reveals a surprising underlying simplicity to the vast array of avian vocalizations.
