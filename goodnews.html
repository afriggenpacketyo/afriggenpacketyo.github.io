2025-07-17 10:25:21 EDT

The procedure combines the mother's egg and father's sperm with a small amount of healthy mitochondrial DNA from a female donor. This method successfully reduced the transmission of mutated DNA, which can cause debilitating and fatal conditions, by 77% to 100% in the newborns.
