This condition becomes more common with age due to natural shifts in circadian rhythms, declining melatonin production, and hormonal changes associated with menopause. Other significant factors include mental health conditions like depression and anxiety, as well as evening alcohol consumption, which disrupts sleep in the later part of the night.
