Guidelines for Pet Care During Cold and Snowy Weather

2026-01-04 00:26:07 EST

As cold and snowy conditions affect many parts of the UK, pet owners are advised on how to keep their animals safe and warm, both indoors and outdoors. The RSPCA and PDSA veterinary charity recommend providing extra blankets for pet beds and creating additional warm spaces around the home. Raising older dogs' beds off the ground can prevent draughts, while increased indoor playtime helps pets stay active if they spend less time outside. For walks, dogs with thinner fur may need a winter coat, and owners should always check and rinse paws to remove salt or grit.
