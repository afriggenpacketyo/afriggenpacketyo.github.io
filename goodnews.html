Prominent AAP MP and Six Others Defect to Ruling BJP in India

2026-04-25 03:36:00 EDT

Raghav Chadha, a prominent Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) Member of Parliament, has resigned from the party and joined the ruling Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) along with six other MPs. This move significantly weakens the AAP's presence in the upper house of the Indian parliament, reducing its strength from ten members to three. The defection is strategically timed ahead of upcoming assembly elections in Punjab, a key AAP stronghold. The mass resignation allows Chadha and the other defecting MPs to avoid disqualification under India's anti-defection law, which exempts Rajya Sabha members if two-thirds of their party's legislators agree to merge with another party.
