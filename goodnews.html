VAR Controversy: Florian Wirtz's Apparent Offside Goal for Liverpool Against Fulham Stands Due to Premier League Rule

2026-01-04 15:26:47 EST

A controversial goal by Florian Wirtz for Liverpool against Fulham, which appeared offside, was allowed to stand after a VAR check, ultimately contributing to a 2-2 draw. The decision is attributed to the Premier League's unique "tolerance level" rule, which grants attackers a benefit of the doubt of approximately 5cm on offside calls. This policy, in place since the 2021-22 season, aims to account for potential technology inaccuracies, even with semi-automated offside technology (SAOT). Both Fulham's manager and Wirtz himself expressed surprise at the ruling, highlighting how this approach diverges from other major leagues that employ millimetre-precise offside determinations.
