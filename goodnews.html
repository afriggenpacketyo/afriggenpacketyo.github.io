China Develops Free Bamboo Drone Flight Control Software

2026-04-04 02:00:10 EDT

China has successfully developed and released the world's first free flight control software specifically designed for bamboo drones, addressing the unique challenges posed by the material's low-frequency vibrations. This innovative software, detailed in a paper published in Heilongjiang Science, features redesigned control algorithms that leverage bamboo's natural vibration-damping properties. By implementing an extended Kalman filter, the system significantly reduces control latency from 15-20 milliseconds to an impressive 8-10 milliseconds, thereby improving responsiveness while maintaining crucial stability. This groundbreaking development aims to overcome the limitations of existing commercial flight controllers, fostering the industrialization of sustainable bamboo-based unmanned aerial vehicles and impacting drone technology globally.
