2026-01-21 20:00:12 EST

Chinese scientists from Jilin University have discovered straw-shaped, ultra-thin carbon nanotubes in moon samples collected from the far side. Using high-resolution electron microscopes, researchers believe these structures formed under extreme conditions involving micrometeorite impacts, solar wind exposure, and ancient volcanic activity.
