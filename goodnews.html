ABC News chief medical correspondent Dr. Tara Narula stressed the necessity of an "individualized approach" to weight management, acknowledging that while significant weight regain is common, a sustained loss of 4% to 5% of baseline weight remains clinically significant. The article underscores that strategies such as lifestyle counseling, consistent exercise, and potentially a gradual dose tapering of medications may be crucial for maintaining long-term weight loss after discontinuing GLP-1 drugs.

Understanding Gold IRAs: Mechanics, Rules, and Investor Suitability

2026-03-05 17:04:20 EST

Gold Individual Retirement Accounts (IRAs) are gaining attention as gold prices surge, prompting investors to consider precious metals for long-term financial plans. A gold IRA is a self-directed account allowing physical gold, silver, platinum, and palladium, distinct from traditional stocks and bonds. It requires a specialized custodian and IRS-approved depositories for storage, meaning metals cannot be held at home. These accounts typically incur higher fees due to custodianship, storage, and dealer markups. Gold IRAs are best suited for investors already maximizing conventional retirement contributions who seek diversification and a hedge against economic turbulence, rather than those seeking aggressive growth or managing smaller balances.
