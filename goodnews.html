2026-03-31 19:01:43 EDT

The NHS in England will offer the weight-loss jab Wegovy for free to over a million people at risk of heart attacks and strokes, or those with serious circulation problems who are also overweight. This weekly injection, prescribed alongside existing heart medicines and a healthy diet, has shown in trials to reduce the risk of future cardiovascular problems by 20%, even before significant weight loss. The drug works by mimicking a hormone that promotes fullness, though side effects like bloating or nausea can occur. The rollout is expected this summer, following an agreement ensuring the treatment's cost-effectiveness for the NHS.
