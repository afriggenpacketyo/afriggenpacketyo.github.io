Conversely, Chapter 13 bankruptcy has no income ceiling but mandates a steady income to manage court-approved repayment plans. Beyond these bankruptcy options, debt relief alternatives such as debt settlement programs, which negotiate balance reductions, and debt management programs, which consolidate payments and reduce interest, offer viable solutions for individuals overwhelmed by debt.
