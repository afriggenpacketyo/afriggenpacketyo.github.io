NASA Prepares for Historic Artemis II Crewed Moon Orbit Mission

2026-03-30 17:19:59 EDT

NASA is set to launch the Artemis II mission this week, sending four astronauts on a historic nine-day flight around the moon. This mission, after weeks of delays, marks the first crewed flight of the Space Launch System rocket and the Orion deep space capsule. Commander Reid Wiseman, pilot Victor Glover, astronaut Christina Koch, and Canadian astronaut Jeremy Hansen will become the first humans to venture towards the moon since Apollo 17 in 1972. The mission aims to thoroughly test the Orion capsule's systems, serving as a crucial step toward future moon landings and establishing a lunar base.
