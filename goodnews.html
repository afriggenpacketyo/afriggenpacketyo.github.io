2025-11-22 12:45:21 EST

The Internal Revenue Service is set to enforce new, stricter tax reporting rules for cryptocurrency transactions beginning with the 2025 tax year. Under the new regulations, crypto brokerages will be required to issue Form 1099-DA to report gross proceeds from digital asset sales, a measure aimed at curbing tax evasion. This change treats crypto similarly to other properties like stocks, where capital gains or losses must be reported upon sale.
