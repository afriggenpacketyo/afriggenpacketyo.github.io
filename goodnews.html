Archaeologists in Peru Uncover 3,500-Year-Old City Believed to Be Ancient Trading Hub

2025-07-06 09:31:40 EDT

Researchers suggest the discovery provides insight into the fate of the Caral, the oldest known civilization in the Americas, after it was impacted by climate change.
