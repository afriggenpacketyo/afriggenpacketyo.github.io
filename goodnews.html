NASA Launches Twin TRACERS Satellites to Study Solar Wind and Space Weather

2025-07-23 19:48:00 EDT

NASA has successfully launched the twin TRACERS satellites, a mission designed to study the interaction between the solar wind and Earth's magnetic field. The identical spacecraft will fly in tandem to provide detailed measurements of how energy from the sun couples with our planet's magnetosphere. This process creates space weather, which can generate spectacular auroras but also disrupt GPS signals, power grids, and satellites. By better understanding these dynamics, scientists hope to eventually predict and mitigate the potentially dangerous effects of solar activity on critical Earth-based and space-based infrastructure.
