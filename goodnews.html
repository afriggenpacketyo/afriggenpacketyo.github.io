NASA's Artemis II Moon Rocket Returns for Repairs, Further Delaying Launch

2026-02-26 03:19:27 EST

NASA's Artemis II moon rocket has been returned from its launch pad to the Vehicle Assembly Building for critical repairs, pushing its crewed mission around the moon to no earlier than April 1. The problem is reminiscent of a similar issue faced by the Artemis I mission, despite prior corrective actions.
