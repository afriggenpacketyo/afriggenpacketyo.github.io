Rare Copy of US Declaration of Independence Discovered in UK Archives

2026-07-02 19:39:29 EDT

A rare and previously undiscovered copy of the US Declaration of Independence has been unearthed by a volunteer at The National Archives in Kew, UK, marking it as the sole known example of its kind outside the United States. This significant document, one of only 11 copies originally printed in Exeter, New Hampshire, in July 1776 to disseminate news of American independence, was found among the papers of Royal Navy captains from the American Revolutionary War. It was notably seized by British forces on Christmas Eve 1776 from an American ship, the Dalton. Following meticulous restoration, this "extraordinary discovery" will be prominently featured in the archives' exhibition dedicated to the path to American independence, providing unique historical insight.
