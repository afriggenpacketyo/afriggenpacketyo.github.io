2026-02-15 13:53:34 EST

Located 20 miles offshore between Racine and Kenosha, Wisconsin, the 217-foot ship, a popular steamer, went down in October 1872 during a severe gale, leading to eight fatalities when a lifeboat capsized. Ehron, who has dedicated decades to this search, found the vessel using side-scan sonar.
