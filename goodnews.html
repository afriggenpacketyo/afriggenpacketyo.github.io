Indian billionaire Gautam Adani and nephew settle SEC fraud allegations for $18 million

2026-05-15 01:30:22 EDT

Indian billionaire Gautam Adani and his nephew Sagar Adani have agreed to pay $18 million in a settlement with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) to resolve civil fraud allegations. The SEC had accused the two men of misleading investors as part of a scheme involving solar contracts in India. Gautam Adani will pay a $6 million penalty, and Sagar Adani will pay $12 million, with both consenting to the settlement without admitting or denying the allegations. Adani Green Energy, the company at the center of the allegations, stated it is not part of these proceedings. This settlement could ease legal uncertainty for the Adani Group and potentially accelerate its expansion plans.
