Chinese Team Develops and Releases World's First Free Flight Control Software for Bamboo Drones

2026-04-04 02:00:10 EDT

A Chinese research team has successfully developed and made available the world's first free flight control software specifically engineered for bamboo drones, addressing unique challenges posed by natural materials. Unlike conventional airframes, bamboo structures introduce low-frequency vibrations, typically in the 8-20 hertz range, which standard flight controllers struggle to manage effectively. The team innovatively redesigned control algorithms and utilized an extended Kalman filter to match bamboo’s structural properties, leveraging its natural vibration-damping characteristics. This breakthrough reduces control latency from 15-20 milliseconds to an impressive 8-10 milliseconds, significantly improving responsiveness and stability, and is poised to accelerate the industrialization of sustainable, bamboo-based unmanned aerial vehicles by offering an accessible, open-source solution.
