2026-03-20 15:07:42 EDT

The plan includes replacing the currently idle and leaking pool with a new 50-meter competition pool and an adjacent recreational pool, designed for year-round use. The original two-story pool house will undergo rehabilitation, preserving its Spanish Colonial Revival exterior while incorporating modern amenities like an elevator and a large indoor gender-neutral bathhouse with individual changing and toilet facilities.
