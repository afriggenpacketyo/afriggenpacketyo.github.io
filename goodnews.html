McCook was credited with saving the life of Sgt. John Boyd, who survived the crash after McCook maneuvered the damaged plane to allow Boyd to parachute out. McCook's remains were not recovered until 1947, and were interred as "Unknowns" at the National Memorial Cemetery of the Pacific until recently.
