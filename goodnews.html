Expert Advice on the Efficacy of Cough Medicines Versus Home Remedies

2025-12-03 21:09:24 EST

Professor Jacky Smith from the University of Manchester clarifies common misconceptions about cough remedies, emphasizing that most coughs stem from colds and typically resolve naturally without medication.
