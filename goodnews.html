Google Invests in Proxima Fusion to Develop Europe's First Commercial Fusion Power Plant

2026-07-07 02:24:28 EDT

Google has invested in Germany-based Proxima Fusion, a nuclear fusion startup, participating in a €411 million ($468 million) funding round. This significant backing aims to accelerate the development of Europe's first commercial fusion power plant, underscoring Google's long-term interest in fusion as a potentially abundant, carbon-free, and inherently safe energy source. Proxima Fusion is advancing stellarator technology, with aspirations for a demonstrator plant by the early 2030s and a commercial facility later that decade. The capital will facilitate expanding high-temperature superconducting cable production and developing essential engineering and manufacturing systems, though commercializing this complex technology remains a substantial challenge.
