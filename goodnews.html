Despite fears that the Palisades fire and subsequent debris flows would decimate the fish in Topanga Creek, biologists recently discovered 30 adult trout and 21 juveniles. This resilience is attributed to the fish finding refuge in less impacted areas of the creek. The recovery of the creek's ecosystem, aided by recent rainfall, also offers hope for other native species.
