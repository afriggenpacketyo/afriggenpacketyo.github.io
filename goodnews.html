2025-12-22 06:55:00 EST

Recent years have seen increased interest in the aurora borealis and australis, vibrant light displays caused by solar storms ejecting charged particles toward Earth's magnetic poles. Visible farther from the poles during a solar maximum, auroras have also been observed on most planets in our solar system.
