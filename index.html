Kelce, who professes to be a lifelong Six Flags enthusiast, expressed a personal motivation to help make the parks exceptional for future generations. This high-profile partnership emerges as Six Flags endeavors to boost attendance and profitability following several years of leadership changes and a pandemic-induced slump, a development that saw the company's shares surge by over 15%.
