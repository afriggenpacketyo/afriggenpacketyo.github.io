California Storm Causes Fatalities and Widespread Flooding in Southern California

2025-11-16 00:45:17 EST

Heavy rain has pounded Southern California, resulting in at least one fatality and widespread flooding, with evacuation warnings and orders issued across Los Angeles County. A 71-year-old man died when his vehicle was swept off the Pleasant Grove Creek Bridge in Sutter County by two to three feet of water. Although the worst of the storms has passed, an unsettled weather pattern is expected to continue into next week, bringing more wet conditions. Wildfire burn scar areas remain particularly vulnerable to flash flooding, mudslides, and debris flows.
