Vanderbilt quarterback Diego Pavia has apologized for a "disrespectful" social media post made after he finished as the runner-up for the Heisman Trophy. Pavia expressed his disappointment at not winning the award, which went to Indiana quarterback Fernando Mendoza. In his apology, Pavia stated his desire to win was intense and that he did not handle his emotions well, regretting his "disrespectful" comments towards the Heisman voters. He also conveyed respect for Mendoza's accomplishments and the selection process. Pavia reflected on his journey of overcoming doubt and fighting for his success, expressing gratitude for his support system. He looks forward to competing in the ReliaQuest Bowl with his team.
