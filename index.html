A Polymarket trader reportedly made approximately $300,000 by correctly betting on President Biden's last-minute pardons, including those for prominent critics of former President Trump. Analysis of cryptocurrency transactions suggests these prescient bets, placed just hours before Biden left office, were highly unlikely to occur by chance. Experts suggest the trader may have possessed insider information, raising concerns about potential abuse of prediction markets for profit. Similar suspicious trades have been noted on Polymarket related to geopolitical events, prompting scrutiny over the lack of regulation and enforcement in these markets.
