Crediting former teammate Aeneas Williams and NBA standout Avery Johnson for catalyzing his decision, Faulk expressed enthusiasm for the challenge, stating he thrives when uncomfortable and proclaiming, "Winners win." His appointment is seen as a pivotal moment for Southern University's football program.
