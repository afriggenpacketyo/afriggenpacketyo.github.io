Article Critiques Trump's "Stone Age" Threat Against Iran, Warns of Tragic Outcomes

2026-04-03 13:10:00 EDT

An article critically examines President Trump's recent threat to bomb Iran "back to the Stone Ages," arguing that this shift in rhetoric reflects growing frustration over Iran's unexpectedly vigorous resistance in the ongoing conflict. The author suggests that this destructive approach, which prioritizes annihilating Iranian capabilities over achieving regime change, could lead to immense and widespread devastation for the Iranian populace. The article highlights a fundamental mismatch between American and Iranian definitions of victory, noting Iran's steadfast emphasis on resistance despite significant material losses. It warns that pursuing this dynamic to its grim conclusion could cripple Iran's economy and consign ordinary Iranians to years of hardship.
