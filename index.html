A major Russian oil refinery in Tuapse has been targeted by Ukrainian drones for the third time this month, resulting in a "massive fire" and forcing the evacuation of nearby residents. Over 160 firefighters responded to the blaze, with no casualties reported. Local authorities warned of airborne combustion products and advised residents to take precautions. Ukraine confirmed the strikes as part of efforts to reduce Russia's "military-economic potential." President Putin has ordered an urgent response to the incident.
