Senator Bill Cassidy is battling for reelection in Louisiana's GOP primary, facing a Trump-backed challenger, Julia Letlow, and state Treasurer John Fleming. Cassidy, one of seven Republicans who voted to convict Trump in 2021, is testing the former president's influence on the party. While Cassidy emphasizes his legislative achievements and ability to work across the aisle, Letlow, endorsed by Trump, criticizes his loyalty. The article highlights voter sentiment, with some viewing Cassidy's impeachment vote as a betrayal, while others prioritize his policy work. The outcome, likely heading to a runoff, will reveal the balance between party loyalty and independent representation in a deeply red state.
