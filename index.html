2026-02-07 15:58:18 EST

West Ham's recent 2-0 victory against Burnley has significantly reignited the Premier League relegation battle, reducing the points gap between 17th and 18th place to a mere three. Following a notable mid-season renaissance, marked by winning three of their last four matches, West Ham now appears to be single-handedly sustaining the fight for survival. While Wolves and Burnley are statistically almost certain for relegation, West Ham's newfound momentum, fueled by key players and recent striker acquisitions, injects renewed optimism despite Opta ranking their relegation chances at 75.77%. The article posits that the relegation race will be tight, likely involving Nottingham Forest, Crystal Palace, and West Ham, potentially extending to the season's final day. (not investment advice)
