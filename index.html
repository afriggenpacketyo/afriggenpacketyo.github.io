Williams, 44, received a standing ovation and showed flashes of her former brilliance, saving a match point in the second set before Joint ultimately prevailed. Joint expressed awe at playing the legend on Centre Court, calling it a "crazy" dream come true. Williams, who released a statement after the match, expressed gratitude for the atmosphere and the opportunity to compete again. This match marked a significant moment in her return to professional tennis, ahead of her scheduled doubles play with sister Venus.
