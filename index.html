Zayas, a 23-year-old Puerto Rican star, is boxing's youngest active world champion and has unified WBA and WBO titles. The article highlights the collision of two storied boxing lineages and the high stakes for both fighters, with oddsmakers favoring Ennis but Zayas confident in launching his own stardom. The fight is expected to be a compelling test of skill and mettle, promising an exciting spectacle.
