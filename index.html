Major companies like Target, Amazon, and UPS have announced job reductions, contributing to a weakening labor market, further obscured by delayed federal jobs data. Experts warn that these layoffs could be an early indicator of broader cutbacks, potentially influenced by wider AI adoption in the coming year. (not investment advice)
