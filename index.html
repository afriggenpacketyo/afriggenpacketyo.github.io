Understanding the 90-Day Rule in Chapter 7 Bankruptcy and Its Implications

Payments of $600 or more to a creditor during this period may be considered preferential transfers and can be recovered by the trustee for equitable distribution among all creditors. This rule is particularly important for payments made to "insiders" like family members, which extends the period to one year.
