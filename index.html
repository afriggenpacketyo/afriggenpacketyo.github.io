Her family announced her death, stating she died peacefully surrounded by loved ones after a battle with Alzheimer's disease. Chalmers had a distinguished broadcasting career spanning over 60 years, also appearing on programs like "Come Dancing" and BBC radio shows. ITV described her as a "national treasure" and a "lasting favorite" amongst audiences, acknowledging her indelible contribution to television. She began her career at 13 and transitioned to television in the 1950s.
