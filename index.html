A man was hospitalized after allegedly exchanging gunfire with Border Patrol agents near Arivaca, Arizona. Agents attempted to stop a pickup truck, but the driver fled on foot, firing at an Air and Marine Operations helicopter and Border Patrol agents, who returned fire. The driver, identified as a U.S. citizen with a suspected history of human smuggling, was treated at the scene and transported to a hospital. The FBI is leading the investigation into an alleged assault on federal officers, with assistance from CBP and the Pima County Sheriff's Department. CBP has also initiated its own internal investigation into the shooting.
