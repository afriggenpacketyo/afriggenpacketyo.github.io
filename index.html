Five of the seven crew members aboard the International Space Station (ISS) were temporarily moved to a "safe haven" within the docked SpaceX Crew Dragon spacecraft as cosmonauts worked to repair two small air leaks in the Russian Zvezda module. Roscosmos, the Russian space agency, stated the station was safe, one leak was sealed, and preparations were underway for the second. NASA directed the crew to the capsule out of "an abundance of caution" during the repair efforts. While the situation was monitored closely, Roscosmos later confirmed the leaks did not pose a threat to the crew or onboard systems, and the crew was cleared to return to normal operations after the repair work was paused.

June Offers Homebuyers Advantages Despite Elevated Mortgage Rates

2026-06-05 14:02:02 EDT

Despite slightly elevated mortgage interest rates, June presents several advantages for homebuyers. Mortgage rates have seen a minor decline, with qualified borrowers potentially finding rates below 6%. The article highlights reduced competition among buyers, as fewer individuals are actively seeking homes compared to recent years. This decrease in competition can lead to less pressure at open houses and fewer bidding wars. Additionally, sellers may be more open to negotiation, potentially offsetting higher borrowing costs. Seasonal timing also favors buyers, with warmer weather making property viewings and inspections easier, and allowing families to potentially close before the next school year.
