2025-12-15 23:00:10 EST

Taiwan is moving to tighten its conscription rules, aiming to close loopholes and ensure fairness in the draft system following high-profile cases of draft dodging. The proposed amendments to physical classification standards would significantly narrow exemption criteria, requiring a higher Body Mass Index (BMI) or more extreme height measurements for exemption. The defense ministry's draft also includes provisions for alternative service for transgender and intersex individuals, a move that has faced backlash from rights groups. The revisions seek to ensure that all eligible citizens contribute to national defense.
