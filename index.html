Scientists Uncover Mechanism Linking Epstein-Barr Virus and Multiple Sclerosis Development

This "fatal similarity" causes the immune system to mistakenly attack its own nerve tissue. While HLA-DR15 doesn't guarantee MS, it is identified as the most crucial genetic risk factor, with infections during adolescence and environmental factors also playing roles. Efforts are now focused on developing targeted treatments and early childhood vaccinations to prevent the disease.
