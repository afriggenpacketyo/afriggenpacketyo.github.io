British Transport Police (BTP) confirmed the suitcase was located but its contents were not inside, and they are investigating the high-value theft, having released CCTV images of a suspect. This article reports on the unfortunate development in the theft case, highlighting the distress caused by the loss of irreplaceable personal belongings, particularly the ashes.
