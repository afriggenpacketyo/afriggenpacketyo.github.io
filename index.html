This strategic initiative seeks to counteract a recent surge of mysterious drone flights that have disrupted European airports and approached military installations, by integrating cost-effective technologies akin to those employed by Ukraine. Ministers are additionally evaluating ongoing support for Ukraine, as a recent study indicates a substantial decline in Western military aid in recent months, coinciding with continued Russian advances on Kupiansk. The meeting emphasizes collective efforts to fortify alliance security and sustain assistance for Kyiv amid evolving threats.
