The article explains that Cambricon's remarkable stock gains were largely propelled by a broader market rally, fueled by China's favorable policy support for indigenous artificial intelligence chips and new AI models. However, the company also reminded investors of its ongoing vulnerability to supply chain disruptions due to existing US export restrictions, underscoring the complex interplay between market enthusiasm, government policy, and geopolitical challenges in the tech sector.
