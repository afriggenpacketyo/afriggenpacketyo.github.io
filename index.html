Buffett acknowledged the potential for larger payoffs in tech but consistently prioritizes certainty and understanding over chasing big, uncertain risks. Charlie Munger concurred, raising questions about the long-term saturation and future growth of the tech field, further reinforcing their cautious approach to industries they don't fully grasp.
