MPA's extraordinary performance of 'Cut Piece' at REDCAT, where audience members cut pieces from her garment, underscored its enduring themes of social taboos, aggression, and ego, linking it to Ono's anti-war sentiments. The performance highlighted how the piece, originally conceived in the 1960s, still compels active participation and contemplation of violence and peace in a contemporary context.
