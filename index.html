Tottenham manager Roberto de Zerbi has called on his team to "silence the voice inside of us" as they fight to avoid relegation from the Premier League. Despite a recent win against Wolves, Spurs remain in the relegation zone with four games remaining. De Zerbi believes negativity, including blaming external factors like injuries or pitch conditions, is their biggest opponent. He urged players, staff, and fans to adopt a more positive mindset and "die on the pitch" in their upcoming matches against Aston Villa, Leeds United, Chelsea, and Everton. De Zerbi emphasized that they are not yet relegated and must fight for every point.

Atlassian Stock Surges on Strong Earnings Driven by Cloud and Data Center Growth

2026-05-01 10:21:31 EDT

Atlassian shares experienced a significant surge of over 20% following the release of its fiscal third-quarter earnings, which exceeded Wall Street expectations. CEO Mike Cannon-Brookes expressed confidence in the company's performance, suggesting that fears about AI disruption may be overblown. Atlassian's revenue increased by 32% year over year, with cloud revenue growing by 29%. The company also boosted its full-year guidance for cloud and data center revenue, with analysts noting the Teamwork Collection product as a key growth engine.
