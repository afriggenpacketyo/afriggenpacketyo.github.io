Despite the challenging conditions, including a lack of resources and the pervasive impact of trauma on students, the return to learning is seen as a vital step for the children's well-being and future. The effort highlights the resilience of the community and the urgent need for sustained educational support and humanitarian aid amidst ongoing conflict and restrictions.
