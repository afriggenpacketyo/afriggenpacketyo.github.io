A suspect wanted for grand theft died after crashing a vehicle into a building during a police pursuit in Jurupa Valley, in what authorities believe was a self-inflicted gunshot wound. Law enforcement attempted to use a 'grappler' device to slow the fleeing vehicle, but the suspect evaded capture and eventually lost control, crashing into a wall. Deputies then found the suspect unresponsive inside the vehicle. The Riverside County Sheriff's Department stated that all pursuits are reviewed for policy adherence and effectiveness of intervention tactics. The suspect had evaded law enforcement for over an hour before the pursuit concluded.
