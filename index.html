This accounting decision doesn't erase the debt, which often then progresses to collections. While some creditors continue internal collection efforts, many sell charged-off debts to third-party agencies within 30 to 90 days. Resolving charged-off debt, whether by paying in full, negotiating a settlement, or seeking credit counseling or bankruptcy, is crucial to prevent further credit score damage and stop collection calls.
