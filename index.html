Liverpool manager Andoni Iraola expressed his desire for midfielder Alexis Mac Allister and goalkeeper Alisson to remain at the club, despite ongoing transfer speculation. Mac Allister, with two years left on his contract, has not yet begun talks for an extension, leading to rumors of potential summer departures. Iraola acknowledged that other clubs are interested in top players but emphasized his priority is retaining his "good players." Regarding Alisson, who has one year remaining on his contract and has been linked with Juventus, Iraola highlighted the goalkeeper's experience as crucial for the team's adaptation process, especially with new signings and younger players. Iraola also indicated a need for defensive reinforcements, particularly at right-back, due to Conor Bradley's extended injury absence.
