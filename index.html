British Gas to pay £20 million to settle investigation into forced prepayment meter installations

2026-05-15 03:32:09 EDT

British Gas has agreed to pay £20 million into a redress fund and compensate customers to resolve a regulatory investigation into the forced installation of prepayment meters. The investigation uncovered instances where debt agents working for British Gas entered the homes of vulnerable customers to fit these meters. The company has apologized for the scandal, and the settlement also includes writing off up to £70 million in energy debt for vulnerable customers. The regulator, Ofgem, stated that such installations should only be a last resort with rigorous checks.
