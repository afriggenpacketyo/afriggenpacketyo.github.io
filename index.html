2026-04-17 10:00:19 EDT

Sean Garner has been jailed for 12 years after his XL bully dog, Toretto, fatally mauled 84-year-old John McColl in Warrington. The dog savaged Mr. McColl for approximately 45 minutes on Garner's driveway before being shot by armed police. Mr. McColl suffered catastrophic injuries and died five weeks after the attack. Garner was found guilty of owning a dangerously out-of-control dog. The judge described the suffering as "unspeakable" and criticized Garner's "offensive lies" and lack of remorse. Garner had misrepresented the number of dogs he owned to his landlord, failing to disclose the banned XL bullies. The victim's granddaughter expressed the family's deep pain and anger.
