This call follows a suggestion from UK trade minister Chris Bryant that Mountbatten Windsor, stripped of royal titles, should comply with demands for information. Despite Republicans holding the House majority, the oversight committee approved an inquiry into the Epstein case amidst public outcry and questions surrounding Donald Trump's involvement.
