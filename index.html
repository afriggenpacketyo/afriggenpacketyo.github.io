President Trump spoke with the Artemis II astronauts following their record-breaking lunar orbit, commending them as 'modern-day pioneers' who have 'inspired the entire world.' The crew, having traveled further from Earth than any humans in history and witnessed parts of the Moon's far side with the naked eye, received praise for their courage and genius. Trump highlighted the mission as a precursor to future lunar landings and a step towards reaching Mars. The conversation, marked by a slight delay due to the vast distance, underscored the significance of the mission in advancing human space exploration and reinforcing America's leadership in the field.
