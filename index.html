The Israeli military confirmed the handover, indicating the remains would be transported to the National Centre of Forensic Medicine in Tel Aviv for official identification. Hamas's armed wing had earlier reported finding the body of an Israeli soldier in the eastern Shejaiya neighborhood, within Israeli-controlled territory. This action is part of a U.S.-brokered ceasefire agreement, which stipulated Hamas's return of all remaining living and deceased hostages. Israel has accused Hamas of intentionally delaying the recovery process, while Hamas maintains that locating bodies amidst the conflict's rubble presents significant challenges, highlighting the ongoing complexities of the humanitarian situation.
