Projections indicate an Amoc collapse could accelerate climate change in Europe tenfold, with severe impacts on agriculture, infrastructure, and societal stability. The scarcity of direct Amoc observations and the risk of defunding current monitoring initiatives leave scientists and policymakers in a state of high uncertainty, underscoring the urgent need for sustained international funding and collaboration.
