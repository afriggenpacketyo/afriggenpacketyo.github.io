Prosecutors claim Gedel and Samuel Dodsworth were jointly responsible for the attack, with Dodsworth allegedly disposing of the weapon. Gedel reportedly expressed jealousy of "nonce prisoners" and disgust at sharing a wing with sex offenders. The court heard Watkins had received threatening notes the day before his death.
