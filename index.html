Southampton Sacks Manager Will Still After Five Months

2025-11-02 14:18:13 EST

Southampton Football Club has sacked manager Will Still after a brief five-month tenure, following a disappointing start to their Championship season. The 33-year-old, appointed in May after leaving French Ligue 1, only secured two wins in 13 league games, alongside six draws and five defeats, which left the team languishing in 21st place. Technical director Johannes Spors explained that the decision was made to improve performances and results, believing it offers the best opportunity to turn the season around and climb the league table. Men's under-21 head coach Tonda Eckert will assume interim management. Still himself had previously acknowledged the absence of an "instant fix" for the team's persistent issues, suggesting a deeper problem within the club.
