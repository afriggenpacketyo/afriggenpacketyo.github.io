The exhibition was originally scheduled to open on Christmas Day and continue for nearly a year, a prospect that had generated considerable excitement and led numerous fans to make extensive travel arrangements, only to be met with disappointment. While no explicit official reason for the cancellation was provided beyond a general apology, the broader context of strained relations between the two nations points to a clear political undercurrent behind the decision, significantly impacting cultural exchange and public enjoyment.
