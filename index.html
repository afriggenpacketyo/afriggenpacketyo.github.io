LeBron James and Luka Doncic Lead Lakers to Victory Over Pelicans

2026-01-06 22:48:31 EST

LeBron James and Luka Doncic delivered exceptional performances, both tallying 30 points, to propel the Los Angeles Lakers to a 111-103 victory against the New Orleans Pelicans. This marked the second instance in three games where both star players achieved at least 30 points, underscoring their increasingly dynamic partnership on the court. Coach JJ Redick lauded their synergistic play, particularly highlighting their effectiveness in pick-and-roll situations. James, in turn, emphasized Doncic's role as the team's franchise player, stating that the rest of the squad adjusts their game to complement his style. The Lakers' dominant defensive effort in the fourth quarter, limiting the Pelicans to a mere 17 points, proved instrumental in securing the win, even as James's participation in the upcoming back-to-back game remains "TBD" due to his age.
