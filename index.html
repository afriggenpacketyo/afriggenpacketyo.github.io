2026-01-22 06:21:24 EST

The UK Met Office has issued severe weather warnings, including an amber alert, for heavy rainfall and considerable flooding risks across various regions of the UK. Eastern Scotland, Northern Ireland, and specific parts of England and Wales are anticipated to endure substantial precipitation and powerful winds. Additionally, Storm Ingrid, named by the Portuguese weather service, is projected to deliver further heavy rain and wind gusts up to 60mph to southwestern England and Wales. Given that the ground is already saturated from recent weather, the Met Office warns of widespread surface water and river flooding, alongside potential transport disruptions and large overtopping waves in coastal zones, advising residents in affected areas to exercise extreme caution.
