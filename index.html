Belgium has commenced a disruptive three-day nationwide strike, with powerful unions protesting the government's proposed social and budget reforms, leading to significant disruption across transport, schools, hospitals, and various public services. Rail services were immediately and severely affected, with further intensification expected as hospital staff, kindergarten workers, teachers, and public-sector employees join the widespread walkouts. The most severe impact is anticipated on Wednesday when private-sector unions participate, likely grounding all flights at major airports.
