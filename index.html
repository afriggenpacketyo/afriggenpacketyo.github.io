If no candidate secures a majority, the candidate with the fewest first-choice votes is eliminated, and their votes are redistributed based on voters' second choices. This process continues until a candidate reaches a majority, ensuring broad support for the winner. Preliminary results based on first choices will be available shortly after polls close, but the ranked choice tabulations will begin on July 1, with official certification on July 15.
