Jake and Rebecca Haro were arrested on suspicion of killing their son after investigators determined Rebecca's initial report of his disappearance and an attack was fabricated. Jake Haro, 32, was already on probation for a 2018 child cruelty conviction where a baby in his care sustained multiple bone fractures. Despite the parents maintaining their innocence, investigators believe Emmanuel is deceased and are continuing exhaustive efforts to recover his remains. Riverside County child protective services also removed a 2-year-old girl from the Haro home following Emmanuel's reported disappearance.
