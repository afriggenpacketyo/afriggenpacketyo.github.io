US Treasury warns financial institutions about sanctions risk related to Chinese refineries handling Iranian oil

2026-04-28 23:29:58 EDT

The U.S. Treasury has issued a warning to financial institutions regarding potential sanctions for engaging with Chinese refineries that process Iranian oil. These independent refineries, often referred to as 'teapots,' are significant importers of Iranian crude, with revenue ultimately benefiting the Iranian regime. The Treasury urges enhanced due diligence on transactions involving these refineries and entities in Iran's oil supply chain. This action is part of the U.S. strategy to exert maximum pressure on Iran by cutting off its revenue streams, with several Chinese refineries already having been sanctioned.
