It can also be beneficial when dealing with major life changes like divorce or asset sales, or if new debt was recently incurred, to avoid complications or accusations of fraud. Conversely, delaying can be risky if creditors are escalating collection efforts, as interest accrues and immediate protections from an automatic stay are foregone. (not investment advice)
