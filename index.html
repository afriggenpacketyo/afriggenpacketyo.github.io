Jessie Ottman-Combs, 41, was arrested in San Francisco after allegedly leaving a backpack containing over 300 grams of methamphetamine in a rideshare vehicle. Police were alerted to the suspected narcotics by the rideshare driver, who discovered the illegal drugs. Officers, with the driver's cooperation, devised a plan to return the backpack to Ottman-Combs at his motel, leading to his arrest. A subsequent search of his room uncovered additional drug paraphernalia and electronic devices, bringing the total seized methamphetamine to 355.7 grams. Ottman-Combs was charged with possession, possession of drug paraphernalia, and transportation or sale of methamphetamine, and is scheduled for arraignment, highlighting successful law enforcement action against drug trafficking.
