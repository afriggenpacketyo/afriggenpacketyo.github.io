Clarifying the Legality of Debt Collectors Adding Interest and Fees to Unpaid Balances

2025-11-07 12:31:18 EST

Debt collectors are generally permitted to add interest and fees to an unpaid debt, but only if such charges were stipulated in the original contract with the creditor. They cannot arbitrarily add new fees or increase rates beyond what was agreed upon or what is allowed by state law. The Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA) protects consumers by requiring collectors to provide a detailed validation notice of the debt, including any added charges. If a borrower disputes the debt, the collector must verify the amount owed and justify the additional fees, preventing them from falsely inflating the balance.
