Boulter, 29, lost 6-4 6-4 to Ukrainian 27th seed Marta Kostyuk, continuing her Grand Slam struggles and her inability to reach the second week of a major. Despite her loss and recent form issues, Boulter expressed a renewed "buzz" and a new perspective after taking time off, focusing on improving her serve for future tournaments.
