Creatine supplements, increasingly popular due to social media influencers, are being promoted for a wide array of benefits, from enhancing muscle growth to boosting cognition and even regulating blood sugar. Decades of scientific research robustly support creatine's efficacy in increasing lean muscle mass and improving athletic performance, especially when integrated with resistance training, by supplying rapid bursts of energy. Nevertheless, claims concerning its benefits for bone health, general brain function, and protection against Alzheimer's disease remain either nascent or lack substantial supporting evidence. Experts recommend opting for creatine monohydrate from third-party certified brands for healthy adults, particularly those without kidney disease, cautioning that for recreational users, its proven utility is primarily limited to intensifying workouts rather than serving as a "magic bullet."

Senator Warren Calls for Investigation into Trump's Alleged Attacks on Federal Reserve Independence

2025-09-04 06:00:00 EDT

Senator Elizabeth Warren is strongly advocating for the Senate Banking Committee to halt the confirmation hearing for nominee Stephen Miran and instead launch an immediate investigation into President Trump's alleged assaults on the Federal Reserve's independence. Warren points to three critical instances: Trump's threats to dismiss Chair Jerome Powell, his claims of Powell misusing funds for Federal Reserve building renovations, and his efforts to remove Fed Governor Lisa Cook. She argues that a politicized Federal Reserve, one that bases decisions on presidential directives rather than objective economic data, would severely undermine public confidence, increase borrowing costs for American consumers, and inevitably lead to higher inflation, ultimately detrimentally impacting working-class citizens and the broader U.S. economy.
