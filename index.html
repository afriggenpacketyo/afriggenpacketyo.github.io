However, many states offer stricter protections, with some, like Texas and Pennsylvania, largely prohibiting wage garnishment for unsecured debts. To avoid this outcome, consumers can negotiate directly with creditors, work with debt relief companies, enter a debt management plan, or consider bankruptcy as a final resort.
