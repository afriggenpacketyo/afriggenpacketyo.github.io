UK Government to Ban Deepfake AI "Nudification" Apps

2025-12-18 12:43:14 EST

The UK government is set to ban "nudification" apps that leverage deepfake AI to digitally remove clothing from images, forming a key part of its comprehensive strategy to reduce online violence against women and girls. These forthcoming laws will criminalize the creation and distribution of such AI tools, expanding upon existing legislation against sexually explicit deepfakes and intimate image abuse. Technology Secretary Liz Kendall emphasized that this measure aims to bolster online safety and ensure accountability for those who profit from or facilitate the use of these harmful applications. Child protection organizations, including the Internet Watch Foundation, have strongly endorsed the ban, underscoring the severe potential for harm, particularly in the context of child sexual assault material.
