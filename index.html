One individual tragically died, and two others sustained critical injuries in a house fire that occurred early Thursday morning in Montebello, leading police to initiate an arson investigation due to "suspicious circumstances." Montebello police received a 911 call around 4:30 a.m. reporting the blaze, with additional reports suggesting someone might have been on fire. Upon arrival, officers discovered a man and woman outside the engulfed home, both suffering from severe burns; the man was apprehended after attempting to flee the scene despite his injuries. Both injured individuals were hospitalized in critical condition. Detectives are now actively investigating whether the fire stemmed from domestic violence, with the identities of the victims yet to be released.
