Abu-Sittah, a plastic surgeon and professor of conflict medicine, aims to elongate her remaining upper arm bone through a painful "distraction" surgical procedure to eventually enable a functional myoelectric prosthesis. Kenzi's story is one of many, as Abu-Sittah and the Ghassan Abu-Sittah Children's Fund work tirelessly to heal children wounded in war, highlighting the immense and ongoing humanitarian need in Gaza. This mission underscores a profound commitment to making a tangible difference in the lives of these children, one at a time, amidst widespread devastation and loss.
