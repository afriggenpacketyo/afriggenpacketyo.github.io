This article provides practical advice on adjusting AC settings to effectively lower electric bills this summer, addressing concerns about rising energy prices and persistent heatwaves. It highlights that the baseline temperature setting is the most impactful adjustment, with each degree raised potentially saving approximately 3% on cooling costs. Implementing automated schedules and setbacks can further reduce annual heating and cooling expenses by up to 10%. The article also recommends utilizing the "auto" fan mode to conserve electricity and prevent condensation issues, and stresses the importance of managing humidity, which allows for slightly higher temperature settings while maintaining comfort, leading to tangible savings.
