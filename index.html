The study highlights that EVs, like internal combustion engine vehicles, are affected by extreme temperatures. Drivers in colder climates need to plan for reduced range, though EVs can remain practical with proper preparation, such as pre-conditioning the cabin while plugged in and optimizing charging strategies.
