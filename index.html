This agreement emerged during trilateral talks in Abu Dhabi, aimed at ending the nearly four-year-long war. European Command stressed that maintaining military dialogue is crucial for global stability, transparency, and de-escalation. The resumption coincides with the expiration of the last remaining nuclear weapons treaty between the two powers. The article also notes a first prisoner exchange in five months between Ukraine and Russia, highlighting diplomatic efforts despite ongoing obstacles.
