Travelers without U.S. passports who have been in these affected nations within the past three weeks will be restricted from entering the U.S. U.S. citizens, green-card holders, and service members are exempt. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) stated that the outbreak poses a low immediate risk to the U.S. public but emphasized the need for vigilance and coordination with international partners to monitor potential exposures.
