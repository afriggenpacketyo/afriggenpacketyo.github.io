British heavyweight boxer Anthony Joshua stated he is "not ready" for the loss of his two close friends and team members to "sink in yet" as he prepares for his first fight since their deaths. Joshua and his companions were involved in a car crash in Nigeria on December 29, 2025, which resulted in the fatalities of Sina Ghami and Latif "Latz" Ayodele. Joshua, who sustained minor injuries, admitted to feeling "a little bit nervous" before his upcoming bout against Kristian Prenga. He is focused on keeping his mind occupied to avoid dwelling on the tragedy, aiming to perform well for his team and make himself proud.
