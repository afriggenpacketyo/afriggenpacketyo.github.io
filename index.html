China has condemned an intrusion at its embassy in Tokyo and a bomb threat as "grave violations" of international law and threats to diplomatic personnel. The acting head of the embassy reported a threatening letter from an organization claiming to be former Japanese police and Self-Defense Forces members, along with an online terrorist threat from an individual identifying as a reserve officer. China alleges that Japanese police did not take these incidents seriously or implement effective measures. These events have heightened diplomatic tensions between the two nations, with China demanding accountability and stronger security protections for its diplomatic missions.
