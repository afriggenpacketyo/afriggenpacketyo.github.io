Five Italian divers tragically die during cave exploration in the Maldives

2026-05-15 03:38:17 EDT

Tragedy struck the Maldives as five Italian citizens lost their lives in a scuba diving accident. The divers were reportedly exploring caves at a depth of approximately 50 meters in the Vaavu Atoll. The Maldives' military confirmed finding one body in a cave and believed the other four were also in the vicinity. The incident is considered the worst single diving accident in the nation's history, occurring amidst rough weather conditions. British Gas fined £20 million for forcibly installing prepayment meters on vulnerable customers

2026-05-15 03:32:09 EDT

British Gas has agreed to pay £20 million into a redress fund and compensate customers following an investigation into the forced installation of prepayment meters. The regulator, Ofgem, found that debt agents working for British Gas had illegally entered the homes of vulnerable customers to fit these meters. The company has also agreed to write off up to £70 million in energy debt for vulnerable customers. This practice continued despite British Gas being aware of the issue since 2018 and an internal audit flagging it in 2021. Ofgem emphasized that forced meter installations should only be a last resort with rigorous checks.
