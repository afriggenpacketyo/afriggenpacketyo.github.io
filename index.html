Lakers focus on rebounding in preparation for Rockets playoff series

2026-04-14 19:18:06 EDT

The Los Angeles Lakers are emphasizing rebounding in their practice sessions as they prepare for their first-round playoff series against the Houston Rockets. The Lakers ranked as the fourth-worst rebounding team in the NBA during the regular season, while the Rockets led the league in rebounding. The absence of key rebounders Luka Doncic and Austin Reaves due to injury further heightens the importance of this area. Coach JJ Redick stated that ball control and boxing out are critical keys to winning the series. The team is dedicating significant practice time to rebounding drills, recognizing the Rockets' strong offensive rebounding capabilities and physical defense as major challenges.
