Chinese Biotech Scizeng Secures Global Licensing Deal Valued Up to $1.44 Billion

2025-12-16 02:47:10 EST

Chinese biotechnology firm Scizeng has entered into a global licensing agreement for its product GenSci098, potentially yielding up to $1.44 billion. The deal includes an upfront payment of $70 million and up to $1.37 billion in milestone payments tied to the product's development, regulatory approval, and commercial success. Scizeng will also receive royalties exceeding 10% on future sales. GenSci098 is currently undergoing clinical trials in China and the U.S. This partnership is considered a strategic step for Scizeng's parent company, Changchun High-Tech, in its ambition to become a global pharmaceutical innovator, highlighting the product's significant potential.
