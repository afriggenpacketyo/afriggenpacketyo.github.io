Facing a medical debt collection lawsuit can be daunting, but understanding settlement options is crucial to avoid severe consequences like wage garnishment. Promptly responding to the lawsuit and verifying the debt's legitimacy are the first steps. Negotiating a settlement, often for 50% to 70% of the original debt, is common as creditors prefer avoiding costly litigation. Lump-sum payments or payment plans can be proposed, but all agreements must be documented in writing. For complex cases or multiple debt actions, seeking assistance from a debt relief expert can be beneficial, though it involves fees. Direct negotiation may suffice for simpler situations, but written agreements are paramount.
