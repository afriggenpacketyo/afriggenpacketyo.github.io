2025-11-03 18:30:07 EST

Uspace Technology Group, a satellite manufacturer, is pivoting towards artificial intelligence applications by establishing a new AI application technology center in Shenzhen, following the relocation of its headquarters to Guangzhou. This initiative, spearheaded by its unit Gang Hang Ke, will focus on intelligent decision-making for satellite remote sensing data and AI-based satellite data processing and analysis. The move aligns with Uspace's broader efforts to operate a network of over 100 "Golden Bauhinia" satellites above the Greater Bay Area, with the remaining satellites expected to launch before 2026. This strategic realignment aims to enhance satellite-ground computing synergy and leverage AI for advanced space business operations, reflecting a commitment to technological integration and future growth.
