California is pioneering this approach as the first state to sell its own brand of generic prescription drugs, aiming to significantly reduce overall healthcare costs. This move aligns with Governor Newsom's long-standing commitment to healthcare affordability and follows the recent signing of legislation that caps insulin co-pays at $35 within the state.
