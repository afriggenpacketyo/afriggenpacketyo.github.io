Despite the U.S. Supreme Court upholding the constitutionality of these state laws, the pork industry, after numerous failed lawsuits, is now actively lobbying Congress to overturn them through the "Save Our Bacon Act" provision embedded within the House Farm Bill. This contentious effort has created divisions within the industry itself, as many farmers who have already invested significant capital to comply with crate-free systems oppose federal intervention. The ongoing debate underscores a fundamental clash between strong state voter mandates and powerful industry interests seeking to maintain practices widely criticized by animal welfare advocates as cruel.
