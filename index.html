This opinion piece presents a cynical and largely apathetic retrospective on the pop culture and absurdities that defined 2025, characterizing the year as a "deluge" of disheartening events met with collective resignation. The author contrasts significant "monstrosities," such as the Los Angeles wildfires and Donald Trump's second inauguration, with various "inanities" like the widespread craze for Labubu dolls, a manufactured internet outrage over a Sydney Sweeney advertisement, and the "Coldplaygate" scandal. The engagement of Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce is notably portrayed as a meticulously stage-managed affair designed for mutual personal and financial gain. The article concludes with a sarcastic wish for readers to embrace becoming "walking sponsored content" in 2026, anticipating future financial challenges.
