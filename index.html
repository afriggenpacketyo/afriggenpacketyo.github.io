The victim sustained severe wounds to his eyes and body. Members of the public intervened before police arrived. The suspect, who had leave to remain in the area, is in custody, and police are not seeking anyone else. Prime Minister Keir Starmer condemned the violence, and local political leaders urged transparency and calm during the investigation.
