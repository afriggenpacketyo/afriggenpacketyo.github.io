Rafael Arutyunyan, "Quad Maker," Revolutionizes Figure Skating Coaching for Elite Athletes

2026-02-09 09:00:00 EST

Rafael Arutyunyan, affectionately known as the "Quad Maker," is being celebrated as a coaching genius for his instrumental role in developing elite figure skaters, including Olympic champion Nathan Chen and Ilia Malinin, the sole skater to successfully land a quad axel in competition. With nearly 50 years of experience, Arutyunyan employs a unique coaching philosophy that blends Soviet biomechanics with European teachings, utilizing analytics and distinctive metaphors to refine complex jumping techniques. His remarkable ability to transform struggling skaters into podium contenders, combined with his recent induction into the U.S. Figure Skating Hall of Fame, underscores his profound and lasting impact on the sport. His students consistently praise his innovative methods and unwavering dedication to pushing the boundaries of athletic achievement.
