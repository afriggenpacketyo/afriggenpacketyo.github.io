As of March 27, 2026, average home equity loan rates range from 7.85% to 8.00%, while the average HELOC rate is 7.04%. These rates are poised to decline further if the Federal Reserve implements future rate cuts. The article advises shopping around for the best terms and notes potential tax benefits for IRS-eligible home repairs, while cautioning about using a home as collateral. (not investment advice)
