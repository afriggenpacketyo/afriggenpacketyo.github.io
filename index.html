Nintendo shares experienced a significant rally this week, jumping 18%, following the unexpected viral success of "Pokémon Pokopia," which sold an impressive 2.2 million units in its first four days and quickly became the Switch 2's fourth-best-selling title. This spin-off game, exclusive to the Switch 2 console, has ignited investor optimism that its popularity will provide a substantial boost to sales of the flagship console, helping Nintendo achieve its fiscal year targets of 19 million units. The game's high production value, deep gameplay mechanics, and strong multiplayer aspects have contributed to its critical acclaim, earning a Metacritic rating of 89, despite initial lower expectations for the title.
