UK Fuel Prices See Slight Decrease After Weeks of Increases

2026-04-17 07:42:41 EDT

Petrol and diesel prices in the UK have seen a minor reduction, marking the first decrease after a period of consistent rises. This change is attributed to a slight easing of wholesale prices, which had surged due to the effective closure of the Strait of Hormuz, a critical oil and gas transit route. While the decline is small, with diesel down by 0.6p and petrol by 0.3p per liter, the RAC anticipates further reductions. Drivers will likely welcome this relief after significant price hikes over the past six weeks, though filling a tank remains considerably more expensive than in late February. The rises were driven by increased crude oil prices following hostilities in the Gulf, impacting living costs for many.

Significant Prehistoric Discoveries Made Beneath Welsh Castle

2026-04-17 07:35:43 EDT

Archaeologists have uncovered a hidden prehistoric cave containing the bones of a hippopotamus and evidence of early human occupation beneath Pembroke Castle in Wales. This 'once-in-a-lifetime discovery' is expected to significantly enhance the understanding of ancient life in Britain. The cave, described as a 'remarkable prehistoric archive,' has yielded remains from the last interglacial period, around 120,000 years ago, including those of extinct hippos, mammoths, and woolly rhinoceroses, alongside stone tools. Researchers hope the ongoing five-year project will provide insights into past climates, extinct species, and human activity across multiple periods.
