Google is expanding its AI robotics footprint through a new partnership with Agile Robots, a company developing intelligent, sensor-based robotic arms and humanoid robots. This collaboration will integrate Google DeepMind's Gemini Robotics foundation models with Agile Robots' hardware, aiming to improve performance via robot deployment, data collection, model training, and iteration. The partnership provides Google with crucial real-world deployment data, as it views robotics as a key application for AI, competing with Amazon and Tesla. Agile Robots' existing 20,000 deployed robotic systems globally will integrate Google's technology, initially focusing on high-value industrial manufacturing tasks, further solidifying Google's commitment to advancing AI in the physical world.
