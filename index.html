Verified footage shows students marching and chanting slogans against the Supreme Leader at Sharif University of Technology in Tehran, with scuffles reported between protesters and government supporters. Similar protests and sit-ins have occurred at other universities, with students honoring those killed in previous unrest. These demonstrations occur amidst heightened international tensions, with the U.S. reportedly considering military action against Iran due to concerns over its nuclear program, despite ongoing diplomatic talks. The scale and nature of these new protests indicate continued dissent within the country.
