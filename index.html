These arrests follow raids on the Aragatsotn Diocese and homes of clergy, based on a priest's claims of pressure to join opposition rallies. The Church, which enjoys significant public support and has become an opposition force, views these actions as a systematic anti-church campaign ahead of the 2026 elections.
