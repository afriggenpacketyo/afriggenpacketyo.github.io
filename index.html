2026-02-09 11:26:54 EST

The abduction of 84-year-old Nancy Guthrie, mother of "Today" show host Savannah Guthrie, has now extended into its ninth day, prompting her family to publicly pledge payment of a bitcoin ransom to her alleged kidnappers. Savannah Guthrie, appearing with her siblings, released a video acknowledging a message from the abductors and declared, "We will pay" for their mother's safe return. Authorities, including the FBI and local sheriff's department, are intensely investigating the mysterious case, diligently analyzing the authenticity of the ransom notes and conducting thorough searches of Nancy's home and her daughter Annie's property. Despite these comprehensive efforts, investigators have not yet identified any suspects, persons of interest, or vehicles linked to the disappearance.
