A gunfight in Santa Ana on Sunday night resulted in the deaths of two men and injuries to a third. Police responded to reports of gunshots and discovered a "quick exchange of gunfire" involving "two different types of weapons." A 49-year-old man from Santa Ana died at the scene, while a 46-year-old man from Fullerton was hospitalized and later pronounced dead. A 43-year-old man from Los Angeles sustained injuries and is in stable condition. Authorities have not yet released information regarding a suspect or the motive behind the shooting. The Santa Ana Police Department is investigating the incident.

2026-04-20 12:25:18 EDT

Somerset's James Rew is making a strong case for inclusion in England's post-Ashes squad with his consistent batting performance this season, scoring 379 runs in five innings and achieving his 12th first-class century at age 22. While Rew is not an opener, his form could present a dilemma for England selectors, particularly regarding Zak Crawley's potentially vulnerable spot. Rew has limited experience opening in first-class cricket but has performed well in List A matches. England faces decisions on how to integrate him into the lineup, potentially considering him as a specialist batter or even an opener with a dress rehearsal in upcoming Lions fixtures.
