He sharply criticized what he termed 'egregious' practices by some rival insurers, including offshore structures and aggressive credit assumptions, expressing concern about industry contagion. Despite strong current results and $1 trillion in assets under management, Rowan is increasing Apollo's capital reserves and reducing exposure to riskier sectors. He predicts significant socioeconomic upheaval due to AI, with a potential 'blue-collar ascendancy and white-collar stress.'
