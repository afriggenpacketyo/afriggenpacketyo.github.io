Speaking after EU leaders joined U.S. peace talks, Kallas stressed that Russia has made "not one single concession" and that Putin is "just laughing," continuing to escalate the conflict. She emphasized the need for "credible and robust" security guarantees for Ukraine, focusing on a strong Ukrainian army rather than mere paper agreements. This stance contrasts with Donald Trump's suggestion of "swapping of territories" and highlights ongoing disagreements among Western allies regarding a path to peace.
