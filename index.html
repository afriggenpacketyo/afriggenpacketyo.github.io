Its inclusion in the Winter Olympics has ignited global interest, yet it also presents challenges, including heightened competitiveness and concerns about preserving the sport's traditional "gentleman's game" etiquette. The introduction of a new professional Rock League aims to offer financial viability for curlers, though grassroots participation faces a decline.
