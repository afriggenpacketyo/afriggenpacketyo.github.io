Fugitive Sex Offender Hid for 21 Years as Yacht-Owning Biotech Executive

2026-07-21 23:43:22 EDT

Ronald L. Fischer, a fugitive for over 20 years, was apprehended off the coast of New Jersey after living under the alias Richard Graydon as a yacht-owning biopharmaceutical executive in Los Angeles. Fischer, accused of rape in Rhode Island, fled during his 2005 trial and was convicted in absentia. Federal authorities utilized multiple aliases and a sophisticated network to evade capture. The U.S. Marshals Service and federal prosecutors emphasized that "time does not erase accountability." His arrest raises questions about the hiring practices of Immix Biopharma, where he was employed.
