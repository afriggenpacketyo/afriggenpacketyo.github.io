This significant growth was primarily propelled by the record-breaking domestic animated film "Ne Zha 2," which grossed nearly US$2.20 billion globally, with 97% from mainland China. Local films continued to dominate the market, accounting for almost 80% of total takings. The success of "Ne Zha 2" notably drew back many moviegoers who had not visited cinemas in several years, revitalizing the industry. (not investment advice)
