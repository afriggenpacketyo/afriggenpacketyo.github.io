These reconstructions, based on 3D scans and forensic principles, primarily reveal features of Ancient Ancestral South Indians, with traces of Middle-East Eurasian and Austro-Asiatic ancestries, suggesting global migration and population mixing. The findings support the claim of an independent urban civilization in southern India dating back to 580BC, adding a new dimension to the subcontinent's historical narrative and sparking discussions about India's diverse heritage.
