China has responded to Japan and the Philippines' announcement of maritime boundary delimitation negotiations by expanding its sea patrols in waters east of Taiwan. The China Coast Guard (CCG) stated that these actions are necessary to counter what it perceives as infringements on China's territorial sovereignty and maritime rights. Beijing views Taiwan as an integral part of its territory and has urged Japan and the Philippines to cease "illegal actions." The CCG spokesman emphasized China's commitment to firmly safeguarding its territorial integrity and maritime interests in the region, signaling continued assertiveness in its maritime claims.
