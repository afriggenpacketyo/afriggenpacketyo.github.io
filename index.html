Maintaining peace and stability in the Taiwan Strait, consistent with the long-standing U.S. one-China policy, is presented as crucial for managing risks related to trade, AI, and the Middle East. The piece suggests that reducing ambiguity and avoiding encouragement of independence forces would yield the highest return for U.S. interests.
