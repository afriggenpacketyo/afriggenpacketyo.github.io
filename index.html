Taylor described her 20-year journey as "amazing" and expressed a desire to end her career in Ireland. With a professional record of 25-1, including avenging her only defeat, Taylor is considered one of boxing's greatest. Her career revolutionized women's boxing, overcoming early sexism to become a global icon and a symbol of dignity and excellence for Ireland.
