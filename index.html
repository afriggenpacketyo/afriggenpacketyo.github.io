The suspect reportedly scaled a compound wall, stating he wanted to meet the ambassador to urge him to refrain from "hardline remarks" and threatened self-harm if rejected, with a knife found at the scene. Japan's top spokesperson, Minoru Kihara, assured strengthened security for the embassy and preventative measures to avoid recurrence, while China urged strict legal action against the individual.
