Understanding Chapter 13 Bankruptcy and Its Impact on Personal Income

Instead, the process involves creating a three-to-five-year repayment plan funded by the filer's "disposable income." This is the amount left over after accounting for reasonable and necessary living expenses such as housing, food, and transportation. The goal is to create a sustainable payment structure, allowing individuals to maintain a basic standard of living while systematically addressing their debts, making it a viable option for those with regular income.
