OpenAI's lawyers argue that Musk's lawsuit is motivated by his desire to undermine a competitor after failing to gain control of the company and that he understood the decision to establish a commercial arm. The trial's outcome could significantly impact the future of artificial intelligence development and corporate governance in the tech sector.
