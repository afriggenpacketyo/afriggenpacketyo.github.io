Do was later sentenced to prison for taking bribes and steering public funds to a nonprofit run by his daughter, which was tasked with building the memorial. County Supervisor Janet Nguyen has called the monument a "disgrace" and is advocating for it to be torn down and rebuilt correctly, noting it also lacks basic accessibility for elderly veterans. The scandal has left the county with a costly and dishonorable tribute.
