Mandelson, appointed UK ambassador to Washington in December 2024, was later removed in September 2025 due to his ties with convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein. The DV process involves extensive checks on finances, personal life, and potential blackmail risks. While Downing Street claims the decision to override the vetting agency's recommendation was made by Foreign Office officials without ministerial knowledge, the situation has led to intense pressure for explanations and the resignation of Sir Olly Robbins, the Foreign Office's top civil servant.
