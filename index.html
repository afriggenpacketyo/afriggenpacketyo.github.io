2026-02-27 06:00:00 EST

LAUSD Superintendent Alberto Carvalho's ambitious AI chatbot project, "Ed," failed months after its 2024 launch and has now led to FBI raids on his San Pedro home and district office. The multimillion-dollar deal with startup AllHere, which later declared bankruptcy and whose founder was indicted for fraud, is under intense scrutiny. While Carvalho and consultant Debra Kerr, also under investigation, have not been charged, sources indicate Carvalho himself is a focus of the probe related to financial issues. The article notes the Trump administration's focus on fraud and Carvalho's outspoken criticism of its policies, suggesting a potentially politically charged investigation.
