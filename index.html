This severe pronouncement follows weeks of escalating concerns about food insecurity and occurs nearly two years into the ongoing armed conflict with Israel, where restrictions have significantly impeded the delivery of crucial food and humanitarian aid. The IPC's declaration is based on documented evidence of widespread starvation, illness, and mortality, and it warns that the famine could spread to central and southern Gaza by late September. This critical classification serves as a powerful call to action, aiming to galvanize international response efforts and exert pressure for a cessation of hostilities and unhindered humanitarian access to the affected population.
