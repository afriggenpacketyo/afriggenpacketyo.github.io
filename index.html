Twelve others received final written warnings, and two received first written warnings. The victims, Barnaby Webber, Grace O'Malley-Kumar, and Ian Coates, were fatally stabbed by Valdo Calocane. The trust informed Nottinghamshire Police and the Information Commissioner's Office of the outcomes, with further investigations ongoing into access of records for surviving victims. Dr. Manjeet Shehmar, NUH medical director, expressed deep apologies to the families, emphasizing that unauthorized access is unacceptable and damages patient trust.
