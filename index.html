He noted that some CFTC-regulated prediction platforms bear a strong resemblance to traditional derivative contract activities, suggesting potential areas for integration into Goldman's existing business operations. While expressing enthusiasm for these developments, Solomon cautioned that the pace of Wall Street's adoption of prediction markets might be more gradual than widely anticipated by industry pundits.
