Bystander Recounts Harrowing Scene and Helping Children After Minneapolis Church Shooting

2025-08-28 08:59:57 EDT

Pat Scallen, a bystander, recounted the horrific aftermath of the Minneapolis church shooting, rushing to Annunciation Catholic School where he found children suffering emotional and physical wounds. He described seeing a boy and a girl shot in the head, and a girl shot in the neck, with one child asking him, "Please just hold my hand." Two children, aged 8 and 10, were killed, and 17 others injured, including 14 children and three parishioners in their 80s. While all injured are expected to survive, the article emphasizes the profound trauma. The shooter, Robin Westman, died by suicide, and the FBI is investigating the incident as domestic terrorism and a hate crime.
