However, mortgage refinance rates saw an increase, with the 30-year term rising to 6.94% and the 15-year term to 6.06%. The higher inflation reading is expected to delay potential Federal Reserve rate cuts, potentially leading to higher borrowing costs in the future. Borrowers are advised to shop around for the best rates and consider acting sooner rather than later, as market conditions remain unpredictable. While not ideal, current rates may still offer value for some buyers and homeowners.
