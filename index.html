While this is an accounting action for the lender, the debt remains legally owed by the borrower and significantly damages their credit report for up to seven years. Removal is possible if the seven-year reporting window expires, the information is inaccurate or doesn't belong to the individual, or in rare cases, through a goodwill adjustment or "pay-for-delete" arrangement with the creditor.
