2026-03-19 05:13:00 EDT

The war in Iran has introduced significant uncertainty into the economic outlook for Europe, complicating interest rate decisions for central banks. The European Central Bank, Bank of England, Sweden's Riksbank, and Swiss National Bank are all expected to keep rates unchanged or signal a cautious approach. The geopolitical uncertainty is a key factor influencing these central banks' cautious stance.
