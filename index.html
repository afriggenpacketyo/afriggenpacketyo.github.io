Two Arrested in Connection with Cyber-Attack on London Nursery Chain

2025-10-07 17:11:42 EDT

A 17-year-old boy and a 22-year-old man have been arrested in connection with a cyber-attack on the Kido nursery chain in London, where hackers allegedly stole photographs, names, and addresses of about 8,000 children. The Metropolitan Police made the arrests on suspicion of computer misuse and blackmail, following a referral from Action Fraud. The hackers, calling themselves Radiant, initially attempted to extort £600,000 in Bitcoin and later posted images of children online, a move cyber experts deemed a "new low." Although the hackers eventually removed the data and claimed to have deleted all files, the incident highlights the severe risks of cybercrime targeting vulnerable data.
