2026-03-31 12:25:20 EDT

Ten premature babies, who were dramatically rescued from Gaza's Al-Shifa Hospital in November 2023 amidst intense conflict, have finally been reunited with their families after spending over two years in Egypt. These infants, who gained international attention when wrapped in aluminum foil for warmth due to power outages, were transported across the southern Gaza border for urgent medical care. While seven of the original 28 babies tragically died, and some had returned during previous ceasefires, Monday's emotional reunion in Khan Younis marked a poignant moment for parents who had never met their children. This reunion, facilitated by the Red Crescent and the United Nations, brings immense joy but also underscores the enduring hardships and challenges of life in the war-ravaged Gaza Strip.
