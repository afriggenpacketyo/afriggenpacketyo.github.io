The over 15,000-square-foot facility will feature a 4,000-square-foot soundstage for live performances and fan events, along with two radio studios for live interviews, a spatial audio mixing room, and booths for songwriting and podcasting. Designed by architect Eric Owen Moss, the studio aims to foster creativity and support artists, serving as an anchor for a global network of creative hubs, with more planned worldwide. This strategic move reinforces Apple's commitment to the music and content creation space, especially as technology reshapes storytelling.
