Doncic, often criticized for arguing with officials, now remains at 15 technical fouls, second in the NBA. This decision is crucial as Doncic is currently the NBA's leading scorer, fueling the Lakers' impressive nine-game winning streak and their climb to third in the Western Conference, with his historic scoring run continuing.
