Early reviews of the controller's features, such as haptic trackpads and magnetic charging, have been largely positive. However, the price has shifted the perception from an "insta-buy" to a more considered purchase for many, particularly concerning its suitability as a primary PC controller rather than a niche peripheral for enthusiasts.
