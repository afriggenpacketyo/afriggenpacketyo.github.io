2025-11-18 11:30:41 EST

A Stockholm court has sentenced four men to prison for aggravated racist assaults, shining a spotlight on the alarming growth of white supremacist "Active Clubs" in Sweden and internationally. The men, members of Aktivklubb Sverige, brutally attacked three men of foreign origin in Stockholm, shouting racial slurs and causing severe injuries. Prosecutors presented evidence including swastikas and "Love Sweden, Hate Islam" stickers, confirming the hate crime motives. The case underscores warnings from Sweden's intelligence service about the radicalization of young men attracted to such far-right organizations.

Millions of Federal Student Loan Borrowers to Begin Repayment Amid Policy Changes

2025-11-18 11:25:00 EST

Millions of federal student loan borrowers, particularly recent spring graduates, are soon required to begin repaying their debts as the six-month grace period concludes. With over $1.6 trillion in outstanding federal education debt, this repayment cycle begins during a challenging time, marked by over 5 million borrowers already in default and the Trump administration phasing out several affordable repayment plans. Experts advise borrowers to identify their loan servicers and research available repayment options, such as the Pay As You Earn (PAYE) and Income-Contingent Repayment (ICR) plans, which offer income-based payments and potential forgiveness, though these are also being phased out by 2028. 2025-11-18 11:24:32 EST

Scotland and northern England are bracing for significant travel disruptions as the Met Office issues yellow weather warnings for widespread snow and ice. Further alerts for snow and ice are in place for northern Scotland, including the Orkney, Shetland, and Western Isles, expecting up to 4 inches of snow on higher ground.
