Singer-songwriter Olivia Rodrigo is releasing her third album, "You Seem Pretty Sad for a Girl So in Love," which explores the complexities of a relationship that ultimately falls apart, a departure from her previous heartbreak-themed work. Rodrigo shares her dream of an outdoor proposal in Central Park and reveals her pre-selected wedding song, "I Melt with You." She discusses her evolving songwriting process, moving from angsty reflections to capturing romantic joy, and the challenges of portraying happiness authentically in music. Rodrigo also touches on her growing control over her career, her outspokenness on social issues, and her decision to part ways with her managers to build a hand-picked creative team, emphasizing integrity and authenticity as key to her appeal.
