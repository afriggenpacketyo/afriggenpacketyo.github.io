The revised subscription revenue forecast for the fiscal year ending January 2026, implying 14.4% growth, represented only a modest $13 million increase from previous projections, leading analysts to interpret it as bordering on a "slight guide down." This market reaction occurs amid broader pressures on software stocks, with commentators increasingly worrying about generative AI's potential disruptive impact on incumbent companies.
