This innovative system fundamentally challenges the traditional, polluting image of coal by entirely eliminating combustion. A critical feature is the in-situ capture of the high-purity carbon dioxide generated, which is then catalytically converted into valuable chemical feedstocks or mineralized into compounds like sodium bicarbonate.
