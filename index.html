They, along with public health experts, express deep concern that weakening the recommendation is a dangerous regression that will leave a new generation of children vulnerable. Critics of the panel's decision argue that it ignores decades of evidence proving the vaccine's safety and effectiveness in preventing a virus that often spreads silently within families.
