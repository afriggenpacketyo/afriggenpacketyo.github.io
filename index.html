President Trump Hosts IndyCar Drivers Ahead of National Mall Race

2026-07-13 18:11:45 EDT

President Trump hosted top-ranking IndyCar drivers at the White House for a "Grand Prix Showcase" ahead of an unprecedented street race scheduled for August on the National Mall. The Freedom 250 Grand Prix plans a 1.7-mile track around iconic landmarks like the National Archives and Smithsonian, with Trump predicting record-high ratings for the event. He was joined by IndyCar racers Alex Palou, Felix Rosenqvist, and David Malukas, alongside various industry and government officials. An IndyCar demonstration on Pennsylvania Avenue followed the remarks. The event, organized by the Freedom 250 quasi-government group, aims to celebrate American freedom and motorsports in the nation's capital.
