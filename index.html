UK Cake Sheds Face Regulatory Scrutiny Amidst Growing Popularity

2026-06-06 19:41:06 EDT

However, this sweet success is now under threat as some local councils consider enforcing stricter licensing rules. Bakers worry that these new regulations, potentially requiring expensive street trading licenses, could force many to close down.
