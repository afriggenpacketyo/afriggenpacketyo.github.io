Notably, major department stores like Macy's and JCPenney, which operate as independently owned buildings with separate, compliant fire protection systems, remain open. This closure reflects a broader national trend of struggling malls, with another Orange County mall slated for demolition, highlighting the significant challenges facing the retail sector and the urgent need for infrastructure investment to ensure public safety.
