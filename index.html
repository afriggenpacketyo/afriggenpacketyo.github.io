This article notes that the confidence-building initiative follows repeated clashes and incidents, including water cannons, between ships from both nations in the disputed waters. Analysts, however, remain skeptical about the long-term effectiveness of such a deal, given past failures to agree on a code of conduct and resolve underlying tensions.
