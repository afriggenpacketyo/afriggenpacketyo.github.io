Tyrrell Hatton secured the winning half-point for Europe, while Shane Lowry's crucial putt earlier ensured trophy retention. Captain Luke Donald earned legendary status by masterminding Europe's second consecutive victory, becoming only the second captain to lead the side to back-to-back home and away wins, a testament to his meticulous preparation and leadership amidst a highly charged and often abusive crowd environment.
