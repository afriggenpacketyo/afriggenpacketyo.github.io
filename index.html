The debt may be sold to collection agencies, which can pursue aggressive repayment tactics, including lawsuits and wage garnishment. Strategies to manage charged-off debt include negotiating settlements, setting up payment plans, enrolling in debt management programs, or, as a last resort, filing for bankruptcy. Acting promptly is crucial to resolving the debt and rebuilding credit.
