The incident, occurring north of Warroad, included two cars classified as "dangerous goods," though authorities have confirmed no reported leaks, injuries, or fires. Hazardous materials teams are actively on site, monitoring the air quality and have reported no environmental concerns thus far. An evacuation zone has been established along Highway 313, with a local motel providing shelter for affected residents. First responders and CN employees are diligently working on clearing and cleaning up the scene, an operation anticipated to last two to three days, with the cause currently under investigation.
