This initiative is part of a broader government campaign to improve children's readiness for reception, with a target of 75% achieving a good level of development. Teachers have noted an increase in children starting school without basic skills like toilet training or adequate communication abilities. The council is also offering parent workshops and resources to support families in developing these essential skills before the autumn term.
