Easley reportedly acquired his tigers from figures like Joe Exotic and Bhagavan 'Doc' Antle, known from the Netflix series 'Tiger King.' All tours at the private facility have been canceled indefinitely. This article highlights the inherent dangers of working with large predatory animals.
