Americans are facing significant price hikes on Apple products, dubbed 'MacBook Sticker Shock,' as the cost of a fully loaded MacBook Pro now reaches $10,000. This surge is primarily attributed to a quadrupling in the price of RAM, a crucial component in electronics, driven by the insatiable demand from the AI boom. Apple, along with other tech companies like Microsoft, has increased prices across most of its Mac and iPad models, citing the "rapid expansion of AI data centers" as the cause. The article highlights Apple's struggle to maintain its supply chain dominance as AI companies outbid for components. Analysts expect further price increases, including for iPhones, as customers acclimate to higher costs. (not investment advice)

GE Vernova's Electrification Division Thrives Amid AI Boom, Beyond Gas Turbines

2026-07-02 13:44:06 EDT

GE Vernova's electrification division is rapidly emerging as a critical beneficiary of the AI buildout, extending beyond its well-known gas turbines. This segment, which produces transformers, switchgear, and grid software, booked $2.4 billion in data center equipment orders in Q1 2026, surpassing its total for fiscal year 2025. The company's overall backlog is projected to hit $200 billion by 2027, driven by accelerating electricity demand from energy-intensive AI data centers. GE Vernova's acquisition of transformer manufacturer Prolec further strengthens its position, allowing better control over supply chains and improved lead times. The article highlights GE Vernova's strategic positioning to capitalize on the AI boom, with its stock surging over 694% since its 2024 spin-off. (not investment advice)
