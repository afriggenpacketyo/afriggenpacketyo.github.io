This U.S. military buildup occurs amidst intelligence indicating the Iranian government is at its most vulnerable point in decades, following a severe crackdown on internal protests. While Trump initially threatened military action over the protests, they have significantly reduced without intervention, and the administration is now primarily focused on securing a diplomatic agreement regarding Iran's nuclear program and ballistic missile capabilities.
