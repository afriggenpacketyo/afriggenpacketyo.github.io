While US officials asserted the operation targeted individuals illegally present or working unlawfully, critics, including Democratic Senator Raphael Warnock, have questioned both the administration's immigration strategy and how these firms allegedly employed workers without proper documentation. This incident further complicates US-South Korea trade and investment ties, prompting calls for a swift resolution from both sides.
