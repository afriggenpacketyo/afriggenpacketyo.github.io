One child was recovered at the scene of the shootout, and the other two children were subsequently found hours later at a remote campsite in a rugged forest, unaccompanied. Phillips had been wanted for previous crimes, including an armed bank robbery, and had initially taken his children over a custody dispute, denying them formal education or healthcare for years. The children's mother has since expressed deep relief that the "ordeal" for her children has finally ended.
