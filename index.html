Los Angeles DA Withdraws Resentencing Motion for Menendez Brothers

Los Angeles County District Attorney Nathan Hochman is withdrawing the previous DA's motion to resentence Lyle and Erik Menendez for the murder of their parents. Hochman cites the brothers' continued denial of responsibility and fabrication of self-defense claims. He points to premeditation and the brothers' multiple conflicting accounts of the event. The brothers' resentencing hearing is still scheduled for March 20 and 21, and they are pursuing other avenues for release, including a habeas corpus petition and a clemency request.
