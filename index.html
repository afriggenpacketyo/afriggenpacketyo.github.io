2026-03-13 11:53:03 EDT

Adobe announced that CEO Shantanu Narayen will step down once a successor is appointed, though he will remain as the company's chairman. Narayen, who has led Adobe since 2007, is credited with transforming the company from software licenses to a subscription model with its Creative Cloud suite and expanding into generative AI. Under his leadership, Adobe's stock saw significant growth. The company also reported strong quarterly results and guidance, with revenue up 12% year-over-year. The leadership transition comes as Adobe navigates challenges in the AI-driven software market, including the abandoned acquisition of Figma.
