Jetten emphasized that "millions of Dutch people have turned a page," signaling a rejection of "politics of negativity" and a desire for a stable, ambitious, and broad-based coalition. This outcome represents a significant turnaround for D66, which had polled much lower just weeks ago, reflecting a clear voter desire for new leadership and a potential new era in Dutch politics following the collapse of Wilders' previous coalition.
