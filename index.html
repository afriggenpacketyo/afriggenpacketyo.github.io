This forces all non-essential government functions to cease until funding is restored, as mandated by the Antideficiency Act. During a shutdown, essential employees, such as military personnel and air traffic controllers, must work without immediate pay, while non-essential workers are furloughed. While entitlement programs like Social Security and Medicare continue to pay benefits due to permanent funding, administrative services can be severely delayed, impacting the public in various ways.
