Understanding Debt Collection Timelines for Deceased Estates

Debt collectors have limited time, known as creditor claim periods, to file against an estate, with deadlines varying by state from a few months to over a year. After this period, creditors may lose the right to collect from estate assets, though secured debts remain attached to collateral.
