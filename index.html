The lawsuit claims Mack was subjected to torture, dismemberment, and concealment of her remains, causing profound harm to her son, who was six years old at the time of her murder. The suit also targets potential proceeds from a documentary involving Heuermann's family. Mack's remains were found in areas linked to the Gilgo Beach homicides. Heuermann has denied involvement in the deaths of seven women, and reports suggest he may change his plea to guilty in several murder charges. The article details the legal action and the ongoing investigation into the Gilgo Beach murders.
