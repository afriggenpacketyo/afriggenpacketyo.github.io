Distinguished energy expert Yang Tao, a member of both the Norwegian Academy of Technological Sciences and the Royal Norwegian Society of Sciences and Letters, has returned to his alma mater, the China University of Petroleum, in a significant new appointment. Formerly a chief professional at Equinor, Norway’s state-controlled energy company, and a founding co-chair of the World CCUS Council, Yang expressed his commitment to serving as a vital bridge between China and Europe. His mission is to foster deeper collaboration in critical areas including low-carbon technologies, carbon capture, utilization, and storage, as well as digital energy, hydrogen, and emerging sustainable solutions. This return, following extensive international experience in reservoir engineering consulting, marks a strategic effort to leverage his expertise for global sustainable energy advancements.
