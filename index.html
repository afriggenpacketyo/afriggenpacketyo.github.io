Turkey Arrests 115 IS Suspects Foiling Planned New Year's Attacks

2025-12-25 05:19:28 EST

Turkish authorities have arrested 115 suspected members of the Islamic State group, successfully thwarting planned attacks on Christmas and New Year's events across Istanbul. Mass raids conducted at 124 addresses led to the seizure of firearms, ammunition, and crucial organizational documents. Officials stated that IS supporters had been actively planning attacks, particularly targeting non-Muslims, highlighting a significant security threat. This operation follows a recent raid on the Afghanistan-Pakistan border where a senior IS operative, accused of planning civilian attacks, was detained. Turkey's security services regularly target individuals with suspected links to IS, with President Ahmed al-Sharaa vowing cooperation to root out surviving elements of the group.
