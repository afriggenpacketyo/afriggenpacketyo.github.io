Innovative Mentoring Program Boosts GCSE Language Uptake in Wales

2026-04-18 16:50:31 EDT

GCSE language uptake in Wales is experiencing a significant turnaround, with increases of 3.6% in 2023-24 and 9.6% in 2024-25, thanks to an innovative Cardiff University-led modern foreign languages (MFL) mentoring program. The program, which deliberately targets undecided pupils, focuses on cultural understanding and identity rather than traditional grammar drills, making language learning more engaging. Project head Lucy Jenkins explains that the approach helps pupils understand diverse perspectives, leading to unexpected interests like Hugo's decision to learn Mandarin. The article highlights the program's success in revitalizing language education by connecting it to real-life experiences and expanding students' global awareness.
