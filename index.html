HSBC Reports Mixed Annual Results, Exceeding Estimates with Revenue Growth

2026-02-25 10:18:27 EST

HSBC, Europe's largest lender, reported mixed annual financial results, with a 7.4% drop in pre-tax profit but a 4% increase in revenue, ultimately surpassing analysts' estimates. The bank's wealth division and Hong Kong businesses demonstrated strong performance, contributing to a significant rise in fourth-quarter profit, largely driven by favorable one-off items. Group CEO Georges Elhedery characterized 2025 as a year of "decisive action and swift execution," noting strong momentum across all four of the bank's businesses. HSBC is now targeting a higher return on average tangible equity by 2028 and aims for an 8% reduction in payroll costs through strategic simplification and de-duplication of roles. Benfica's Appeal Against Gianluca Prestianni's Suspension Rejected Ahead of Real Madrid Match

2026-02-25 10:16:56 EST

Benfica's appeal against the provisional one-match suspension of Argentine winger Gianluca Prestianni has been rejected by UEFA, effectively ruling him out of the crucial Champions League tie against Real Madrid. Prestianni was initially banned after Real Madrid's Vinicius Jr reported alleged racist abuse during the first leg of their knockout round play-off. UEFA upheld the ban, stating there was sufficient evidence to support a "prima facie" case of discriminatory language, pending a full investigation. While Prestianni has denied the allegations, the decision stands, preventing him from participating in the significant European club competition match and underscoring UEFA's firm stance against racism in football.
