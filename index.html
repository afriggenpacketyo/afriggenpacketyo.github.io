2026-01-07 01:28:49 EST

In a column, former manager Tony Pulis strongly endorses Darren Fletcher as the ideal candidate for Manchester United's permanent manager role, following Ruben Amorim's departure. Pulis, who managed Fletcher at West Brom, praises him as an "outstanding person" and a "top professional" with deep club ties, having worked under Sir Alex Ferguson and played with club legends. He highlights Fletcher's clear leadership, tactical understanding, and willingness to challenge decisions, noting his relentless winning mentality. Pulis argues that if given the necessary time and belief, similar to the backing Sir Alex Ferguson received in his early tenure, Fletcher could indeed be the long-awaited leader Manchester United fans have been hoping for, capable of making hard decisions for the club's success.
