2026-05-12 05:45:00 EDT

British Prime Minister Keir Starmer is resolute in his position despite facing significant pressure following Labour's heavy losses in recent UK elections. 2026-05-12 05:40:36 EDT

WHO: No Sign of Larger Hantavirus Outbreak After Cruise Ship Evacuation

2026-05-12 05:35:32 EDT

The head of the World Health Organization (WHO), Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus, stated there are no current signs of a larger hantavirus outbreak following the evacuation of passengers from the MV Hondius cruise ship. However, he cautioned that the situation could change, and more cases might emerge due to the virus's long incubation period. While three deaths have been confirmed and several passengers have tested positive, health officials maintain that the global public health risk remains very low. The WHO is working with countries to implement quarantine and contact tracing measures, emphasizing that hantavirus is not similar to COVID-19 and requires prolonged close contact for transmission.
