Analyst Ming-Chi Kuo suggests that controlling both the operating system and hardware is crucial for OpenAI to deliver comprehensive AI agent services, with smartphones being the ideal platform for real-time AI inference. This partnership could lead to a new AI agent ecosystem, potentially bundling subscriptions with hardware. Qualcomm's Snapdragon processors currently power many Android phones.
