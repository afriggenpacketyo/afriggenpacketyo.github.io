The American Airlines pilot confirmed visual contact with the other aircraft before successfully averting a collision. This alarming incident underscores persistent concerns about flight safety, particularly amidst the ongoing government shutdown, which has led to Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) staffing cuts and an unprecedented directive to cancel flights nationwide. The U.S. House of Representatives is anticipated to vote on measures to end the shutdown, potentially bringing relief to the aviation sector.
