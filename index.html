While Israeli Prime Minister Netanyahu initially expressed regret for a "tragic mishap," the IDF later claimed it targeted a Hamas surveillance camera and that six of those killed were terrorists, without providing immediate evidence. This article details the tragic sequence of events, identifies the slain journalists, and highlights widespread international condemnation, with calls for a prompt and impartial investigation into the incident.
