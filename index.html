Governor Polis stressed that clemency determinations are strictly merit-based, weighing factors such as Peters' status as a nonviolent, first-time, elderly offender against her consistent refusal to take accountability for actions she maintains were aimed at exposing voter fraud, despite a lack of supporting evidence. He unequivocally stated that President Trump's public demands would not influence his ultimate decision.
