Experts raise ethical concerns, questioning the system's reliance on data correlations rather than human logic, potentially leading to false positives and blurring the lines between combatants and non-combatants. Despite its effectiveness in Lebanon, Hezbollah is adapting with decentralized structures and secure communication, while critics highlight the dangers of AI turning correlation into action without sufficient context.
