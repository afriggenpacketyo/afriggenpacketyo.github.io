2026-03-07 06:00:13 EST

Daylight Saving Time for 2026 is set to begin on Sunday, March 8, at 2 a.m. local time, causing most Americans to "spring forward" and lose an hour of sleep. Historically, it was first adopted in the U.S. in 1918 to conserve fuel during World War I, though subsequent studies have shown minimal energy savings.
