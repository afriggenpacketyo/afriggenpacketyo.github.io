Melania Trump Unveils "Fostering the Future" Initiative for Foster Youth Opportunities

2025-11-13 17:04:46 EST

First Lady Melania Trump has launched "Fostering the Future," a new initiative aimed at enhancing career and education opportunities for children in the foster system. President Donald Trump signed an executive order creating the program, which unites federal entities, non-profits, educational institutions, and the private sector. The initiative includes an online hub to connect foster youth with resources and increase access to vouchers and grants. Melania Trump expressed pride in the "empathetic and strategic" effort, urging broader leadership participation. This program expands on her "Be Best" campaign, offering scholarships to foster youth and demonstrating a focus on supporting vulnerable children.
