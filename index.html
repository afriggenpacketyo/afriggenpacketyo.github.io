Hussen, known for roles in an Eastenders spin-off and a Jason Statham film, was arrested after police allegedly found the meth hidden in bags of charcoal within shipping containers from Ghana. Authorities intercepted the drugs before they were delivered to a storage facility in Sydney. Hussen has been denied bail and faces a maximum penalty of life imprisonment. The investigation also led to the arrest of a couple in South Australia for allegedly using false identities.
