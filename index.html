Furthermore, traffic problems compelled numerous spectators to exit tournament shuttle buses and walk through residential areas to reach the course. This event marks the first time a sitting president has attended the Ryder Cup, and organizers had reportedly anticipated even greater logistical challenges than those that ultimately materialized.
