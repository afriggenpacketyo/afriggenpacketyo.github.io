To prevent this, feeders should be cleaned every two to three days with hot water or a vinegar and water mixture. It is also important to use the correct recipe of one part refined white sugar to four parts water, avoiding other sugars, sweeteners, and red food coloring. Planting native hummingbird-friendly wildflowers is also recommended.
