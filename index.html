Daylight Saving Time 2026 Begins Sunday, Shifting Clocks Forward

2026-03-08 01:18:54 EDT

Daylight Saving Time (DST) for 2026 commences on Sunday, March 8, at 2 a.m. local time, requiring most Americans to "spring forward" and lose an hour of sleep. This annual practice effectively shifts an hour of daylight from morning to evening, with its origins tracing back to World War I efforts to conserve fuel. Despite its long history and consistent observance since the 1960s, studies have shown minimal energy conservation benefits and some associated negative health effects on individuals.
