2026-05-14 14:07:48 EDT

Original Tommy’s, a beloved and iconic Southern California burger chain, is proudly celebrating its 80th anniversary by offering a special 80-cent chili burger on Friday, available from noon to 8 p.m. at all its locations. Customers will be limited to three of these signature sloppy burgers while supplies last, and the company plans live entertainment and giveaways at its original "Shack" stand in Los Angeles. Founded in 1946 by Tom Koulax, the family-owned chain has grown to 32 locations across California and Nevada, becoming renowned for its distinctive secret chili sauce. This celebration marks a significant milestone for the regional staple, which continues to inspire numerous imitators while steadfastly maintaining its unique brand and family legacy.
