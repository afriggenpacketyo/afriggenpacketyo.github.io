Throughout 2025, diverse forms of protest art, encompassing murals, statues, and performances, emerged as significant focal points for collective action across numerous cities and towns in the United States. This article, presented as a visual compilation, illustrates how artists effectively employed creative expression to highlight pressing political and social issues of the year. Noteworthy examples include a controversial statue depicting Donald Trump and Jeffrey Epstein, alongside artistic interpretations of "melting democracy" and human banners. These artistic endeavors served as potent visual commentaries and rallying points, galvanizing communities in dissent and advocating for various forms of societal change.
