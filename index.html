Senators, including Richard Blumenthal and Elizabeth Warren, argue these practices may violate the FTC's ban on misleading fees and are calling for stronger enforcement. The ongoing federal trial examining whether Ticketmaster operates as an illegal monopoly further complicates the situation, with many states continuing litigation despite a recent settlement with Live Nation Entertainment.
