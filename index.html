Wang faced public ridicule on social media after a misstatement regarding mask production during the early stages of the Covid-19 pandemic. He was later transferred to a senior semi-retirement position in Beijing. His political career began in Jiangxi and Guizhou provinces before his move to Hubei. The article suggests that his current scrutiny may be linked to his past handling of the pandemic, though the specific nature of the corruption allegations is not detailed.
