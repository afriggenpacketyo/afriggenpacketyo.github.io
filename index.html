He successfully secured a spot in the Padres' starting rotation, which faces several uncertainties due to other key pitchers' injuries and departures. Buehler has expressed feeling significantly healthier and trusting the team's new process, with the Padres organization holding strong hopes that he can once again become a dominant force on the mound against opposing teams, particularly in his return to Southern California.
