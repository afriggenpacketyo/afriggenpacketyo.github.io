Strategies discussed include leveraging public outrage over Republican redistricting efforts and actively pushing back against the negative impacts of Trump's policies on healthcare and costs. Despite acknowledged internal concerns regarding financial standing and leadership, a discernible shift in energy towards unity and a more proactive, "fighting" resistance was noted, with a renewed focus on core issues like affordability.
