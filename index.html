Understanding Credit Card Grace Periods and Their Importance

2025-06-25 12:51:58 EDT

A credit card grace period is the time between the end of your billing cycle and your payment due date when you won't be charged interest on new purchases, provided you pay your full statement balance by the due date. This feature allows cardholders to make purchases without accruing interest, effectively offering a temporary, interest-free loan. To maximize the benefits of a grace period, it's crucial to pay the full statement balance each month, automate payments, track billing cycles, and avoid cash advances.
