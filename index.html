Artist JR unveils large-scale installation on Paris's Pont Neuf bridge

2026-06-16 04:03:00 EDT

French street artist JR has opened his latest large-scale installation, titled "La Caverne du Pont Neuf," on Paris's historic Pont Neuf bridge. The artwork, inspired by the quarries from which the bridge's stones were extracted, features images of rugged rock covering the structure. This project is JR's largest to date and aims to transform the way people experience the city, paying homage to the late artists Christo and Jeanne-Claude's "The Pont Neuf Wrapped."
