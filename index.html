Paul McCartney discusses new album, family, and teaching Paul Mescal guitar

2026-05-27 19:13:45 EDT

Paul McCartney reflects on his new album, "The Boys of Dungeon Lane," a collection of "memory songs" inspired by his childhood and experiences with The Beatles. He shares anecdotes about his family, his father's influence on his musicality, and the creative process behind some of the album's tracks, including "As You Lie There" and "Lost Horizon." McCartney also discusses his perspective on The Beatles' "Get Back" documentary, which helped him re-evaluate his role in the band's breakup. He recounts teaching actor Paul Mescal guitar, noting Mescal's impressive proficiency. McCartney expresses continued passion for music, dismissing retirement thoughts and showing interest in evolving performance technologies like Abba's Voyage concerts.
