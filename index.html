Concurrently, Britain's roads are also bracing for one of their busiest Christmas Eves, with the RAC advising travelers to avoid peak hours due to expected delays on major routes. Rail services will be limited or closed for maintenance over the festive period, pushing more travelers onto roads. Despite the high traffic, National Express coaches will operate to many destinations on Christmas Day.
