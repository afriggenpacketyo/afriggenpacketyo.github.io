2026-05-31 14:51:53 EDT

Jacob Baker, 36, of Pahoa, Hawaii, has been charged with one count of first-degree murder and three counts of second-degree murder, alongside almost a dozen other criminal offenses, in connection with the alleged killings of three elderly men. The victims, identified as Robert Shine, 69, John Carse, 69, and an unnamed 79-year-old man, were discovered deceased at residences in Pahoa over a two-day period. Baker was apprehended following an extensive manhunt involving state and federal authorities and is currently being held without bond. The article notes that investigators have not yet established connections between the victims, apart from two residing in close proximity, nor have Baker's motives for the alleged triple homicide been publicly disclosed.
