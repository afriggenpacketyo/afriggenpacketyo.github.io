Marvell Technology Stock Surges on News of S&P 500 Inclusion

2026-06-08 09:34:10 EDT

Marvell Technology's stock experienced a significant jump after the company was announced as an addition to the S&P 500 index, effective June 22. The AI chipmaker, specializing in high-performance chips for data infrastructure, has seen substantial growth this year, with its stock up over 200%. Nvidia's CEO Jensen Huang recently touted Marvell as a potential 'next trillion-dollar company,' and Nvidia has invested $2 billion in the firm. The inclusion in the S&P 500 is expected to increase its visibility and investment appeal.
