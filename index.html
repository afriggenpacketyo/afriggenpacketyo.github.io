The protein uses its own structure as a mold to assemble DNA building blocks sequentially, creating a long, sequence-specific strand. This finding opens new avenues for understanding DNA synthesis and could potentially lead to novel tools for creating custom DNA molecules. While not breaking the central dogma entirely, it reveals an unexpected evolutionary pathway for DNA creation.
