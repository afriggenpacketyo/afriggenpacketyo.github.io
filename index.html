Billy Joel has canceled his upcoming concerts due to a diagnosis of normal pressure hydrocephalus (N.P.H.), a brain disorder affecting hearing, vision, and balance. N.P.H. occurs when excess cerebrospinal fluid accumulates in the brain, leading to difficulties in walking, bladder control issues, and memory problems. The condition is often underdiagnosed and its symptoms can be mistaken for normal effects of aging, but early diagnosis and treatment, typically involving surgery to insert a shunt, can significantly improve symptoms.
