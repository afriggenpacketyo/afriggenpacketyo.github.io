2026-01-22 10:13:05 EST

General Motors plans to shift production of a next-generation Buick compact SUV from China to its Fairfax Assembly plant in Kansas City, Kansas, for domestic sales, beginning in 2028. This move aligns with President Trump's pressure on companies to onshore manufacturing and amid increasing U.S.-China trade tensions. The Buick Envision SUV has been produced in China for the U.S. market since 2017. GM stated this decision reinforces its domestic manufacturing footprint and supports U.S. jobs, building on recent investments in its U.S. facilities. The Kansas plant will also produce the Chevrolet Equinox, further strengthening GM's domestic production capabilities.
