Named after Pearl Jam frontman Eddie Vedder, who grew up in San Diego and founded the band in Seattle, the trophy features his hand-drawn designs and a logo for his EB Research Partnership charity. Vedder himself "test drove" the guitar for an hour before it was shipped for the competition. The initiative aims to bring awareness to epidermolysis bullosa and canonize a geographic rivalry, with the Mariners taking the inaugural season series.
