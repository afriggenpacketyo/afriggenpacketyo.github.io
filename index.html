These halts, known as 'circuit breakers,' are triggered by rapid declines in stock prices and futures. During non-U.S. trading hours, a 7% drop in S&P futures results in a trading halt until buyers emerge at the 'limit down' level, with Russell 2000 futures briefly hitting this threshold overnight.
