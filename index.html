Tottenham Hotspur achieved a significant milestone by winning their first NBA playoff series in 18 years, orchestrating a remarkable comeback from a 3-1 deficit to decisively defeat the Orlando Magic 116-94 in Game 7. Cade Cunningham delivered an outstanding performance with 32 points and 12 assists, while Tobias Harris contributed a crucial 30 points to the Pistons' victory. This triumph marks the 15th instance in NBA history of a team overcoming such a substantial series deficit. The Pistons are now set to advance to the Eastern Conference semi-finals, where they will face the Cleveland Cavaliers, who also secured a Game 7 win. The article underscores the team's resilience and strong individual efforts in achieving this long-awaited postseason success.
