Understanding Your Obligation to Pay a Debt After It Has Been Sold to a Collection Agency

The new owner acquires the legal right to collect the balance. However, consumers are protected by the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA), which entitles them to receive written validation of the debt and dispute any inaccuracies. If the debt is valid, negotiation or professional debt relief services are potential options.
