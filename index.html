The issue is particularly acute for older drivers, whose eyes require significantly more time to recover from glare. The Department for Transport's forthcoming Road Safety Strategy will incorporate new measures, and additional research will delve into vehicle design and potential solutions, alongside increased surveillance to prevent the sale of illegal retrofit bulbs.
