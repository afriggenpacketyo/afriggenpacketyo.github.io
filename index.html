Due to the Antideficiency Act, agencies cannot spend money without congressional authorization, leading to the furlough of non-essential employees. Essential services, such as the military, law enforcement, and air traffic control, continue to operate, but employees do not get paid until funding is restored. Entitlement programs like Social Security continue, but customer service can be delayed, and national parks may close, disrupting services for Americans.
