The current war in Iran has further exacerbated these issues, disrupting global supply chains for both fertilizer and fuel. Many farmers, including lifelong Republicans like Taylor, are expressing frustration with the Trump administration's policies, which they argue have gutted export markets and provided insufficient relief. The situation is described as a "make or break" year for many, with some considering abandoning farming.
