China's State-Owned Enterprises (SOEs), including Sinochem Holdings and China Huaneng Group, are relocating their headquarters and thousands of employees to Xiongan New Area, Xi Jinping's "city of the future." Launched in 2017 to coordinate development in the Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei region, Xiongan aims to relieve Beijing of non-capital functions and ease congestion. The influx of SOEs is attracting further investment and people, though the 100km distance from Beijing poses some challenges for staff. This strategic move is central to China's long-term urban development and economic planning, showcasing a concerted effort to build a modern, innovative urban hub.
