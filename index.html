This public reaffirmation of cooperation between the two nations takes place amidst disturbing reports of "slave-like" conditions for thousands of North Korean workers dispatched to Russia and Ukraine's claims that as many as 10,000 North Korean soldiers are fighting alongside Russian forces. The call underscores the unprecedented and deepening collaboration between Moscow and Pyongyang, highlighting Russia's increasing reliance on North Korean assistance due to its near-complete economic and diplomatic isolation from the West.
