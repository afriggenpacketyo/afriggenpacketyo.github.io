However, he conceded that such actions could compromise the "integrity and solemnity" of the proceedings. The disciplinary report also highlighted other inappropriate conduct, such as expressing political affiliations from the bench. Judge Thornhill accepted the commission's recommendation for suspension followed by retirement, bringing an end to his two decades of service in a rather unconventional manner.
