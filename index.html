Meanwhile, 2022 champion Elena Rybakina struggled but ultimately survived a tough second set to beat Loïs Boisson of France 6-4, 1-6, 6-3, acknowledging the need to improve. However, there was a significant upset as No. 8 seed Elina Svitolina crashed out, losing 7-5, 6-2 to fellow Ukrainian Dara Snigur, world No. 77, marking a surprising early exit.
