While some cafe owners, like Hyun Sung-joo, have resorted to blocking power outlets due to high rents, others cater to Cagongjok with specific amenities. This cultural phenomenon, fueled by South Korea's hyper-competitive society and lack of public study spaces, highlights a societal challenge where young people seek affordable, ambient environments for learning amidst academic and job insecurity.
