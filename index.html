Orbiting a young star, this planet is estimated to be about the mass of Saturn and is located approximately 50 times farther from its star than Earth is from the Sun. Scientists utilized a technique called high-contrast imaging, using a coronagraph to suppress the star's glare and reveal the faint planet.
