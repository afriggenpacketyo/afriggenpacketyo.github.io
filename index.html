Critical minerals, especially rare earths, are vital components in modern technologies and a key bargaining chip in US-China geopolitical rivalry, with China dominating their supply chain. This initiative underscores US efforts to shore up its domestic critical minerals supply chain and reduce reliance on foreign sources. (not investment advice)
