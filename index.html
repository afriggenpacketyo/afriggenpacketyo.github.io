Initially aimed at reasserting US economic dominance, tariffs are now being used to impose Trump's political will on unrelated issues, which many countries view as an infringement on their sovereignty. Leaders from Brazil, Russia, India, and China are discussing collective strategies to bypass US economic leverage, with the BRICS alliance emerging as a potential counterweight. This shift is driven by a shared belief that unchecked tariff diplomacy threatens not only their economies but also their national independence, prompting calls for diversification and alternative trade currencies.
