The poll also indicates that if other independent candidates, Eric Adams and Curtis Sliwa, were to drop out, the race would tighten considerably. Despite efforts by Donald Trump's advisers to clear the field for Cuomo, Adams and Sliwa have publicly stated their intention to remain in the race, ensuring a multi-candidate contest.
