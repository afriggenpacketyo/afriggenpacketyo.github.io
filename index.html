2026-04-30 11:43:13 EDT

The prime minister's sister-in-law, Judith Alexander, described feeling "scared" and "struggling to breathe" after an arson attack at her rented home in Kentish Town, north London. Ukrainian nationals are accused of targeting the property, along with another residence and a car linked to Sir Keir Starmer. Alexander recounted hearing loud bangs and seeing smoke billowing from the entrance of the house while her daughter was asleep upstairs. The incident, which occurred in the early hours, prompted a rapid response from emergency services. The defendants deny charges of conspiring to damage property by fire, with one alleging he was recruited and threatened by a Russian-speaking individual.

Trump administration appeals court order seeking to reduce childhood vaccine recommendations

2026-04-30 11:39:35 EDT

The Trump administration is appealing a court order that blocked Health Secretary Robert F. Kennedy Jr.'s decision to reduce recommended childhood vaccinations. The March 16 order halted the removal of recommendations for vaccines against flu, rotavirus, hepatitis A and B, and RSV. The appeal was filed after a six-week delay, with the government providing no immediate explanation for the delay or the basis of the appeal. This action is part of a lawsuit filed by medical groups concerned about Kennedy's reconstitution of the vaccine advisory committee, which included anti-vaccine voices. The plaintiffs aim to prevent what they describe as the "steady destruction of vaccine policy and public health."

Trainee driver crashes bus into River Seine near Paris

2026-04-30 11:34:09 EDT

A trainee bus driver lost control of a vehicle near Paris and drove it into the River Seine, an incident that occurred after the bus collided with a parked car. All four passengers on board, along with the trainee driver and a lead driver, were rescued from the water by emergency services, which included firefighters, divers, and police. Drug and alcohol tests for the trainee driver came back negative, and the cause of the accident is currently under investigation. The local transport authority has initiated an internal inquiry into the incident, which took place in Juvisy-sur-Orge.
